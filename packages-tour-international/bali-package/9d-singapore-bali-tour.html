🌴 Overview

A signature city and island experience

Singapore is a city that surprises you at every corner. From walking around Marina Bay Sands to exploring Gardens by the Bay, there’s a lot to take in. And the Night Safari? Totally worth it — seeing animals roam around at night is an experience you’ll remember for years. The food is amazing too, and the city is so clean and easy to get around.

Then comes Bali — and it’s a completely different vibe. Beautiful beaches, green rice fields in Ubud, and sunsets at Tanah Lot that just take your breath away. Whether you’re traveling with family, friends, or a group, there’s plenty to do, but also enough time to just relax and enjoy the island. A Singapore Bali tour package like this lets you see the highlights without rushing.
