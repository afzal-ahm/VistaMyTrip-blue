🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: TOUCH DOWN IN PARADISE: CHECK-IN & CHILL IN UBUD

Arrival

Land in Bali and meet your tour coordinator at the airport. Transfer to your hotel in Ubud, Bali’s cultural and spiritual hub surrounded by lush rice terraces and jungle landscapes. After check-in, relax and refresh from your journey. Spend your evening soaking in Ubud’s serene atmosphere stroll through art-filled streets, explore cozy cafés, or simply unwind amidst the greenery. Note: Pickups will be arranged at a common scheduled time.

UbudCheck-in

Day 2: ADVENTURE MODE ON – ATV RIDE + COFFEE ESTATE + BALI SWING

Adventure

Start your day with an exhilarating ATV ride through Bali’s rugged trails, forests, and rice fields. Visit Alas Harum Plantation – learn about Luwak coffee, walk through scenic terraces, and take in stunning views. Get your adrenaline fix (and best photos) with the famous Bali Swing that flies high over the jungle.

ATV RideBali Swing

Day 3: OFF TO THE ISLANDS – TRANSFER TO GILI FOR PURE PARADISE

Gili Islands

After breakfast, it’s time to island-hop! Check out from Ubud and cruise your way to the Gili Islands – car-free, carefree, and drop-dead gorgeous. Arrive and check in to your beachside stay. Spend the rest of the day at leisure – chase sunsets, sip fresh coconut, or explore the island by foot or cycle. Note: Local transfers in Gili (by cidomos or bicycles) are to be managed on your own. Guests are required to pay a harbour fee of IDR 20,000 per person directly at the harbour.

Ferry RideIsland Leisure

Day 4: DIVE IN – SNORKELING AND ISLAND LEISURE IN GILI

Snorkeling

Today’s all about living the island dream. Embark on a 3-point snorkeling tour across Gili’s best underwater spots – crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and sea turtles await. The rest of the day is yours – chill on a hammock, beach hop, or join the fun at beach bars as the island lights up at night.

SnorkelingBeach Bars

Day 5: TRANSFER TO MAINLAND + CLIFFSIDE SUNSET WITH KECAK FIRE DANCE

Uluwatu

Say goodbye to Gili and journey into Bali’s buzzing beach town. After check-in, unwind, and in the afternoon, visit the stunning Uluwatu Temple sitting dramatically on a cliff. Watch a fiery Kecak Dance performance against a golden sunset backdrop – a true Balinese spectacle. Return to Kuta and soak in the island nightlife or relax.

Uluwatu TempleKecak Dance

Day 6: ZAMNA FESTIVAL DAY 1

Festival

After breakfast, enjoy a relaxed morning at your own pace. You can choose to unwind at the hotel, explore nearby beaches, hop between some aesthetic cafés, or stroll through local markets soaking in the Bali vibe. Later in the afternoon, get ready for one of the most awaited experiences of your trip as you head towards the iconic Zamna Festival at Savaya Bali. Set against a stunning cliffside backdrop overlooking the ocean, this festival brings together some of the best international artists and an unmatched party atmosphere.

Savaya BaliMusic Event

Day 7: ZAMNA FESTIVAL DAY 2

Festival

Kickstart your day with breakfast at the hotel and take it slow for the first half of the day. You can plan your time based on your mood, whether it’s unwinding with a relaxing Balinese massage, heading to the beach for some surfing or water activities, or exploring iconic spots like Tanah Lot. As the day moves forward, get ready for another high energy evening as you head out for Day 2 of the Zamna Festival at Savaya Bali. Enjoy the electrifying beats, soak in the stunning cliffside setting, and make the most of the night.

FestivalParty

Day 8: SAY GOODBYE (FOR NOW) – DEPARTURE DAY

Departure

After breakfast, check out from your hotel. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight. Head home with a full heart, sun-kissed skin, and unforgettable memories.

Check-outAirport
