🌴 Overview

An unforgettable festive journey

Embark on a journey to Bali with Gili Island through the breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural tapestry of Bali and the Gili Islands, in which each moment is a symphony of sights, sounds, and sensations with the intention of leaving you fully mesmerized.

Begin your trip in Kuta, where the sun-kissed beaches and bustling streets beckon with the promise of adventure. As you weave through the colorful markets, you may feel the vibe of Bali’s vibrant lifestyle coursing through your veins. Take a second to pause and soak in the panoramic vistas of the Indian Ocean, where the horizon stretches with no end in sight before you, a canvas painted in shades of gold and azure.

Journey onwards to the majestic Uluwatu Temple, perched atop towering cliffs that plunge dramatically into the sea below. Right here, because the sun dips low on the horizon, casting a fiery glow across the sky, you may be treated to a mesmerizing Kecak dance overall performance. And as you bid farewell to Bali and the Gili Islands, it won’t be simply recollections you bring with you; it will be a profound sense of connection and belonging that will live with you long after your adventure has ended.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: TOUCH DOWN IN PARADISE: CHECK-IN & CHILL IN UBUD

Arrival

Land in Bali and meet your tour coordinator at the airport. Transfer to your hotel in Ubud, Bali’s cultural and spiritual hub surrounded by lush rice terraces and jungle landscapes. After check-in, relax and refresh from your journey. Spend your evening soaking in Ubud’s serene atmosphere – stroll through art-filled streets, explore cozy cafés, or simply unwind amidst the greenery. Note: Pickups will be arranged at a common scheduled time.

UbudCheck-in

Day 2: ADVENTURE MODE ON – ATV RIDE + COFFEE ESTATE + BALI SWING

Adventure

Start your day with an exhilarating ATV ride through Bali’s rugged trails, forests, and rice fields. Visit Alas Harum Plantation – learn about Luwak coffee, walk through scenic terraces, and take in stunning views. Get your adrenaline fix (and best photos) with the famous Bali Swing that flies high over the jungle.

ATV RideBali Swing

Day 3: OFF TO THE ISLANDS – TRANSFER TO GILI FOR PURE PARADISE

Gili Islands

After breakfast, it’s time to island-hop! Check out from Ubud and cruise your way to the Gili Islands – car-free, carefree, and drop-dead gorgeous. Arrive and check in to your beachside stay. Spend the rest of the day at leisure – chase sunsets, sip fresh coconut, or explore the island by foot or cycle. Note: Local transfers in Gili (by cidomos or bicycles) are to be managed on your own.

Ferry RideIsland Leisure

Day 4: DIVE IN – SNORKELING AND ISLAND LEISURE IN GILI

Snorkeling

Today’s all about living the island dream. Embark on a 3-point snorkeling tour across Gili’s best underwater spots – crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and sea turtles await. The rest of the day is yours – chill on a hammock, beach hop, or join the fun at beach bars as the island lights up at night.

SnorkelingBeach Bars

Day 5: TRANSFER TO KUTA + CLIFFSIDE SUNSET WITH KECAK FIRE DANCE

Kuta

Say goodbye to Gili and journey into Bali’s buzzing beach town, Kuta. After check-in, unwind, and in the afternoon, visit the stunning Uluwatu Temple sitting dramatically on a cliff. Watch a fiery Kecak Dance performance against a golden sunset backdrop – a true Balinese spectacle.

Uluwatu TempleKecak Dance

Day 6: INSTAGRAM-WORTHY DAY OUT – FULL-DAY NUSA PENIDA TOUR

Nusa Penida

Embark on a full-day tour to Nusa Penida island. Discover its dramatic cliffs, pristine beaches, and crystal-clear waters perfect for your Instagram feed. Explore famous spots and enjoy an included lunch before returning to your hotel in Kuta.

Island TourSightseeing

Day 7: THRILL & CHILL: WATERSPORTS + NEW YEAR’S EVE CELEBRATION

New Year's Eve

Experience the thrill of watersports at Benoa Beach including Jet Ski, Banana Boat Ride, and Parasailing. As the evening approaches, gear up for an unforgettable New Year's Eve celebration in Kuta. Enjoy the vibrant nightlife, festive parties, and a spectacular end to the year!

WatersportsNYE Party

Day 8: SAY GOODBYE (FOR NOW) – DEPARTURE DAY

Departure

After breakfast, check out from your hotel. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight. Head home with a full heart, sun-kissed skin, and unforgettable festive memories.
