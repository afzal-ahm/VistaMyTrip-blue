The ultimate girls' getaway

Get ready for the ultimate girls’ getaway with our Bali & Gili Islands 7N/8D Girls Trip. This trip is perfect if you and your friends just wanna escape, have fun, and make memories that last forever. Bali is just stunning – from beaches to temples, you’ll be snapping pics non-stop.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: TOUCH DOWN IN PARADISE: CHECK-IN & CHILL IN KUTA

Arrival

Land in Bali and get greeted by your tour coordinator. Transfer to your hotel in Kuta, Bali’s buzzing beach town. After check-in, unwind and shake off the jet lag. Spend your evening soaking in Bali’s energy – hit the beach, explore funky cafes, or just enjoy sunset vibes. Note: Pickups will be arranged at a common scheduled time.

KutaCheck-in

Day 2: THRILL & CHILL – WATERSPORTS + CLIFFSIDE SUNSET WITH KECAK FIRE DANCE

Watersports

Experience the thrill of watersports at Benoa Beach including Jet Ski, Banana Boat Ride, and Parasailing. Later in the afternoon, head to Uluwatu Temple perched dramatically on a cliff. Watch the mesmerizing Kecak Fire Dance performance with a stunning golden sunset backdrop.

Benoa BeachUluwatu Temple

Day 3: INSTAGRAM-WORTHY DAY OUT – FULL-DAY NUSA PENIDA TOUR

Nusa Penida

Embark on a full-day tour to Nusa Penida island. Discover its dramatic cliffs, pristine beaches, and crystal-clear waters perfect for your Instagram feed. Explore famous spots and enjoy an included lunch before returning to your hotel in Kuta.

Island TourSightseeing

Day 4: OFF TO THE ISLANDS – TRANSFER TO GILI FOR PURE PARADISE

Gili Islands

Check out from your Kuta hotel and take a scenic ferry ride to the idyllic Gili Islands. Arrive and check in at your island accommodation. Enjoy a laid-back evening on the sandy shores, chase sunsets, or explore the car-free island by bicycle.

Ferry TransferIsland Leisure

Day 5: DIVE IN – SNORKELING AND ISLAND LEISURE IN GILI

Snorkeling

Dive into the clear waters with a 3-point snorkeling experience in the Gili Islands. Encounter vibrant coral reefs and majestic marine life. The rest of the day is at your leisure to relax on a hammock, beach hop, or experience the vibrant island nightlife.

Marine LifeRelaxation

Day 6: CULTURE MEETS CALM – TRANSFER TO UBUD & MARKET WALK

Ubud

Bid farewell to the Gili Islands and take a ferry back to the mainland. Transfer to Ubud, the cultural and spiritual heart of Bali. After check-in, enjoy a leisurely walk through the vibrant Ubud market and soak in the calm, artistic vibes.

Ferry RideUbud Market

Day 7: ADVENTURE MODE ON – ATV RIDE + COFFEE ESTATE + BALI SWING

Adventure

Get your adrenaline pumping with a thrilling single ATV ride through Ubud's forests and rice fields. Visit the Alas Harum plantation, learn about Luwak coffee, and capture iconic moments on the famous Bali Swing flying high over the jungle.

ATV RideBali Swing

Day 8: SAY GOODBYE (FOR NOW) – DEPARTURE DAY

Departure

Enjoy your final breakfast in Bali. Check out from your hotel and transfer to the airport for your flight back home, carrying unforgettable memories, sun-kissed skin, and a profound sense of connection with the island.

Check-outAirport Drop
