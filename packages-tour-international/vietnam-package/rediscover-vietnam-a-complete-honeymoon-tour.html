🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Hanoi | Leisure Day

Arrival

Welcome to Hanoi! Upon arrival at Noi Bai International Airport, you’ll get transferred to the hotel for check in. You can relax for the day or explore the beautiful city on your own. Visit places like the Hanoi Opera House, Hoa Binh Dam, and Ba Vi National Park, among other attractions. Later, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Hanoi ArrivalLeisure DayPrivate Transfer

Day 1: Hanoi Sightseeing Tour | Cycling Tour of Old Quarter

Sightseeing

Prepare yourself for an amazing experience as you get driven for a sightseeing tour of Hanoi. Begin the exploration with a visit to the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum Complex, dedicated to the great Vietnamese leader - Ho Chi Minh. Admire the One-Pillar Pagoda and Chua Tran Quoc. Also, explore the Bat Trang Ceramic Village, and Temple Of Literature before going for the cycling tour around the Old Quarters. Later, you’ll get driven back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Ho Chi Minh MausoleumOne-Pillar PagodaCycling Tour

Day 2: Transfer to Halong Bay | Overnight Cruise Experience

Cruise

Get ready for an amazing cruise experience by checking out of the hotel and getting driven to the Tuan Chao Harbour. Then, board your Halong Bay cruise, and enjoy the scenic views of islands and caves. Later, visit the Ti Top Island, and Pearl Farm Village by boat and enjoy activities like kayaking and swimming. Upon returning to the cruise, participate in a Vietnamese cooking class. As the day winds down, settle into your comfortable cabin on the cruise ship and enjoy your overnight stay.

Halong Bay CruiseTi Top IslandKayaking

Day 3: Transfer to Da Nang via Hanoi

Transfer

Wake up to the stunning scenery of Halong Bay. While still on the cruise, enjoy a Tai Chi lesson, a traditional Chinese martial art. Afterwards, deboard the cruise and visit Halong Bay's largest cave, the Surprise Caves. Return to Tuan Chao Harbour and transfer to Noi Bai International Airport for your flight to Da Nang. Upon arrival at Da Nang International Airport, get a transfer to your hotel and check in for an overnight stay.

Surprise CavesFlight to Da Nang

Day 4: Hoi An Day Tour with Boat Ride in Thu Bon River

Heritage

Start your day with a visit to Hoi An as you get transferred from the hotel and explore the narrow lanes filled with centuries-old houses showcasing Japanese, Chinese, and European architecture. Visit the iconic Japanese Covered Bridge and explore the Assembly Hall Of Fujian. Then, enjoy a peaceful boat trip around My Tho, famous for floating markets, and immense rice fields. Later, return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Hoi An TownJapanese BridgeThu Bon River Boat Ride

Day 5: Transfer to Ho Chi Minh City | Leisure Day

Transfer

On this day, check out of the hotel and get transferred to the Da Nang International Airport to board your flight to Ho Chi Minh City. Upon arrival at Tan Son Nhat International Airport, you’ll get driven to the hotel for check in. After relaxing for a while, you can head out to explore the city on your own. Visit places like the Saigon River, Ho Chi Minh City Hall, and the Mariamman Hindu Temple, among other attractions. Afterwards, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Ho Chi Minh CityLeisure Exploration

Day 6: Cu Chi Tunnels Visit | Ho Chi Minh City Tour

Sightseeing

Today, get driven for a visit to Cu Chi Tunnels, an underground network used by the Viet Cong during the war. Next, proceed with a Ho Chi Minh City tour, starting at the Reunification Palace, a historic site symbolising Vietnam's victory. Then, visit the War Remnants Museum, which delves into the history of the Vietnam War, followed by the Saigon Central Post Office, showcasing a blend of neo-classical European architecture and Asian design. Later, get driven to the hotel for overnight stay.

Cu Chi TunnelsReunification PalaceWar Remnants Museum

Day 7: Mekong Delta Tour

Nature

Get ready for an exciting day on the Mekong Delta tour. Board your pre-booked transfer from the hotel to My Tho city, passing through the lush green landscapes of rice paddies. Enjoy a boat ride along the Mekong River, where you can observe fishermen using fish cages from their unique floating houses. Visit the Vinh Trang Pagoda, the largest pagoda in the Mekong Delta. Afterwards, you’ll get transferred to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Mekong DeltaBoat RideVinh Trang Pagoda

Day 8: Departure Day

Checkout

In the morning, check out of the hotel and get transferred to the Tan Son Nhat International Airport to board your flight for an onward journey. This is where your trip ends.

Ho Chi Minh AirportDeparture
