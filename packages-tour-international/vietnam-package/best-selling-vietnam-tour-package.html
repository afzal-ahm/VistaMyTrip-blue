🛶 Overview

Discover the Highlights of Vietnam

Embark on a 5-night, 6-day adventure through Vietnam’s most captivating landscapes and vibrant cities. This tour offers a scenic boat ride to the Mekong Delta, along with visits to iconic cultural sites, bustling cities, and serene countryside. Begin in Hanoi, a kaleidoscope of natural beauty and history, and make your way to Halong Bay, marveling at the beautiful limestone structures over azure blue waters.

Continue your journey to Ho Chi Minh City, a dynamic hub offering exciting nightlife, authentic local delicacies, and artistic colonial buildings. You can also explore the historic significance of the Vietnam War on the former battlegrounds at the Cu Chi Tunnels. This itinerary perfectly balances culture, leisure, and history for an unforgettable Vietnamese escape.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Hanoi

Arrival

Welcome to Vietnam! Upon your arrival at the airport, get transferred to the hotel and check-in. You can spend the remaining day as leisure or explore some of the famous attractions in the city like Hoan Kiem Lake, Ngoc Son Temple, and the Huc Bridge. Afterward, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Hanoi ArrivalPrivate Transfer

Day 1: Excursion to Ninh Binh

Sightseeing

Prepare yourself for an excursion to Ninh Binh City. During this excursion, visit the historic capital of Vietnam, Hao Lu, and its ancient temples. Then, get on a boat from Hao Lu to Tam Coc, and explore its limestone caves. Next, cycle on quiet roads through villages to Bich Dong Pagoda. Afterwards, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Ninh Binh CityHao LuTam Coc Boat Ride

Day 2: Hanoi to Halong Bay Overnight Cruise

Cruise

Prepare yourself for an amazing cruise experience at Halong Bay, a renowned UNESCO Heritage Site. Check-out from the hotel and get transferred to Tuan Chao Harbour to board the cruise. During the wonderful journey, enjoy a visit to Ti Top Island, Duck Islet, and Pearl Farm Village. You can also enjoy kayaking along the shores of the bay. Also, take the Vietnamese cooking demonstration and learn how to make some traditional dishes. Afterward, enjoy your overnight stay on the cruise.

Halong Bay CruiseTi Top IslandKayaking

Day 3: Halong Bay to Hanoi | Transfer to Ho Chi Minh City

Transfer

Today, take the time to explore the wonderful Surprise Cave, before you disembark from the cruise at Tuan Chao Harbour. Afterward, get transferred to the Hanoi airport to board your flight to Ho Chi Minh City (flight tickets included). Upon your arrival, get transferred to the hotel and check-in. After you relax for some time, explore the city's famous places like the Ho Chi Minh Museum and War Remnants Museum. Later, head to the hotel at your own pace for an overnight stay.

Surprise CaveFlight to Ho Chi MinhWar Remnants Museum

Day 4: Mekong Delta Day Trip

Nature

Spend today's day exploring the Mekong Delta on a sightseeing tour. Get transferred from the hotel to see the stunning shrine of Vietnam, Vinh Trang Pagoda. Then, explore some of the bee farms and enjoy a visit to the Tortoise Islet, followed by a special lunch prepared by locals. Also, enjoy a boat ride along the shores of the Mekong River. Later, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Mekong DeltaVinh Trang PagodaTortoise Islet

Day 5: Departure Day

Checkout

Check-out from the hotel get transferred to the airport for your flight back home. This marks the end of your Vietnam trip.

Ho Chi Minh AirportDeparture
