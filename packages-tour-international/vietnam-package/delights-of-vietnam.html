🛶 Overview

A signature Vietnam experience

Embark on a 4-night, 5-day journey through the captivating landscapes and rich culture of Northern Vietnam. This exciting tour offers a unique experience with an overnight cruise at the UNESCO-listed Halong Bay, alongside visits to the vibrant capital city of Hanoi and the historic sites of Ninh Binh.

Explore the charming streets of Hanoi and immerse yourself in its vibrant culture. Set sail on a luxurious overnight cruise through the stunning limestone karsts and emerald waters of Halong Bay, complete with kayaking and swimming. Glide through the stunning landscapes of Tam Coc on a traditional boat ride, surrounded by towering cliffs and lush rice paddies.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Hanoi | Leisure Day

Arrival

Welcome to Hanoi! Upon arrival at Noi Bai International Airport, you will be transferred to your hotel in the city. After checking in, you can relax for a while and spend the rest of the day at leisure. Set out on your own to explore the vibrant streets of Hanoi's Old Quarter, sample local cuisine, or visit nearby attractions like Hoan Kiem Lake and Thap Rua Tower. In the evening, return to the hotel on your own to enjoy an overnight stay.

Hanoi ArrivalLeisure DayPrivate Transfer

Day 1: Transfer to Halong Bay | Overnight Stay at Cruise

Cruise

In the morning, check out and get transferred with a scenic drive to Halong Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its limestone formations and emerald waters. Upon arrival at the Tuan Chau Port, board the cruise ship and set sail through the beautiful bay. En route, visit Ti Top Island and the Pearl Farm Village. While there, participate in fun activities such as kayaking and swimming. In the evening, savour a delicious dinner on board and relax under the starry sky with an overnight stay.

Halong Bay CruiseTi Top IslandKayaking

Day 2: Visit to Surprise Cave | Transfer to Hanoi

Sightseeing

Wake up early to watch a beautiful sunrise over Halong Bay. After breakfast, visit the Surprise Cave (Sung Sot Cave), one of the largest and most impressive caves in Halong Bay. Later, return to the cruise and continue sailing through the bay, before disembarking from the boat in the late morning. From there, get transferred to the hotel in Hanoi and check in for a relaxing overnight stay.

Surprise CaveHalong BayTransfer to Hanoi

Day 3: Ninh Binh Day Tour

Nature

Begin your day by getting ready for a full-day tour to Ninh Binh, often referred to as "Halong Bay on Land" due to the similar landscapes. Get transferred to Hoa Lu, the ancient capital of Vietnam, and visit the historic temples of the Dinh and Le dynasties. Continue to Tam Coc, known for its stunning limestone formations and tranquil rivers. Enjoy a scenic boat ride through the lush landscapes, passing by rice paddies and towering cliffs. Later, get driven to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Ninh BinhHoa LuTam Coc Boat Ride

Day 4: Departure Day

Checkout

On this day, check out from your hotel and get transferred to Noi Bai International Airport for your departure flight. Conclude your Vietnam adventure taking with you unforgettable memories of the trip and some amazing souvenirs. Have a safe journey ahead!
