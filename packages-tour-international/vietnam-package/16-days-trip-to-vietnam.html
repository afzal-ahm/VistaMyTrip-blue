The Ultimate Vietnam Experience

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Hanoi | Leisure Day

Arrival

Commence your Vietnamese adventure with an arrival at the Noi Bai International Airport, followed by a pre-booked transfer to your hotel for check-in. Relax and unwind before immersing yourself in the vibrant energy of Hanoi. Explore the city's nearby markets and nightlife on your own. Later, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Hanoi ArrivalPrivate TransferLeisure Day

Day 1: Hanoi City Tour

Sightseeing

On this day, board the provided transfers for a city tour of Hanoi, starting with a visit to the Tran Quoc Pagoda, Hanoi's oldest temple. Continue to the Ho Chi Minh Complex, exploring the historic residence and mausoleum of the nation's beloved leader. Witness the unique architectural marvel of the One Pillar Pagoda, followed by a leisurely stroll across the iconic Huc Bridge leading to the Ngoc Son Temple. After exploring a few more attractions, get driven to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Tran Quoc PagodaHo Chi Minh ComplexOne Pillar Pagoda

Day 2: Transfer to Halong Bay | Overnight Cruise

Cruise

Get ready for a cruise adventure after checking out of the hotel and getting transferred to Halong Bay. Upon arrival, embark on a Halong Bay cruise at Tuan Chau Harbour. Stop by Ti Top Island and Pearl Farm Village to enjoy recreational activities like kayaking and swimming. Conclude your day with an overnight stay in the cabin as the cruise glides through the limestone karst (hollowed rock formations) and pristine waters of Halong Bay.

Halong Bay CruiseTi Top IslandKayaking

Day 3: Transfer to Sa Pa via Hanoi | Overnight Journey

Transfer

Commence your day with a delightful buffet breakfast while cruising through the bay. Learn the secrets of Vietnamese cuisine with an onboard cooking class before deboarding and getting transferred to Hanoi Train Station. From there, take the train for an overnight journey to Sa Pa.

Cooking ClassTrain to Sa PaOvernight Journey

Day 4: Excursion to Sa Pa | Leisure Day

Trek

Upon arrival at the Sa Pa Train Station in the morning, get transferred to your hotel and check in. Set out for a trek to Cat Cat - Sin Chai Village, where you can explore and experience the local community’s lifestyle. Trek back to the hotel and spend the evening at leisure wherein you can visit Sa Pa Market to try some local delicacies and shop for souvenirs. Return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Sa PaCat Cat VillageSa Pa Market

Day 5: Trek to Lao Chai - Ta Van Village | Overnight Train Journey to Hanoi

Trek & Transfer

Embark on a thrilling trek to Lao Chai – Ta Van village, immersing yourself in the local culture and stunning landscapes. Stroll through the village, interacting with the locals and gaining insights into their daily lives. Trek back to the hotel, check out, and board the train from Sa Pa Train Station for an overnight journey to Hanoi.

Lao ChaiTa Van VillageTrain to Hanoi

Day 6: Transfer to Da-Nang | Transfer to Hoi An | Leisure Day

Flight & Transfer

Upon arrival at Hanoi Train Station in the morning, get transferred to Noi Bai International Airport for your flight to Da Nang International Airport. After reaching Da Nang, get driven to the hotel and check in. Spend the rest of the day at leisure by exploring Hoi An's Night Market, indulging in delicious street food and unique shopping experiences. Also, embark on a boat ride through the Bay Mau Coconut Forest. Later, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Flight to Da NangHoi An Night MarketCoconut Forest

Day 7: My Son Holyland Sanctuary Tour

Heritage

Take the provided transfer from the hotel to explore My Son Sanctuary, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with its intricate stone carvings and ancient ruins. Gain insights into the Champa civilization, culture, and traditions before embarking on a serene boat ride (at an extra cost) along the Thu Bon River, offering breathtaking views of the countryside. Later, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

My Son SanctuaryChampa RuinsThu Bon River

Day 8: Transfer to Nha Trang | Leisure Day

Transfer

On this day, check out of your Hoi An hotel and get transferred to Da Nang International Airport for your flight to Cam Ranh International Airport. Upon arrival, get transferred to the hotel and check in to relax for a while. Spend the afternoon at leisure exploring the beautiful beaches of Nha Trang, including City Beach and Hon Chong Beach, on your own. Later, head back to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Flight to Cam RanhNha TrangCity Beach

Day 9: Nha Trang Island Tour

Island Hopping

Embark on an island-hopping adventure, commencing at Cau Da Port as you get transferred from the hotel. Explore the pristine beaches and vibrant coral reefs of Mun Island before sailing (at an extra cost) to the serene shores of Mot Island, renowned for its tranquil ambiance. Immerse yourself in the crystal-clear waters, engaging in water sports, and relaxing on the beaches of Hon Tam Island. After the adventure, get dropped off at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Mun IslandMot IslandHon Tam Island

Day 10: Leisure Day in Nha Trang

Leisure

Take this day to explore Nha Trang on your own by visiting Po Nagar Temple, dedicated to the worship of the Cham goddess Anapan, and immerse yourself in the serene ambiance of the Long Son Pagoda, renowned for its iconic White Buddha statue. After a fun-filled day, head back to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Po Nagar TempleLong Son Pagoda

Day 11: Transfer to Ho Chi Minh City | Leisure Day

Transfer

Today, check out of the Nha Trang hotel and get transferred to the Cam Ranh International Airport for your flight to Tan Son Nhat International Airport. Upon arrival, get driven to check in at your hotel and set out to explore the city. Pay a visit to the Thien Hau Temple, a testament to the Chinese influence in Vietnam. Also, experience the thrill of Suoi Tien Theme Park, with its diverse range of rides and other attractions. Later, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Flight to Ho Chi MinhThien Hau TempleSuoi Tien Theme Park

Day 12: Ho Chi Minh City Tour | Leisure Day

Sightseeing

Start by getting transferred for a tour of Ho Chi Minh City. Visit Notre Dame Cathedral, famous for its iconic twin towers and stained glass windows. Learn about the Vietnam War at the War Remnants Museum with its exhibits and artefacts. Also, explore the Reunification Palace and Saigon Central Post Office before getting driven to the hotel. You can also visit places like Saigon River and Dong Khoi Street on your own, before returning to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Notre Dame CathedralWar Remnants MuseumReunification Palace

Day 13: Mekong Delta Day Tour

Nature

Prepare yourself for an exciting experience of a sightseeing tour in the Mekong Delta. During this day trip, spend some time at Ving Trang Pagoda, followed by a boat ride in My Tho. Also, enjoy a visit to Tortoise Islet and its various fruit orchards. In the evening, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Mekong DeltaVing Trang PagodaTortoise Islet

Day 14: Leisure Day in Ho Chi Minh City

Leisure

Spend this day exploring the remaining iconic attractions of Ho Chi Minh City at your leisure. Learn about the cultural significance of Vinh Nghiem Pagoda and Mariamman Hindu Temple, dedicated to the Vietnamese Buddhist and Tamil Hindu communities, respectively. For a fun-filled outing, visit the Saigon Zoo and Botanical Gardens, home to over 120 animal species and over 260 plant species. Later, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Vinh Nghiem PagodaMariamman Hindu TempleSaigon Zoo

Day 15: Departure Day

Checkout

As your incredible 16-day journey through Vietnam comes to an end, it's time to prepare for the departure. Check out from the hotel in Ho Chi Minh City and get transferred to the Tan Son Nhat International Airport for your flight. Have a safe journey ahead!
