Simply Thailand Fully Loaded

Thailand is one the most visited countries in Asia and is renowned for its charming culture, beautiful sand beaches, Thai culinary delicacies and some amazing tourist attractions. Enjoy the highlights of Thailand and experience Bangkok and Pattaya, the two most vibrant cities of Thailand. You will enjoy 3 nights stay in Pattaya and 2 nights stay in Bangkok along with complimentary breakfast. Enjoy a Coral Island tour and Pattaya City tour included in this package. The package also includes Economy Class Return Airfare and All Transfers are on seat in coach basis.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Delhi - Bangkok - Pattaya

Arrival

Arrive at Bangkok. After custom clearance, visa processing and baggage claim, Local Tour representative will meet you at Gate No. 3. Thereafter, he will transfer you to Pattaya. Enjoy outside views on your way to Pattaya. On arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Stay overnight at hotel in Pattaya.

BangkokPattaya

Day 2: Pattaya (Coral Island tour)

Sightseeing

Charge yourself with morning breakfast at hotel and set out for an exciting tour to Coral Island. Enjoy water sports, explore beautiful coral, and indulge in parasailing, water-skiing or snorkeling. Stay overnight at hotel in Pattaya.

Coral IslandBeaches

Day 3: Pattaya (Day Free for Leisure)

Leisure

After a morning bed tea, refresh and recharge yourself with breakfast. The day is free at your own leisure. Laze on Pattaya beaches, go for some shopping or explore the raving nightlife. Stay overnight at hotel in Pattaya.

PattayaLeisure

Day 4: Pattaya (City tour) - Bangkok

Transfer

Have your fill at morning breakfast, pack your bags and check out. En-route we take a panoramic city tour of Pattaya and visit World’s biggest Gem Gallery before proceeding to Bangkok. Check in at hotel on arrival. Stay overnight at hotel in Bangkok.

City TourBangkok

Day 5: Bangkok (Day Free for Leisure)

Leisure

Wake up to a vibrant morning and recharge yourself with morning breakfast. Today is free for your own leisure. You can opt for a thrill-filled day at Safari World & Marine Park (on an extra cost) or explore the city. Stay overnight at hotel in Bangkok.

BangkokOptional Safari

Day 6: Bangkok - Delhi

Departure

Pack your bags in the morning & take transfer to the Bangkok Airport (to be arranged on your own) for your flight back home. On your return, we would like to receive your feedback about the trip.
