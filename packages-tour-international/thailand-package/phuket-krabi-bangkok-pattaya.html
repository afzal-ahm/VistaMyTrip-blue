Phuket Krabi Bangkok Pattaya

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Phuket

Arrival

Arrive at Phuket airport. After custom clearance and baggage claim, our local representative will meet you and transfer you to the hotel. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local markets or relaxing by the beach. Stay overnight in Phuket.

PhuketLeisure

Day 2: Phuket (Island Tour / Leisure)

Sightseeing

After breakfast, proceed for an optional full-day tour to Phi Phi Island or take a half-day Phuket city tour. Enjoy the stunning beaches, viewpoints, and vibrant local atmosphere. Stay overnight in Phuket.

Island TourBeaches

Day 3: Phuket to Krabi Transfer

Transfer

Check out from the hotel after breakfast and enjoy a scenic drive to Krabi. Check-in to your hotel upon arrival. The rest of the day is free for you to relax at the beach or explore Krabi's serene environment. Stay overnight in Krabi.

KrabiRelaxation

Day 4: Krabi (Four Islands Tour / Leisure)

Sightseeing

Start the day with breakfast. Today you can opt for the exciting Four Islands Tour by Long Tail boat, visiting Phra Nang Cave, Tup Island, Chicken Island, and Poda Island. Stay overnight in Krabi.

Four IslandsSightseeing

Day 5: Krabi - Bangkok - Pattaya

Transfer

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight to Bangkok. Upon arrival in Bangkok, our representative will meet you and transfer you to Pattaya. Check in at the hotel and relax. Stay overnight in Pattaya.

FlightPattaya

Day 6: Pattaya (Coral Island tour)

Sightseeing

Charge yourself with morning breakfast at the hotel and set out for an exciting tour to Coral Island. Enjoy water sports, explore beautiful coral, and indulge in parasailing, water-skiing or snorkeling. Stay overnight in Pattaya.

Coral IslandWater Sports

Day 7: Pattaya to Bangkok Transfer

Transfer

Have your fill at morning breakfast, pack your bags and check out. En-route to Bangkok, enjoy a panoramic city tour of Pattaya. Check in at the hotel upon arrival in Bangkok. Stay overnight in Bangkok.

BangkokCity Tour

Day 8: Bangkok (City Tour & Leisure)

Leisure

After breakfast, set out to explore the wonderful city. Visit magnificent temples or spend the day shopping at world-class malls and local markets. You can also opt for a thrill-filled day at Safari World. Stay overnight in Bangkok.

ShoppingTemples

Day 9: Bangkok - Delhi (Departure)

Departure

Enjoy your final breakfast in Thailand, pack your bags, and check out. You will be transferred to the Bangkok Airport for your flight back home, taking with you beautiful memories.
