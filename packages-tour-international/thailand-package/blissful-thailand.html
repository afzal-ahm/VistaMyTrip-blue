Blissful Thailand – Honeymoon Special

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Phuket

Arrival

Arrive at Phuket airport. After custom clearance and baggage claim, our local representative will meet you and transfer you to the hotel. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, soaking in the tropical vibes of Phuket with your partner. Stay overnight in Phuket.

PhuketLeisure

Day 2: Phuket - Phi Phi Island Tour

Sightseeing

After breakfast, proceed for a full-day tour of the stunning Phi Phi Islands. Cruise along the turquoise waters, explore Maya Bay, and enjoy snorkeling. A delicious local lunch is included. Return to the hotel in the evening. Stay overnight in Phuket.

Phi Phi IslandSnorkeling

Day 3: Phuket City Tour

City Tour

Enjoy your breakfast and head out for a half-day Phuket city tour. Visit famous viewpoints, beautiful beaches, and explore the charming Sino-Portuguese architecture of Phuket Old Town. Spend the evening at your own pace. Stay overnight in Phuket.

Phuket Old TownViewpoints

Day 4: Phuket to Krabi Transfer

Transfer

Check out from the hotel after breakfast and enjoy a scenic drive to Krabi. Check-in to your hotel upon arrival. The rest of the day is free for you to relax at the beach or explore Krabi's serene environment with your loved one. Stay overnight in Krabi.

KrabiRelaxation

Day 5: Krabi - Four Islands Tour

Sightseeing

Start the day with breakfast and head for the exciting Four Islands Tour by Long Tail boat. Visit Phra Nang Cave, Tup Island, Chicken Island, and Poda Island. Enjoy swimming and a picnic lunch on the beach. Return to the hotel for a relaxing evening. Stay overnight in Krabi.

Four IslandsBeach Picnic

Day 6: Krabi (Day Free for Leisure)

Leisure

Wake up to a beautiful morning and enjoy breakfast. Today is entirely at your leisure. You can opt for a relaxing Thai massage, take a romantic walk along the beach, or explore local cafes and markets. Stay overnight in Krabi.

KrabiLeisure

Day 7: Krabi (Optional Activities)

Optional

Spend another day relaxing or choose an optional excursion like the Emerald Pool tour or a romantic sunset dinner cruise (at extra cost). Make the most of your last evening in this paradise. Stay overnight in Krabi.

KrabiOptional Tour

Day 8: Departure from Krabi/Phuket

Departure

Enjoy your final breakfast in Thailand, pack your bags, and check out. You will be transferred to the airport for your flight back home, taking with you beautiful memories of your honeymoon.

DepartureFlight
