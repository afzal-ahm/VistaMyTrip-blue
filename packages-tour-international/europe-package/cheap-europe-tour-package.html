🗺️ Overview

The best of two iconic capitals

From beautiful Paris, to smoke filled coffeeshops in Amsterdam, there’s so much to see in Europe that you won’t have any problem filling your time. Begin your tour in Paris wandering around the cobblestone streets, visiting the beautiful iconic architecture of the Eiffel Tower, and witnessing the Louvre museum. Move towards the eclectic Dutch capital, float down a canal, and unwind in the typical Dutch countryside alongside traditional wooden houses and windmills.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Paris | The City of Love

Transfer

Arrive at Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport and transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Capture a glimpse of the beautiful city as you pass through the swift roads and beautiful architecture. Rest for some time or explore the nearby places.

ParisPrivate Transfer

Day 1: Paris | Explore the city in a Hop On and Hop Off Bus

City Tour

Hop on board an air-conditioned double-decker bus to take in the city’s top sights. Discover Paris with a Histopad and experience the history. Cruise along the Seine River on a glass-enclosed trimaran. Visit the Eiffel Tower and ascend to the 2nd floor for stunning aerial views.

Eiffel TowerSeine River Cruise

Day 2: Paris | A day at the Louvre Museum

Museum

Visit the most-visited art museum, the Louvre, with a collection spanning ancient civilizations to the mid-19th century. Walk across themed galleries, see the famous Mona Lisa, and spend the evening strolling the streets of Paris.

Louvre MuseumArt & Culture

Day 3: Paris to Amsterdam | Welcome to the Dutch Capital

Travel

Post-check-out, reach the station to board a train for Amsterdam. Reach your pre-booked hotel and get ready to explore the city. Hop on to a Canal Cruise and glide along the UNESCO World Heritage-listed canals while taking in top attractions.

AmsterdamCanal Cruise

Day 4: Amsterdam - Zaanse Schans - Volendam

Countryside

Discover the charms of the Dutch countryside on a day trip to Edam, Volendam, Marken, and Zaanse Schans. Admire quintessential Dutch lighthouses, visit a cheese factory, and learn about traditional wooden clog shoes at a local factory.

Zaanse SchansWindmills

Day 5: Departure | It's time to Bid Farewell

Checkout

Wake up in the morning, have a scrumptious breakfast and pack your bags. Complete the check-out formalities and get ready to leave for Amsterdam airport to end the trip with unforgettable memories.
