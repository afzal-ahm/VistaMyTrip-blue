🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Maldives | Day at Leisure

Arrival

Welcome to the Maldives! Upon arrival at the airport, you’ll be transferred to Sun Siyam Olhuveli Beach and Spa Resort. Check in and take the day to unwind and explore your surroundings. Relax on the pristine beaches, soak in the breathtaking views, or savor delicious cuisine at the resort. In the evening, you can indulge in a unique floating dining experience under the stars or head to the Namaste Restaurant for an authentic Indian flair Cabanas. Enjoy your overnight stay.

Shared Speedboat TransferCheck-in

Day 2: Thrilling Water Sports at the Resort

Leisure & Adventure

This day, you can indulge into a range of thrilling water activities, including diving, kitesurfing, and more. Try your hand at fishing and catch a glimpse of traditional Maldivian angling practices. To immerse yourself in the local culture, join a Maldivian island tour where you'll interact with local fishermen and experience traditional island life. Alternatively, unwind on pristine beaches or explore the resort's other exciting offerings.

BreakfastLunchDinnerGrand Beach Villa

Day 3: Recreational Activities at the Resort

Water Villa

Check out from your villa and make your way to grand Water Villa. Once settled in, you can indulge in various recreational activities which the resort offers. You can play tennis, badminton, football or beach volleyball. Alternatively, you can embark on a thrilling dive to discover a hidden shipwreck in Vaavu Atoll, just a short boat ride away. Swim among vibrant schools of fish and explore the enchanting Underwater World. After a fun filled day, walk back to your villa.

Day 4: Departure Day

Checkout

Check out from the hotel and get transferred to the airport via shared speedboat to board your flight back home, taking with you unforgettable memories of the Maldives.
