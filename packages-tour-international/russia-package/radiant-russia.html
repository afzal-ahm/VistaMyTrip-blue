🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Welcome to Moscow!

Arrival

Welcome to Moscow! Upon arrival at A.S. Pushkin international airport, meet a local representative for your private transfer to the hotel. Check-in and enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.

MoscowRelaxation

Day 2: Moscow Sightseeing

City Tour

Meet your guide for a Metro tour to explore the beautifully designed stations. Continue to the Red Square area for a walking tour covering the Kremlin Inside Territory, St. Basil’s Cathedral, Lenin Mausoleum, and Gum Shopping Center.

KremlinRed Square

Day 3: Panoramic City Tour of Moscow

Sightseeing

Proceed for a panoramic tour seeing Sparrow Hills, Moscow State University, Stalin Buildings, White House, and Cathedrals of Christ the Savior. Enjoy a walking tour of Arbat street followed by a scenic Moscow Radisson river cruise.

Arbat StreetRadisson Cruise

Day 4: Moscow to St. Petersburg

Transit

Check out and transfer to the railway station for the high-speed Sapsan train journey to St. Petersburg. Upon reaching, transfer to your hotel and spend the evening at leisure.

Sapsan TrainSt. Petersburg

Day 5: Discovering St. Petersburg

Culture

Visit the world-famous Hermitage Museum and Kazan Cathedral. Enjoy a panoramic city tour featuring the Church on the Savior of Split Blood, St. Isaac’s Cathedral, The Bronze Horseman, and the Cruiser Aurora.

Hermitage MuseumKazan Cathedral

Day 6: Peterhof Palace & Fortress

History

Explore the magnificent Peterhof Palace and its stunning parklands. Next, visit the historic Peter and Paul Fortress. Wrap up the day with a relaxing 1-hour Neva Boat Ride.

Peterhof PalaceNeva Boat Ride

Day 7: Departure

Checkout

Enjoy breakfast at your hotel before checking out. Transfer to the airport for your onward journey, bringing home treasured memories of your Russian holiday.

DepartureBreakfast
