🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Welcome to Moscow!

Arrival

Welcome to Moscow - the capital and the largest city of Russia. On arrival, check-in to your hotel. The evening is free at leisure.

MoscowRelaxation

Day 2: Guided city tour of Moscow

City Tour

Visit the Red Square, see the St. Basil’s Cathedral, the White House and Christ the Saviour Cathedral. Enjoy a cable car ride at Sparrow Hill, explore the Metro Tour, and watch the Russian Circus.

Red SquareRussian Circus

Day 3: Fly to Murmansk – Northern Lights Tour

Arctic Night

Take a flight to Murmansk, known as the Russian Lapland. Tonight, experience the Northern Lights phenomenon and ride around the surroundings to observe the auroras.

MurmanskNorthern Lights

Day 4: Visit Saami Village

Adventure

Visit the Saami village and experience the popular Saami show. Enjoy a Husky Dog Sleigh and Snowmobile Ride in the snow. Later, an orientation tour of Murmansk.

Saami VillageHusky Sleigh

Day 5: Fly to St Petersburg

City Tour

Fly to St Petersburg. On arrival proceed for a guided city tour including Issac's Cathedral, The Peter and Paul Fortress, and Kazan cathedral.

St PetersburgKazan Cathedral

Day 6: Palaces & Museums

Culture

Visit the iconic Hermitage Museum holding masterpiece paintings. Later, visit the Grand Maket; a private museum showcasing the largest miniature model of Russia.

Hermitage MuseumGrand Maket

Day 7: Train to Moscow

Transit

Check out and proceed to the railway station to board the train onto Moscow. On arrival, check in to the hotel and relax.

MoscowTransit

Day 8: Departure

Checkout

After continental breakfast, transfer to the airport. Board your flight back home with pleasant memories of your Russian tour.

DepartureBreakfast
