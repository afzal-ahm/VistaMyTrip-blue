🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Kuala Lumpur

Arrival

Welcome to Malaysia! Upon arrival at Kuala Lumpur International Airport, you will be transferred to your pre-booked hotel. Upon checking in and settling into your room, spend the remainder of the day at your leisure. Wander through the vibrant streets, marvel at the city's charming architecture, and soak in the scenic views. You can visit the KL Tower or head to MinNature Malaysia.

Kuala LumpurLeisure

Day 2: Kuala Lumpur City Tour | Visit to Petronas Twin Towers

City Tour

In the morning, you will be driven for a city tour of Kuala Lumpur. Marvel at the towering KL Tower, dive into the history at Tugu Negara, and admire the architectural beauty of the National Mosque of Malaysia. Next, you'll be transferred to the Petronas Twin Towers for stunning panoramic views of the city skyline. Enjoy the rich cultural tapestry of Malaysia's vibrant capital.

KL TowerTwin Towers

Day 3: Day Trip to Sunway Lagoon Park

Excursion

Today, you will be driven to Sunway Lagoon Park for an exciting day (tickets included). Dive into the Water Park's wave pools and thrilling slides or explore the wildlife park area to encounter exotic animals. Experience the Scream Park's haunted attractions and head to the Amusement Park with rides for all ages for a fun time. It's a day packed with excitement!

Sunway LagoonWater ParkWildlife

Day 4: Day at Leisure

Leisure

Enjoy a relaxing slow morning at the hotel. Today is yours to explore Kuala Lumpur at your own pace. You can indulge in some shopping at Bukit Bintang, visit the cultural sites, or simply relax and enjoy the hotel's amenities. Consider visiting the vibrant Chinatown or the peaceful KL Forest Eco Park. The day is yours to create unforgettable memories.

ChinatownBukit Bintang

Day 5: Departure Day

Checkout

In the morning, get ready to check out from the hotel. You will be transferred from the hotel to the airport. Board your flight back home and take a bag full of memories with you.
