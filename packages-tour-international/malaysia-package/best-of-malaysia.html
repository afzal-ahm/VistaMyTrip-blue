A signature Malaysia experience

Main Stops

Kuala Lumpur, Langkawi, and Genting Highlands.

Main Draw

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Kuala Lumpur

Arrival

Upon arrival at Kuala Lumpur International Airport, you will be transferred to your pre-booked hotel. After checking in and settling in your room, enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.

Kuala LumpurLeisure

Day 2: Kuala Lumpur City Tour

City Tour

Marvel at the towering KL Tower, explore the historical Tugu Negara, and admire the architecture of National Mosque Of Malaysia. Visit Petronas Twin Towers to enjoy panoramic views.

KL TowerTwin Towers

Day 3: Excursion to Genting Highland With Batu Caves | Transfer to Langkawi

Excursion

Embark on a day trip to Genting Highlands. On the way, visit the iconic Batu Caves. Later, transfer to the airport for your flight to Langkawi.

Batu CavesGentingTransfer

Day 4: City Tour Of Langkawi

Sightseeing

Ride the cable car at Mount Mat Chinchang, visit Atma Alam Batik Art Village, and end your tour at Eagle Square, perfect for photography.

Cable CarEagle Square

Day 5: Island Hopping Tour Of Langkawi

Island Hopping

Visit Dayang Bunting Island, explore Pulau Singa Besar for eagle feeding, and visit Beras Basah Island for white sandy beaches and turquoise waters.

Dayang BuntingPulau Singa Besar

Day 6: Departure Day

Checkout

In the morning, get ready to check out from the hotel. You will be transferred to the airport in Langkawi. Board your flight back home with unforgettable memories.
