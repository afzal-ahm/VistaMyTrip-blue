🇦🇿 Overview

Discover the heart of Azerbaijan

Start your adventure in Baku, the capital city where ancient and modern worlds collide. Wander through the UNESCO-listed Old City (Icherisheher), explore Gobustan National Park's millennia-old petroglyphs and mud volcanoes, and head to Gabala for a picturesque retreat. The highlight of your tour awaits at Yanar Dagh, offering an unforgettable glimpse into Azerbaijan’s fiery heritage.

Main Stops

Baku, Old City, Gabala, and Gobustan National Park.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Baku & City Sightseeing Tour

Baku

Welcome to Azerbaijan! Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel. Later, explore modern Baku with visits to architectural marvels like the Flame Towers & Heydar Aliyev Center, ending with a magical stroll on Nizami Street.

ArrivalFlame TowersNizami Street

Day 2: Baku Old City Tour

Old City

Step back in time exploring Icherisheher (Old City). Admire the Palace of the Shirvanshahs, ascend the ancient Maiden Tower for breathtaking views, and visit the intricate Juma Mosque.

Maiden TowerShirvanshahs Palace

Day 3: Transfer to Gabala & Tufandag Mountain

Gabala

Journey to Gabala. Enjoy a day trip featuring a cable car ride at Tufandag Mountain for panoramic views, and participate in optional activities like hiking or zip-lining before resting at your Gabala hotel.

GabalaTufandag MountainCable Car

Day 4: Gobustan National Park & Mud Volcanoes

Gobustan

Journey back to Baku. Explore prehistoric rock carvings at Gobustan Museum, then go on an adventure to witness the bubbling Mud Volcanoes, an otherworldly landscape.

PetroglyphsMud Volcanoes

Day 5: Absheron Peninsula Tour

Absheron

Journey to the Absheron Peninsula, beginning with a visit to Yanar Dag, the legendary "Burning Mountain." Then dive into history at the Ateshgah Fire Temple, an ancient Zoroastrian pilgrimage site.

Yanar DagAteshgah Fire Temple

Day 6: Departure Day

Checkout

Check-out from the hotel, and get transferred to Heydar Aliyev International Airport for your onward journey. Depart Azerbaijan with cherished memories of your unforgettable adventures.
