🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Baku & Baku Sightseeing Tour

Baku

Salam! Welcome to Baku! Upon arrival, get transferred to the hotel and check-in. Afterward, in the evening, board your transfer from the hotel for the Baku panoramic tour. Wander around the beautiful Nizami Street and take-in the views of Baku's skyline from Highland Park's vantage point.

ArrivalNizami StreetHighland Park

Day 2: Baku Old City Sightseeing Tour

Old City

Engage yourself in Baku's history on a city tour. Board the transfer from the hotel and see places like the Maiden Tower and the Palace of Shrivanshahs. Visit Little Venice and take a leisurely walk along the beautiful Boulevard before enjoying a sea voyage in the Caspian Sea.

Maiden TowerLittle VeniceCaspian Sea

Day 3: Transfer to Shahdag & Mountain Ski Resort

Shahdag

Set off on a memorable adventure in Shahdag. Check-out and get transferred from the hotel to Shahdag Ski Resort. Enjoy fun-filled activities like skiing and a cable car ride in the beautiful landscapes of the Shahdag Mountains.

ShahdagSki ResortCable Car

Day 4: Transfer to Baku & Absheron Peninsula Tour

Absheron

After you check-out from the hotel, get transferred to the Absheron Peninsula. See the beautiful Yanardagh and the ancient Zoroastrian temple. Also, admire the grandeur of Mardakan Castle and see the amazing architecture of Heydar Aliyev Center.

YanardaghZoroastrian TempleHeydar Aliyev Center

Day 5: Departure Day

Checkout

Its time to say goodbye to all the amazing experiences. Check-out from the hotel and get transferred to the airport to board your flight back home. Have a safe journey!

Airport DropDeparture
