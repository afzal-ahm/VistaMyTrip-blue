🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Singapore | Embark the Royal Caribbean Cruise

Embarkation

Welcome to Singapore! After your arrival at the Singapore airport and completion of documentation, you'll be driven to the Marina Bay Cruise Center to embark the Royal Caribbean cruise. Once aboard, you can enjoy the entire day as you please. Opt for soothing spa session, turn up the excitement with bumper cars or go to war against cosmic invaders at the Royal Caribbean arcade. At night you can also enjoy an outdoor movie night under the stars in the open sea.

Singapore ArrivalMarina BayEmbark Cruise

Day 1: Cruise to Penang | Explore the Island

Excursion

In the morning, get ready for an exciting day. Relax at the Solarium or spend your time shopping souvenirs at the boutique outlets. Your cruise will be heading to the beautiful Penang island in Malaysia for a day excursion. Hop off the cruise and head out, visiting iconic landmarks like Penang Bird Park, Penang War Museum, and other such attractions. Later, you will head back to the cruise and spend the rest of the day exploring the nightlife or relaxing onboard.

Penang IslandGeorge TownNightlife

Day 2: Leisure Day on Cruise

Leisure

Today, experience the adventurous and exciting entertainment options of the Royal Caribbean cruise. Savor exquisite flavors at the dining venues or admire the impressive art collection. You can also indulge in wine tasting, or learn a thing or two at the salsa dance classes. For a dash of adventure, shoot hoops in the sports area or scale the rock climbing wall with a stunning ocean view. Cap off the day by diving into the vibrant nightlife before retreating to your cabin.

Onboard EntertainmentFine DiningRelaxation

Day 3: Disembark | Departure Day

Departure

After breakfast and a leisurely morning, you will disembark from the Royal Carribean Cruise at the Marina Bay Cruise Centre Singapore. From the cruise center, you will be driven to the Singapore airport for your flight back home. Bid farewell to the stunning Singapore skyline, taking back home many unforgettable memories.
