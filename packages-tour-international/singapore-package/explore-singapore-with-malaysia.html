🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Singapore | Enjoy Night Safari in the Evening

Arrival

Welcome to Singapore, ‘The Garden City’. Upon arrival at the airport, you will be transferred to your hotel. Check-in and relax. Later in the evening, embark on a Night Safari at the Singapore Zoo, the world's first nocturnal wildlife park, home to over 130 species. Hop on a tram ride that takes you through various geographical zones such as the Himalayan Foothills, Southeast Asian Rainforest, Indian Subcontinent and more. Afterward, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

SingaporeNight Safari

Day 1: Singapore City Tour

Sightseeing

In the morning, get transferred to begin your full-day Singapore city tour. Explore iconic landmarks like the Helix Bridge, Thian Hock Keng Temple, and the majestic Marina Bay Sands. Then, marvel at the Merlion statue and enjoy panoramic views from the Singapore Flyer. In the evening, indulge in the vibrant energy of Chinatown's bustling markets or shop at Suntec City Mall. Lastly, get transferred back to the hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

Helix BridgeMarina Bay SandsSingapore Flyer

Day 2: Day Trip to Universal Studios | Explore the Seven Different Zones

Theme Park

Get transferred to Universal Studios and prepare yourself for a thrilling adventure. Explore seven movie-themed zones, meet beloved characters in Hollywood, brave thrilling rides in Sci-Fi City, and immerse yourself in movie magic in New York. Discover ancient mysteries in Ancient Egypt and embark on Jurassic Park adventures in the Lost World. Conclude your day exploring magical kingdoms in the Far Far Away zone before being driven to the hotel.

Universal StudiosTheme ZonesRides

Day 3: Day Trip to Sentosa Island | Enjoy Adventure Cove Waterpark & Wings of time show

Sentosa

Begin your day by getting transferred to Mount Faber Station. Then, experience a scenic cable car ride to Sentosa Station. Upon arrival, head to Adventure Cove Waterpark, featuring thrilling slides and snorkeling adventures. As the evening sets in, be mesmerized by the captivating Wings Of Time laser light show set against the sea. Feel free to explore more island activities at your own cost before returning to your hotel for the night.

Sentosa IslandAdventure CoveWings of Time

Day 4: Singapore to Kuala Lumpur | Kuala Lumpur Sightseeing tour

Kuala Lumpur

Bid adieu to Singapore as you check out from the hotel and get transferred to begin your journey to Malaysia. Upon arrival in Kuala Lumpur, begin your city tour. Visit the King's Palace (Istana Negara), the National Mosque, and the Sultan Abdul Samad Building. Visit the National Museum and gain insights into Malaysia's rich history and cultural heritage. Stop by the Handicraft Centre to admire unique handmade items such as batik textiles. Later, get driven to the hotel. Check-in and rest.

Kuala LumpurIstana NegaraNational Museum

Day 5: Excursion to Genting Highland | Visit The Batu Caves

Genting

After breakfast, get transferred to the enchanting Genting Highlands. Experience the breathtaking beauty of Genting Highlands as you embark on a scenic road journey. Explore the vibrant casino resort and enjoy various activities in the Snow World. On your way back, make a stop at the majestic Batu Caves and marvel at the giant statue of Lord Murugan. Conclude your day with some shopping before being transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Genting HighlandsSnow WorldBatu Caves

Day 6: Departure | Leave with Happy Memories

Checkout

Check out from your hotel and bid adieu to Malaysia as you get transferred to the Malaysia airport. Depart for your flight back home with pleasant memories of your unforgettable journey.
