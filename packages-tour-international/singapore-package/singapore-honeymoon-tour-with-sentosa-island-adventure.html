🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Singapore & Leisure

Arrival

Begin with a flight from Delhi to Singapore. Upon arrival at the Singapore Changi Airport, you will be driven to your hotel. Check in and spend your day at leisure. You can visit IFly Singapore for an exciting indoor skydiving experience or stroll through the National Orchid Garden to admire its stunning collection of orchids. You can also spend time at the Singapore Zoo, renowned for its diverse wildlife and immersive habitats. Afterwards, return to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Flight from DelhiSingaporeLeisure Day

Day 1: City Tour, Marina Bay Sands & Gardens by the Bay

Sightseeing

Today, get driven for a sightseeing tour. Begin by visiting Merlion Park, where you'll see the iconic Merlion statue, symbolizing the city’s maritime heritage. Stroll across the Helix Bridge, and explore the vibrant streets of Chinatown. Next, head to Marina Bay Sands SkyPark to take in panoramic views of Singapore’s skyline from its observation deck. Continue to Gardens By The Bay and wander through the Cloud Forest and the Flower Dome. Afterward, get transferred to your hotel.

City TourMarina Bay SandsGardens By The Bay

Day 2: River Wonders & Night Safari Zoo

Wildlife

Today, get driven to River Wonders Zoo, where you can explore fascinating exhibits such as the Amazon Flooded Forest, which showcases unique aquatic life, and the Giant Panda Forest, home to adorable pandas. Afterwards, get transferred to your hotel to get some rest and get driven to the Night Safari Zoo in the evening. There, you’ll enjoy a tram ride through different zones to see nocturnal animals like tigers, lions, giraffes, and elephants. End the day with getting driven to your hotel.

River WondersNight Safari

Day 3: Universal Studios Singapore

Theme Park

Begin your day by boarding your transfer to Universal Studios, and dive into seven immersive zones: Hollywood, New York, Sci-Fi City, Ancient Egypt, Lost World, Far Far Away, and Madagascar. Enjoy exciting rides, interact with your favorite movie and TV characters, and experience exciting parades and live street performances. After an action-packed day, you'll be transferred back to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Universal StudiosRides & Shows

Day 4: Sentosa Island, Adventure Cove & Wings of Time

Sentosa

Today, get transferred to Mount Faber and board a cable car to Sentosa Island. Once there, spend the day at Adventure Cove Waterpark, where you can enjoy exciting rides like the Tidal Twister and float along the Adventure River. In the evening, experience the enchanting Wings Of Time show, featuring mesmerizing storytelling and impressive light and sound effects. After the show, ride the cable car back to Mount Faber and then get driven to your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

Sentosa IslandAdventure CoveWings of Time

Day 5: Departure Day

Checkout

In the morning, check out from the hotel and get driven to Singapore Changi Airport for your onward flight (flight included). This marks the end of your trip.
