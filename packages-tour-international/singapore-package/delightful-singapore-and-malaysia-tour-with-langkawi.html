🌍 Overview

The Ultimate Asian Twin-Country Escape

Embark on a captivating holiday that takes you through the vibrant cities of Singapore and Malaysia, with a special highlight on the enchanting Langkawi. Begin your adventure in Singapore, where modern marvels like the Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and Sentosa Island’s attractions await. Enjoy world-class shopping, exquisite dining, and vibrant street markets as you explore this dynamic city.

Next, travel to Malaysia’s bustling capital, Kuala Lumpur, to marvel at the Petronas Twin Towers and explore cultural treasures like Batu Caves. Finally, unwind in Langkawi, a tropical paradise known for its pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and crystal-clear waters. This holiday offers the perfect mix of urban exploration, cultural discovery, and beachside relaxation, making it an unforgettable getaway.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Singapore | Visit to Night Safari Zoo

Arrival

Welcome to Singapore! Upon arrival, you'll be transferred to your hotel for check in. Later, in the evening, get driven to the world's first nocturnal zoo, Night Safari Zoo, where you can walk through 7 unique trails, and enjoy a fire dance show and animal show. Watch creatures like White African Lion, and Tasmanian Devil, showcase their talents in the famous Creatures of the Night show. Later, get transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

SingaporeNight Safari Zoo

Day 1: Singapore Sightseeing Tour | Leisure Day

Sightseeing

In the morning, you’ll be driven for a sightseeing tour of Singapore, starting with attractions such as Merlion Park, Chinatown, and Plaza Singapura. Walk across the world’s largest double Helix Bridge and admire the unique lotus-shaped architecture of the ArtScience Museum before getting driven back to the hotel. Later, you can explore the city on your own and visit places like Singapore Zoo, Gardens By The Bay and River Wonder. Later, return to your hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Merlion ParkHelix BridgeChinatown

Day 2: Day Trip Sentosa Island | Visit to Madame Tussauds & Wings of Time Show

Sentosa

In the morning, board your transfer for a day trip to Sentosa Island. Take a cable car ride from the Mount Faber Station, straight to the island, offering panoramic views of Singapore. Once you reach, explore Madame Tussauds, where you can see lifelike wax figures of celebrities. Enjoy the scenic Skyride at the Skyline Luge. Then, as evening falls, be captivated by the Wings Of Time laser show by the sea. After a fulfilling day, get transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Sentosa IslandMadame TussaudsWings of Time

Day 3: Day Trip to Universal Studios

Theme Park

Today, get ready to board your transfer from the hotel to the Universal Studios. Enjoy 28 thrilling rides and attractions that bring your favourite movies to life. Explore the seven zones: Hollywood, Sci-Fi City, New York, Ancient Egypt, Lost World, Madagascar, and Far Far Away. Don’t miss the ‘Hollywood Dreams Light Up Parade,’ featuring themed floats, characters, and performers. As the day ends, return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Universal StudiosRides & Parades

Day 4: Transfer to Langkawi | Langkawi Sky Bridge & 6D Cinemotion

Langkawi

In the morning, head to the airport for your flight to Langkawi. Check in at the hotel and relax for a while. Later, you’ll be dropped at the Langkawi Cable Car from where you can take a scenic cable car ride to Machinchang Mountain. A curved bridge suspended high above the lush rainforest, the Sky Bridge offers spectacular views of Malaysia’s Langkawi archipelago. Later, head to the 6D Cinemotion for an immersive adventure before getting transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Langkawi FlightCable CarSky Bridge

Day 5: Langkawi Island Sightseeing Tour

Island Tour

Today, your Langkawi Island tour starts at Underwater World, Malaysia's largest aquarium, showcasing over 4,000 marine species and a 15-metre-long tunnel walkway. Head to Kuah Town or you can visit Atma Alam to see traditional batik art. Visit Eagle Square, featuring a 12-metre-tall eagle statue overlooking the bay. Later, explore the Gamat Factory and learn about Gamat, a special sea creature known for its medicinal properties before getting driven back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Underwater WorldEagle SquareGamat Factory

Day 6: Transfer to Kuala Lumpur | Sightseeing Tour

Kuala Lumpur

In the morning, check out of the hotel in Langkawi and get transferred to Kuala Lumpur. Post check in, relax for a while before heading out for a city sightseeing tour. Visit Sultan Abdul Samad Building followed by the National Mosque Of Malaysia. Continue to Tugu Negara, and pay your respects to the heroes. You can also explore Buckingham Palace, the King's Palace, and Istana Negara. Later, in the evening, get transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Kuala LumpurCity TourIstana Negara

Day 7: Day Trip to Genting Highlands and Batu Caves

Genting

Today, get transferred to Genting Highlands. Take a cable car ride from Awana SkyWay to reach the highlands. Once at the top, explore a variety of amusement parks, and shops that cater to all ages. Try your luck at the Genting Casino for an exciting experience. Later, take your cable car ride back to the station for a transfer back to the hotel. On the way, visit Batu Caves and admire the 140-foot-tall statue of Lord Murugan. After this, reach your hotel for an overnight stay.

Genting HighlandsAwana SkyWayBatu Caves

Day 8: Departure Day

Checkout

In the morning, check out of the hotel. Then, board your transfer to Kuala Lumpur International Airport. This is where your trip comes to an end.
