🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival at Bagdogra – Phuentsholing

Arrival

Arrive at Bagdogra and transfer to Phuentsholing, the gateway to Bhutan. Spend the night relaxing after your journey.

BagdograPhuentsholing

Day 2: Phuentsholing – Thimphu

Scenic Drive

Travel through stunning mountain scenery to Thimphu. Check into your hotel and enjoy the cool, fresh mountain breeze.

PhuentsholingThimphu

Day 3: Thimphu

City Tour

Explore Thimphu's sights, including the National Library, Handicraft Centre, Royal Takin Preserve, and the magnificent Buddha Dordenma Statue.

ThimphuTashichho DzongBuddha Dordenma

Day 4: Thimphu – Punakha

Heritage

Drive to Punakha via the breathtaking Dochula Pass, featuring 108 memorial chortens. Visit the Chimi Lhakhang and enjoy white water rafting.

Dochula PassPunakhaChimi Lhakhang

Day 5: Punakha – Paro

Transfer

Visit the majestic Punakha Dzong before transferring to the beautiful Paro valley.

Punakha DzongParo

Day 6: Paro – Chele La Pass – Paro

Excursion

Take an excursion to Chele La Pass, the highest motorable road in Bhutan, offering stunning views of the snow-capped Himalayan peaks.

Chele La PassParo

Day 7: Paro – Taktshang Monastery

Tiger's Nest

Embark on the iconic hike to Taktshang Monastery (Tiger’s Nest), clinging to a cliff 900 meters above the valley floor.

Tiger's NestParo DzongTa-Dzong

Day 8: Paro – Phuentsholing – Siliguri

Return Journey

Bid farewell to Bhutan as you drive back through Phuentsholing to Siliguri for your final overnight stay.

PhuentsholingSiliguri

Day 9: Departure

Checkout

After breakfast, transfer to Bagdogra for your onward journey back home.
