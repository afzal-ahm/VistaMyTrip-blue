🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival at Bagdogra – Phuentsholing

Arrival

Arrive at Bagdogra airport and proceed to Phuentsholing. Enjoy the scenic drive and check-in for your first night.

BagdograPhuentsholing

Days 2-3: Phuentsholing – Thimphu

Capital City

Travel to Thimphu and explore Kharbandi Gompa, BBS Clock Tower, Tashichho Dzong, Royal Takin Preserve, and the majestic Buddha Dordenma Statue.

ThimphuTashichho DzongBuddha Dordenma

Day 4: Thimphu – Punakha

Heritage

Drive to Punakha via Dochula Pass. Enjoy white water rafting and visit the beautiful Punakha Dzong.

Dochula PassPunakha Dzong

Days 5-7: Punakha – Paro

Monasteries & Passes

Transfer to Paro. Over three days, visit Ta-Dzong, Paro Dzong, embark on an excursion to Chele La Pass, and hike to the iconic Taktshang Monastery (Tiger's Nest).

ParoChele La PassTiger's Nest

Day 8: Paro – Phuentsholing – Siliguri

Scenic Drive

Drive back through Phuentsholing to Siliguri for your final overnight stay before departure.

PhuentsholingSiliguri

Day 9: Departure

Checkout

Transfer to Bagdogra airport for your flight back to Pune with beautiful memories of Bhutan.

SiliguriBagdograDeparture
