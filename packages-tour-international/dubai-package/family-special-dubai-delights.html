✈️ Overview

Skyline and Sandscapes

Dubai, a city where modernity meets tradition, offers an awe-inspiring blend of futuristic architecture and serene desert landscapes. The skyline is a testament to architectural ambition, featuring world-famous structures like the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building on Earth, and the sail-shaped Burj Al Arab.

Just beyond the city’s bustling streets lie Dubai’s majestic sandscapes. The golden dunes of the Arabian Desert offer tranquility and adventure. Visitors can enjoy thrilling activities like dune bashing, camel rides, and sandboarding, or immerse themselves in Bedouin culture through desert safaris, complete with traditional feasts and mesmerizing belly dance performances under starlit skies. This juxtaposition of towering skyscrapers and timeless deserts defines Dubai as a city of contrasts.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai | Day at Leisure

Arrival

Welcome to Dubai, 'The Pearl of the Gulf'. Upon arrival at Dubai Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel for check-in. Spend the rest of the day at leisure. You can head out and explore the city on your own. Visit the famous Dubai Museum to learn about the city's history or the beautiful Jumeirah Mosque. Indulge in shopping at Dubai Mall, one of the world's largest malls. Alternatively, stroll along Dubai Marina, dine at waterfront restaurants, or take a boat tour. Later, return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalDubai MuseumDubai Mall

Day 2: Visit the Iconic Burj Khalifa | Dubai Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner

Highlights

In the morning, you'll be transferred to the iconic Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world. Ascend to the 124th floor in just 35 seconds on a double-decker elevator. Enjoy exceptional views of the city from the observation decks on the 124th and 125th floors. Later, you'll be driven to an exciting Dubai desert safari. As the sun sets, savor a barbecue dinner accompanied by performances like belly dancing and the Tanoura dance. Finally, you'll be driven back to your hotel.

Burj KhalifaDesert SafariBBQ Dinner

Day 3: Day Trip to ​​IMG Worlds of Adventure

Theme Park

Today, you'll be driven to IMG Worlds Of Adventure, the largest indoor-themed entertainment destination. Explore its six themed zones, including Marvel, Cartoon Network, and Lost Valley Dinosaur Adventure. Meet your favorite characters in the Marvel Universe and embark on thrilling rides such as Velociraptor, Predator, & the Forbidden Territory. Wander through IMG Boulevard, the park's themed retail and dining district, perfect for dining and shopping. Later, you'll be driven back to your hotel.

IMG WorldsTheme RidesMarvel Universe

Day 4: Dubai Marina Dhow Cruise

Cruise

Enjoy your half day at leisure, exploring various sites such as Dolphinarium and Global Village. In the evening, get driven to Dubai Marina Harbour for an exciting Dubai Marina Dhow Cruise. Start sailing while enjoying views of the towering skyscrapers lining the marina. Admire prominent landmarks such as the Ain Wheel, JBR, and Atlantis. Experience five-star hospitality while savoring a delicious BBQ meal on board. Afterward, you’ll be driven back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Global VillageDubai MarinaDhow Cruise

Day 5: Departure Day

Checkout

Bid adieu to Dubai and get transferred to the Dubai Airport. Upon arrival, board your flight back home with pleasant memories.
