Discovering Dubai

Embark on an unforgettable adventure to Dubai, the jewel of the United Arab Emirates, with our expertly curated travel packages. From its iconic skyline, crowned by the towering Burj Khalifa, to the serene beauty of its vast desert dunes, Dubai offers a perfect mix of luxury, culture, and adventure. Explore bustling souks, indulge in world-class shopping, or relax on pristine beaches. Thrill-seekers can enjoy desert safaris and water sports, while history enthusiasts will marvel at the heritage sites along Dubai Creek. With personalized itineraries and unparalleled service, we ensure your journey to the Golden Emirates is nothing short of extraordinary.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai | Dhow Cruise Dinner

Arrival

Welcome to Dubai! After arriving at Dubai Airport, you'll be driven to your hotel for check-in. In the evening, head to Dubai Marina Harbour for a Dhow Cruise, where you can enjoy the city's sparkling skyline, indulge in a delicious dinner, and be captivated by a traditional Tanoura dance performance. Following this enchanting evening, get driven to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

ArrivalDubai MarinaDhow Cruise

Day 2: Dubai Sightseeing Tour | Visit the Burj Khalifa

Highlights

Today, explore Dubai's iconic landmarks. Begin with a transfer from your hotel to Jumeirah Beach, where you can admire the breathtaking Atlantis Hotel. Visit the Dubai Museum and marvel at the architectural beauty of Jumeirah Mosque. Cover the major spots in Dubai before heading to the Dubai Mall. Walk to the Burj Khalifa and end your day with stunning panoramic views from the 124th and 125th floors of the world's tallest building. Afterward, you'll be driven back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Jumeirah BeachDubai MuseumBurj Khalifa

Day 3: Visit Dubai Frame | Enjoy Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner

Adventure

Today, embark on an adventure-filled day with a convenient hotel transfer, leading to the iconic Dubai Frame, a masterpiece of design offering breathtaking panoramas of the city. Then, get ready for an adrenaline rush with a thrilling desert expedition, featuring dune rides, sand-surfing, and a leisurely camel ride. As night falls, savor a delectable outdoor feast under the starry sky, accompanied by captivating live performances. Finally, get driven to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Dubai FrameDesert SafariBBQ Dinner

Day 4: Day Trip to the Aquaventure Waterpark

Waterpark

Today, you will be transferred to the Atlantis Aquaventure, the world’s largest waterpark. Dive into the excitement with over 30 slides and attractions, including Ray feeding, Shark Snorkeling, and the thrilling twin racing tube ride. After a day of adventure and fun, you’ll be driven back to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

AquaventureAtlantisWater Rides

Day 5: Visit Dolphinarium | Explore AYA Universe | Discover Museum of Future

Future

In the morning, get transferred from the hotel to embark on an action-packed day starting with the Dolphinarium, where you will interact with intelligent dolphins. Next, immerse yourself in the futuristic AYA Universe, indulging in cutting-edge virtual reality experiences. Following this, explore the Museum Of The Future, a pioneering institution showcasing innovative technologies and visionary ideas. After a thrilling day, get driven back to the hotel.

DolphinariumAYA UniverseMuseum of Future

Day 6: Abu Dhabi Sightseeing Tour | Ferrari World Park Visit

Theme Park

Today, set out on an exploration of Abu Dhabi, starting with a sightseeing tour of the city's iconic landmarks. Visit the magnificent Sheikh Zayed Mosque, take in views of the Arabian Gulf at the Emirates Palace, and explore the Heritage Village and the Louvre Museum. After immersing yourself in the city's culture, head to Ferrari World Park for exhilarating rides, including roller coasters and ziplines. End the day with a transfer back to your Dubai hotel for an overnight stay.

Abu DhabiSheikh Zayed MosqueFerrari World

Day 7: Departure Day

Checkout

Check-out from the hotel in Dubai and get transferred to the airport for your flight back home, carrying with you cherished memories. This marks the end of your trip.
