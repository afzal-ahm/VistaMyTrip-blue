🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai

Arrival

In the morning, board your flight from Delhi airport to reach Dubai. Welcome to Dubai! Upon your arrival at the airport, you'll be transferred to the hotel for check-in and relax for the day. Later, make the most of your day by exploring JBR Beach for a sun-soaked day by the sea. Then, take a walk at Safa Park and visit the Deira Clocktower. End the day with delightful Emirati cuisine, savouring dishes like shawarma and falafel. Finally, head back to your hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

JBR BeachSafa ParkDeira Clocktower

Day 2: Marina Dhow Dinner Cruise Tour

Cruise

Today, enjoy the first half of your day at leisure, where you can relax until the evening. Or you can head out for some fun at Aura Skypool, Kite Beach, and Dolphin Bay. In the evening, you'll be taken to the Dubai harbour, where you'll embark on a Marina Dhow Cruise. As you sail on the waters, enjoy a 3-course buffet and live shows on board, along with cultural performances, including the traditional dance show. Later, disembark the cruise at the harbour and get driven to your hotel.

Marina Dhow CruiseKite BeachDolphin Bay

Day 3: Dubai Sightseeing Tour | Dubai Desert Safari

Adventure

In the morning, get ready and board your transfer to discover the best of Dubai! Begin by relaxing under the sun at Jumeirah Beach and head to the iconic Atlantis Hotel for some stunning views. Then, dive into history at the Dubai Museum in Al Fahidi Fort, followed by some shopping at Dubai Mall and the Mall Of The Emirates. Later, experience the Desert Safari with dune bashing and a BBQ dinner with a Tanoura dance show. Finally, transfer back to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

City TourDubai MallDesert Safari

Day 4: Visit to Burj Khalifa

Highlights

In the morning, have a leisurely start to your day. You can explore various attractions, such as Ain Dubai and Dubai Garden Glow. Later in the afternoon, board your transfer from your hotel to the sky-high Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building. Upon reaching, ascend in the world's fastest double-deck elevators for panoramic views of Dubai and the Arabian Gulf from the 124th and 125th floors. After an amazing day, get transferred to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Burj KhalifaAin DubaiDubai Garden Glow

Day 5: Departure Day

Checkout

In the morning, check-out and get transferred to the Dubai International Airport to board your flight to Delhi for your onward journey back home. Upon reaching Delhi, marks the end of your Middle Eastern trip!
