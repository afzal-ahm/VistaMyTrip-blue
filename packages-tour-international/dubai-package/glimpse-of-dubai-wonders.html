✈️ Overview

Tracing Tales of Time

Dubai is a modern, vibrant city in the UAE, known for its futuristic skyline, luxury shopping, and global business hub. Famous landmarks include the Burj Khalifa, the world’s tallest building, and the Palm Jumeirah. The city blends modern architecture with cultural heritage, offering both traditional souks and high-end malls. Dubai thrives on tourism, real estate, finance, and trade, and is a global center for events, sports, and innovation. With its ambitious future plans and focus on sustainability, Dubai continues to grow as a key global city.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival and Dhow Cruise

Arrival

Welcome to this dazzling city and kickstart your magnificent Dubai holiday with a lavish cruise! Upon your arrival in Dubai, our representative will receive you at the airport and drop you at your hotel. Once you reach your hotel, complete your hassle-free registration formalities. Proceed to your room and have some rest. The first day of your Dubai holiday includes a lavish dinner over the traditional Dhow Cruise. Sail across the Dubai Creek and gorge on a mouth-watering buffet dinner. Check out Dubai’s architecture and glittering structures as you sail by, and also admire the beautiful Arabic music. End the first day of your Dubai tour with a comfortable overnight stay at your hotel.

ArrivalDubai CreekDhow Cruise

Day 2: Dubai City Tour & Burj Khalifa

Highlights

After a hearty breakfast, our tour manager will take you on a much-awaited city tour. Some of the city’s top attractions include The Dubai Mall, the Dubai Museum of the Future, Dubai Frame, the Dubai Miracle Garden, the Dubai Aquarium, Ski Dubai, the Lost Chambers, and the Jumeirah Mosque. Later in the day, you will visit the Burj Khalifa, the world’s tallest building. Don’t miss the view from the observation deck on the 124th floor. You will also visit the Dubai Fountain, the world’s largest performing fountain, for a light and music show.

City TourBurj KhalifaDubai Fountain

Day 3: Desert Safari

Adventure

Today, after breakfast, you will spend the rest of the morning relaxing and enjoying the amenities of the hotel. Later in the afternoon, head out for a desert safari. Your guide will take you on a thrilling 4x4 drive for dune bashing. You will also enjoy sandboarding, camel riding, and hot air balloon rides. In the evening, enjoy a BBQ dinner at the desert camp with the local Bedouins and spend the night stargazing. Or, you could return to the hotel.

Desert SafariDune BashingBBQ Dinner

Day 4: Depart Dubai

Checkout

On the day of departure, you will enjoy a hearty breakfast and check out of the hotel. Later, you will head to the Dubai International Airport to catch your return flight home.
