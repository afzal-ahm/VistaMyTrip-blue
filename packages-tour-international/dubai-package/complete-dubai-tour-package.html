✈️ Overview

The Ultimate Honeymoon Escape

Dubai is the ultimate destination for a honeymoon escape, where the mystique of Arabian nights intertwines with the brilliance of city lights. This vibrant city offers newlyweds an enchanting mix of romance, adventure, and luxury. Begin your journey with a stay in opulent accommodations, from sky-high suites with breathtaking views of the iconic Burj Khalifa to serene beachfront resorts.

A desert safari is a must for honeymooners seeking adventure and romance. Revel in the thrill of dune bashing, ride camels across the golden sands, and witness a mesmerizing desert sunset. Complete the experience with a starlit dinner in a Bedouin-style camp. As day turns to night, the city transforms into a glittering spectacle, perfect for candlelit dinners and unforgettable moments.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai | Day at Leisure

Arrival

Welcome to the City of Gold, Dubai! Upon your arrival at the Dubai International Airport, get transferred to your hotel. Check in and spend the rest of the day at leisure, where you can relax or explore the city. You can visit iconic spots like the Dubai Miracle Garden, which holds the Guinness World Record for the largest flower garden. You can also explore the Bollywood Parks, Dubai Garden Glow, & Dubai Fountain, among others. Later, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

ArrivalMiracle GardenDubai Fountain

Day 2: Dubai Sightseeing Tour | Visit to Burj Khalifa

Highlights

In the morning, you'll be driven to explore Dubai on a sightseeing tour. Begin with a visit to the Dubai Museum, offering a glimpse into the roots and evolution of Dubai's cultural identity. Next, visit the iconic Sheik Zayed Road, the Dubai Creek, and many more attractions. Later, visit the Burj Khalifa and ascend to the 124th and 125th floors for breathtaking panoramic views of Dubai's skyline. Afterwards, get transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Dubai MuseumDubai CreekBurj Khalifa

Day 3: Visit to Dubai Frame | Museum of Future Tour

Architecture

Today, get transferred to the Dubai Frame, where you'll witness the city's historic and modern landscapes. Also, walk on the glass-floored bridge & enjoy the amazing panoramic views of Dubai's skyline from its observatory deck. Then, get driven to the Museum Of The Future, to experience cutting-edge technology. Also, explore the various visionary exhibits that showcase innovations shaping our world. Later, get transferred back to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Dubai FrameMuseum of Future

Day 4: Dubai Desert Safari Experience

Adventure

Today, get driven for an exciting desert safari adventure. Feel the adrenaline rush during dune bashing, racing over the golden sand dunes, and try sandboarding, gliding down the sandy slopes. As evening falls, savor a mouthwatering BBQ dinner under the starry desert sky. The night comes alive with a captivating Tanoura dance performance, where skilled dancers showcase traditional Arabic moves. After this memorable experience, get driven back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Desert SafariDune BashingBBQ Dinner

Day 5: Luxury Yacht Cruise Experience

Romance

Today, board the transfer to Dubai Marina Harbour and embark on a private yacht cruise. Sail past the stunning skyline, with highlights including the illuminated Burj Khalifa and the shimmering Dubai Marina. Enjoy a romantic dinner onboard, savoring delicious cuisine as you take in the breathtaking views with your partner. After this unforgettable experience, get driven back to your hotel for a peaceful overnight stay.

Dubai MarinaYacht CruiseRomantic Dinner

Day 6: Visit Miracle Garden and Global Village

Culture & Leisure

Today, board the transfer to the Dubai Miracle Garden, a colorful paradise with over 50 million flowers displayed in elaborate arrangements and breathtaking designs. Next, head to Global Village, a lively cultural and entertainment destination featuring international pavilions, a variety of cuisines, exciting rides, and engaging live shows. After exploring these vibrant attractions, you'll be transferred back to your hotel.

Miracle GardenGlobal Village

Day 7: Leisure Day in Dubai

Free Day

Today, you can explore Abu Dhabi and its iconic attractions at your own pace. You can visit some attractions, like Al Jahili Fort, known for its impressive architecture and cultural significance. You can also explore restored rooms, artifacts, and interactive displays that showcase Emirati culture at the Qasr Al Hosn. You may also relax at some beautiful beaches, like Ghantoot Beach and Saadiyat Public Beach. Later, return to the hotel on your own for an overnight stay.

Abu Dhabi OptionAl Jahili FortGhantoot Beach

Day 8: Departure Day

Checkout

In the morning, check-out from your hotel and get transferred to the Dubai International Airport for your flight back home.
