✈️ Overview

Love Amidst the Dunes

Discover the perfect romantic escape in Dubai, where love flourishes amidst dazzling skylines and serene deserts. Indulge in luxurious beachfront resorts, intimate dining experiences by the Dubai Fountain, or sunset cruises along the enchanting Dubai Marina. Rekindle romance on a private desert safari, enjoying golden dunes, camel rides, and a magical dinner under a starlit sky. Stroll hand-in-hand through historic Al Fahidi, or marvel at the city’s modern wonders like the Burj Khalifa. With its seamless blend of elegance, adventure, and cultural charm, Dubai sets the stage for unforgettable moments and cherished memories with your loved one.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai | Dhow Cruise Experience

Arrival

Welcome to Dubai! Upon arrival at Dubai Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel. Check in and relax for a while. In the evening, head to Marina Harbour to board the Dhow Cruise. Enjoy a delightful dinner while cruising along Dubai's stunning waterways, taking in the city’s sparkling skyline. Savor refreshing beverages while enjoying a Tanoura dance show and Live Music. Later, get transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalDubai MarinaDhow Cruise

Day 2: Dubai Sightseeing Tour | Visit to Burj Khalifa

Highlights

In the morning, you'll be driven from your hotel to explore Dubai’s iconic sites. Begin at Jumeirah Beach and marvel at the luxurious Atlantis Hotel. Also, visit the Dubai Museum and get a chance to admire the beautiful Jumeirah Mosque. Head to the Mall of Emirates followed by Dubai Mall. From Dubai Mall, walk towards Burj Khalifa. As you ascend to its 124th floor, admire the views of Dubai's skyline and surrounding skyscrapers. Later, get driven back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Jumeirah BeachDubai MuseumBurj Khalifa

Day 3: Visit to Dubai Dolphinarium | Desert Safari Experience

Adventure

Begin your day by getting transferred from the hotel to the Dubai Dolphinarium. Here, you'll get a chance to witness an amazing dolphin and seal show. Afterward, get driven to embark on an exciting Desert Safari featuring dune bashing, sandboarding and camel ride. As the sun sets, savor a traditional BBQ dinner under the starlit sky while enjoying Tanoura dance, belly dance, and fire shows. Later, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

DolphinariumDesert SafariBBQ Dinner

Day 4: Visit to Dubai Frame & AYA Universe

Architecture

In the morning, board the transfer from the hotel to visit Dubai Frame. Upon arrival, enjoy panoramic views of Dubai's skyline from the observation deck. Stroll along the glass walkway where you can capture photos of the city's iconic landmarks. Next, head to AYA Universe, where you can explore twelve immersive experiences such as Flora, Outland, and The River, each showcasing Dubai’s rich heritage and futuristic vision. Later, get transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Dubai FrameAYA Universe

Day 5: Day Trip to IMG Worlds of Adventure

Theme Park

Begin your day by being transferred to IMG Worlds Of Adventure, Dubai's largest indoor theme park. Here, you'll visit four themed zones, including Marvel, Cartoon Network, Lost Valley, and IMG Boulevard. Enjoy thrilling rides and encounter iconic superheroes and beloved cartoon characters. Take a stroll through IMG Boulevard, perfect for dining and shopping. After a fun-filled day, get transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

IMG WorldsTheme ParkRides

Day 6: Departure Day

Checkout

Today, you'll check out from the hotel and get transferred to the Dubai Airport to board your flight back home. This marks the end of the trip.
