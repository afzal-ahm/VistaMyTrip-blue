Highlights of Azerbaijan with FREE Tickets to Fire Mountain

Experience the enchanting blend of history, culture, and natural wonders with the Highlights of Azerbaijan tour, now featuring complimentary tickets to the iconic Fire Mountain (Yanar Dag). Azerbaijan, the "Land of Fire," offers a captivating journey through its ancient traditions and modern marvels.

Begin your adventure in Baku, the vibrant capital, where futuristic skyscrapers stand harmoniously alongside historic landmarks like the Maiden Tower and Shirvanshah's Palace. Stroll along the Caspian Sea boulevard and marvel at the Flame Towers, a symbol of the nation's fiery spirit.

Explore the Gobustan National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its ancient rock carvings and mud volcanoes. Visit the charming town of Sheki, home to the stunning Sheki Khan's Palace and vibrant handicraft markets.

Azerbaijan's natural beauty shines in the lush Gabala region, offering breathtaking mountain landscapes, serene lakes, and adventure activities. End your trip with an unforgettable visit to Yanar Dag, the mystical Fire Mountain, where flames have danced on the hillside for millennia due to natural gas seeping through the earth—a true wonder of nature included for free with this package!

Enjoy the country’s warm hospitality, delicious cuisine, and vibrant culture throughout your journey. Perfect for history buffs, nature lovers, and culture enthusiasts, this tour is an exceptional way to experience Azerbaijan’s rich heritage and scenic splendor.

Highlights:

  • Complimentary access to Yanar Dag (Fire Mountain).
  • Explore Baku's historic and modern attractions.
  • Visit Gobustan’s petroglyphs and mud volcanoes.
  • Discover the beauty of Sheki and Gabala.
  • Enjoy Azerbaijani cuisine and local markets.

Embark on this incredible journey to Azerbaijan and create memories of a lifetime!

Trip Hightlights

  • Get to know about the ancient and famous fire-worshipping ritualsof Ateshgah at the Zoroastrian temple, known as a powerful symbol of devotion.

  • Enjoy a gondola ride on your visit to Shahdag Mountain Ski Resort and take-in the panoramic views of the stunning Caucasus Mountains.

  • Visit the first mosque of Azerbaijan, Juma Mosque, and admire its beautiful architectural design reflecting the Islamic heritage of Baku.

  • Explore Baku's Old City, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where the walls show the beautiful Oriental architecture and signify centuries-old tales.

Itinerary

Day 0

Arrival in Baku | Baku Sightseeing Tour

Salam! Welcome to Baku! Upon arrival, get transferred to the hotel and check-in. Afterward, in the evening, board your transfer from the hotel for the Baku panoramic tour. During this evening sightseeing tour, wander around the beautiful Nizami Street, where you can explore various cafes and shops to try local cuisine and buy souviniers. After that, take-in the views of Baku's skyline from Highland Park's vantage point. Later, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 1

Baku Old City Sightseeing Tour

Engage yourself in Baku's history on a city tour. Board the transfer from the hotel and see places like the Maiden Tower and the Palace of Shrivanshahs. Learn about the Azerbaijani culture as you explore its museums, like the National Carpet Museum and the National History Museum. Also, visit Little Venice and take a leisurely walk along the beautiful Boulevard. As the day draws to a close, enjoy a sea voyage in the Caspian Sea and then get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2

Transfer to Shahdag | Day Trip to Shahdag Mountain Ski Resort

Set off on a memorable adventure in Shahdag. Check-out and get transferred from the hotel to Shahdag Ski Resort. Upon reaching the resort, you can enjoy fun-filled activities like skiing and a cable car ride in the beautiful landscapes of the Shahdag Mountains. Also, spend some peaceful time in the serene environment of the place as you see some of the stunning streams. Later, get transferred to the hotel and check-in for an overnight stay.
