Trip Hightlights

✅Savitri Mata Temple: A serene hilltop temple offering panoramic views of Pushkar and the desert. Perfect for a peaceful visit.

✅Brahma Lake: A sacred pilgrimage spot with a tranquil atmosphere, surrounded by temples.

✅Sarafa Bazaar: A lively market in Ajmer with street food and handicrafts, perfect for cultural exploration.

Itinerary

Day 0

Departure From Delhi

  • We depart from Delhi around 8:00 PM in an AC vehicle.
  • We make a pit stop for dinner at a decent roadside restaurant.
  • Overnight journey to Pushkar.

Day 1

Indias Craziest Holi Celebration

  • Check in to the hotel, freshen up, and have breakfast.
  • After breakfast, enjoy a party at the venue with a DJ, rain dance, snacks, and thandai.
  • If you wish to join the famous Holi celebration at Brahma Ghat, you can visit at your own convenience, covering the expenses yourself.
  • Relax at the hotel in the evening.
  • Later, get ready for a DJ night with dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the resort, followed by dinner.

Day 2

Pushkar Sightseeing Ajmer Sharif Dargah | Departure

  • Wake up and have breakfast at the resort.
  • Post breakfast, we will visit Pushkar Lake for sightseeing, including Brahma's Temple, Sunset Point near Pushkar Lake, and Sarafa Bazaar.
  • Later, we will visit Ajmer Sharif Dargah. (The conveyance from parking to Ajmer Sharif Dargah will be self-paid for both sides.)
  • After completing the sightseeing, we depart for Delhi in the evening with lots of memories.

Day 3

Reach Delhi

  • Reach delhi early morning with unforgetteble memories.
