❓ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?
We guarantee a response within 2 business hours on weekdays and 4 hours on weekends. For urgent queries, please call or WhatsApp us directly.
Can I customise a package to my budget?
Absolutely. Every package we offer is fully customisable. Share your budget and preferences and we'll craft the best itinerary within your range.
Do you provide visa assistance for international trips?
Yes, we provide end-to-end visa guidance and documentation support for most international destinations including Bali, Dubai, Thailand, Singapore, Europe and more.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
Our cancellation policy varies by package type and lead time. Please refer to our Refund Policy page, or contact us directly for details on your specific booking.
Do you arrange travel insurance?
Yes, we can assist you with travel insurance options through our partner providers. We strongly recommend insurance for all international travel.
Can I book for a group or corporate event?
Yes! We specialize in group travel and corporate events. We handle groups from 10 to 1,000+ with dedicated coordinators and special group pricing.
