🏞️ Overview

KTM 390 Bike Adventure Overview

Embark on the ultimate bike adventure through the rugged terrain of Leh-Ladakh, specially designed for thrill-seekers and motorcycle enthusiasts. Riding the powerful KTM 390, this 6-day, 5-night journey offers a perfect blend of adrenaline, scenic beauty, and cultural exploration. Your adventure begins in Leh, where you’ll acclimatize and prepare for the exciting journey ahead. The KTM 390 is your trusted companion, ready to conquer the winding mountain passes, rocky trails, and serene valleys of Ladakh including Khardung La and Chang La.

Route Details

Leh - Sham Valley - Nubra - Pangong - Leh

Start & End Point

Leh Airport

Passes Covered

Khardung-la (18,380 ft) & Chang-la (17,688 ft)

Package Duration

6 Days & 5 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation: 3 nights in Leh, 1 night in Nubra, 1 night in Pangong.
  • Daily Breakfast and Dinner as per itinerary.
  • Airport transfers from Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport.
  • An experienced Tour Leader & mechanic for daily maintenance.
  • Backup vehicle (SUV/Traveler/Bolero Camper) & Oxygen Cylinder.
  • Inner Line Permits and Ladakh Union Fees.
  • Leh Sightseeing & KTM 390 rides across the valleys.

Trip Highlights

  • Ride through one of the world's highest motorable roads, the Khardungla Pass.
  • Enjoy a thrilling ride to the magical Pangong Tso Lake and see its changing colors.
  • Experience the magnetic force of the Magnetic Hill.
  • Visit the famous Nubra Valley, and enjoy an adventure ATV ride.
  • Stand at Sangam Point where the Indus and Zanskar rivers merge.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Leh | Leh Sightseeing Tour

Leh

After your arrival at Leh Airport, board the transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Check-in and relax for a while before embarking on a Leh sightseeing tour through the rich Ladakhi history. Experience the royal charm of Leh Palace's architecture, drive up to the scenic Shanti Stupa, and wander through the bustling Leh Market. As the day ends, you'll ride back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalAcclimatizationLeh Palace

Day 2: Bike Trip to Sham Valley | The Mysterious Paths of Ladakh

Sham Valley

After breakfast, begin your bike ride for an exciting trip to Sham Valley from Leh. Your journey kicks off at the Sangam point, where the Indus and Zanskar rivers merge. Then, onto Gurudwara Pathar Sahib and the famous Magnetic Hill. Finally, you'll visit SECMOL, the school featured in "3 Idiots". After a day filled with adventure, ride back to your hotel in Leh.

Magnetic HillIndus Sangam

Day 3: Ride from Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass

Nubra Valley

Post-checkout, hop on your bike and ride to the cold deserts of Nubra Valley through the breathtaking Khardung La Pass. Stop to enjoy steaming Maggi with stunning views. Next, visit the famous Diskit Monastery. Take on an ATV adventure ride or camel safari in the Hunder Sand Dunes before arriving at the campsite in Nubra for an overnight stay.

Khardung-la PassDiskit Monastery

Day 4: Ride from Nubra Valley to Pangong Tso | Camping at the Lake

Pangong Tso

Check out and embark on an adventurous bike ride towards Pangong Lake. Enjoy riding along the spectacular views of the famous Ladakhi peaks. As you continue, you will come across several streams, making this bike ride thrilling. Once you arrive at Pangong Lake, settle into your camp and enjoy the ever-changing hues of this saltwater lake.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 5: Ride from Pangong Tso to Leh | Crossing Chang La Pass

Leh

After checking out from your camp at Pangong Tso, ride back to Leh, crossing the stunning Chang La Pass. En route, take a moment to pay your respects at the Chang La Baba temple. Stop by the Druk Padma Karpo School ('3 Idiots' school). Next, pause to explore the rich cultural heritage at Thiksey Monastery before unwinding at your Leh hotel.

Chang-la PassThiksey Monastery

Day 6: Departure

Checkout

Post check-out, with one last look at the Ladakhi peaks and winding paths, you will get transferred to Leh Airport for your flight back home. This marks the end of your spectacular KTM 390 adventure trip.

Leh AirportDeparture
