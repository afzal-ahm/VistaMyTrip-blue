🏞️ Overview

Incredible Ladakh with Hunder Sand Dunes Overview

Discover the enchanting beauty of Ladakh on this 8-day, 7-night journey that takes you through stunning landscapes, ancient monasteries, and the magical Hunder Sand Dunes. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, this trip offers a blend of cultural immersion and breathtaking scenery. Your adventure begins in Leh, the heart of Ladakh, where you’ll acclimatize and explore iconic landmarks. As you set off on your journey, traverse the world-famous Khardung La, one of the highest motorable roads in the world, offering panoramic views of the snow-clad Himalayas.

Route Details

Leh - Sham Valley - Nubra - Pangong - Leh - Kargil - Srinagar

Start & End Point

Leh to Srinagar

Passes Covered

Khardung-la (18,000 ft) & Chang-la (17,688 ft)

Package Duration

8 Days & 7 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in Leh, Srinagar, Pangong, Kargil, and Nubra.
  • Daily Breakfast and Dinner as per the itinerary.
  • Airport transfers from Leh and to Srinagar airport.
  • Sightseeing tours and transfers in a comfortable vehicle.
  • Inner Line Permits and Ladakh Union Fees.
  • Oxygen cylinder and all applicable toll taxes.

Trip Highlights

  • Explore Pangong Lake, the highest saltwater lake globally.
  • Conquer Khardung La, one of the world's highest motorable passes.
  • Discover the ancient beauty of Lamayuru Village and its monastery.
  • Ride a double-humped camel on the Hunder Sand Dunes in Nubra Valley.
  • Pay homage at the Kargil War Memorial.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Leh | Leh Sightseeing Tour

Leh

After your arrival at Leh Airport, you will get transferred to your pre-booked hotel. Later, leave for your Leh sightseeing tour, exploring Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, and the bustling Leh Market. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalLeh PalaceShanti Stupa

Day 2: Sham Valley Day Tour | The Mysterious Paths of Ladakh

Sham Valley

In the morning, you will be driven to Sham Valley. Visit the Sangam point, Gurdwara Pathar Sahib, the famous Magnetic Hill, and SECMOL school. After a day of adventure, get transferred back to your hotel in Leh for an overnight stay.

Magnetic HillIndus Sangam

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass

Nubra Valley

Board your transfer to Nubra Valley through Khardung La Pass. Visit the famous Diskit Monastery and admire the grand statue of Maitreya Buddha. Embark on an ATV adventure ride in the Hunder Sand Dunes before checking into your campsite.

Khardung-la PassHunder Dunes

Day 4: Nubra Valley to Pangong Tso | Camping at the Lake

Pangong Tso

Check-out and begin your journey towards Pangong Lake. Enjoy spectacular views of Himalayan peaks. Once you arrive, settle into your camp and head out for a stroll along the lake, enjoying its ever-changing hues.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 5: Pangong Tso to Leh via Chang La Pass

Leh

Head back to Leh, crossing the stunning Chang La Pass. En route, pay respects at the Chang La Baba temple and stop by the Druk Padma Karpo School (from '3 Idiots'). Visit Thiksey Monastery before settling into your hotel in Leh.

Chang-la PassThiksey Monastery

Day 6: Transfer from Leh to Kargil | Enroute Sightseeing

Kargil

In the morning, check-out and begin your journey towards Kargil. En route, you will pass the famous Alchi and Lamayuru Monasteries. After reaching Kargil, check-in at your hotel and spend the day at leisure.

LamayuruAlchi

Day 7: Transfer from Kargil to Srinagar

Srinagar

Check-out from your hotel in Kargil and get transferred to Srinagar. Along the way, enjoy breathtaking views and visit the Kargil War Memorial. Upon arrival in Srinagar, check-in at your hotel for an overnight stay.

Kargil War MemorialSrinagar Arrival

Day 8: Departure Day

Checkout

After checking out from your hotel, you'll get transferred to Srinagar Airport. Enjoy one last look at the picturesque surroundings of Kashmir before boarding your flight back home, marking the end of your trip.

Srinagar AirportDeparture
