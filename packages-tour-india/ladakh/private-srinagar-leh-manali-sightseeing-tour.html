🏞️ Overview

Srinagar Leh Manali Sightseeing Overview

Explore the beautiful Lakes of Srinagar, thrilling routes of Ladakh, and beautiful Valleys of Himachal, all in a single tour. This circuit features some of the world’s highest motorable passes with the spectacular beauty of snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and gushing rivers covering breathtaking destinations like Solang valley, Gata Loops, Zojila pass, Kargil, Magnetic hill, Confluence of Indus, And Zanskar river, Dal lake and much more waiting on the way! Admired for its unparalleled scenic views, Ladakh offers beautiful views of valleys & mountains and a starlit sky at the night.

Route Details

Srinagar - Sonmarg - Zojila Pass - Kargil - Lamayuru - Leh - Nubra via Khardung La Pass - Pangong Lake - Leh - Sarchu - Jispa - Manali - Delhi

Start & End Point

Srinagar to Delhi

Passes Covered

Khardung-la (18,000 ft), Chang-la (17,688 ft), Rohtang Pass, Lachung La & Baralacha la

Package Duration

11 Days & 10 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • Airport pick up as per your flight timings.
  • AC Volvo Semi-sleeper bus tickets from Manali to Delhi.
  • Comfortable and hygienic vehicle (Innova/ Xylo/ Tavera/ Scorpio/ Similar) for sightseeing.
  • Stay in hygienic and sanitized standard hotels as per requirements.
  • Breakfast and Dinner as per itinerary.
  • Assured Inner-Line Permit for all restricted areas.
  • Oxygen cylinder and basic first-aid during the tour.

Things Not to Miss

  • Take a soothing Shikara Ride in the famous Dal Lake.
  • Enjoy a bird's eye view with paragliding at Solang valley.
  • River rafting at the world’s highest river-rafting point, Sangam.
  • Ride the remarkable double-humped (Bactrian) camels in Nubra valley.
  • Feast on delicious Maggi at the Khardungla-pass.
  • Pay tribute to brave soldiers at the Kargil War Memorial.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar | Welcome to the Heaven on Earth

Srinagar

Your amazing trip to Ladakh begins, as soon as you arrive in Srinagar. Get transferred to a pre-booked hotel and complete the check-in formalities. The rest of the day is at leisure. In the evening, return back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

ArrivalLeisure

Day 2: Srinagar to Kargil | Coldest Inhabited Place in the World

Kargil

Wake up to a beautiful morning in the Kashmir Valley. Later, begin your transfer to Kargil by passing through the beautiful Zozi-La Pass. View Tiger Hill and take a small stopover at the Kargil War Memorial to pay homage to the martyrs. Reach Kargil by evening.

Zozi-La PassTiger Hill

Day 3: Kargil to Leh | Visit the Legendary Sham Valley

Leh

Start your journey towards Leh. Visit Basgo Palace and Lamayuru Village, experience the Magnetic Hill, and watch the confluence of Zanskar and Indus Rivers. Learn about Indo-Pak Warriors at the world-famous Hall of Fame before reaching Leh.

Magnetic HillIndus Sangam

Day 4: Leh Local Sightseeing | Explore The Heart of Ladakh

Leh

Start with a visit to the popular Shanti Stupa offering amazing sunset and panoramic views of the city. Visit the Leh Palace built in the 17th century, and explore the local Leh Market to buy Tibetan handicrafts or try delicious Ladakhi cuisine.

Shanti StupaLeh Palace

Day 5: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La Pass

Nubra Valley

This journey takes you through Khardung-La Pass, the world's highest motorable road. Descend into the white sand desert of Nubra Valley. Visit Diskit Monastery and enjoy the exquisite double-humped Bactrian Camel Ride before camping under the stars.

Khardung-la PassCamel Ride

Day 6: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

Pangong Tso

Get ready to visit Pangong Lake on the Indo-China border. This high altitude saline water lake will greet you with its changing colors from shades of blue to green to red. Recreate famous Bollywood moments and spend the night camping near the lake.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 7: Pangong Lake to Leh | Excursion to Rancho School

Leh

Mark your way back to Leh via Chang-la Pass. Visit the Changla Baba Temple, the Druk Padma School (famed for the movie ‘3 Idiots’), and the beautifully structured Thiksey Monastery before arriving in Leh for an overnight stay.

Chang-la PassRancho School

Day 8: Leh to Sarchu | Witness the Beauty of Barren Lands

Sarchu

Start your journey back towards Sarchu. Witness the beauty of barren lands at More Plains, travel through Lachung La Pass, and enjoy the ride through Gata Loops (21 hairpin bends). Enjoy a bonfire dinner under a starlit sky at Sarchu.

More PlainsGata Loops

Day 9: Sarchu to Manali | Feel being transported to Another World

Manali

Start your return journey to Manali. Pass through Baralacha La Pass, stop by Suraj Tal and Deepak Tal, and explore Sissu Lake and Waterfall in Keylong. Travel through the famous Atal Tunnel before checking into your hotel in Manali.

Baralacha LaAtal Tunnel

Day 10: Day at Leisure and Departure from Manali

Manali

Begin your day early, complete check-out, and spend the day exploring Manali at your own pace. In the evening, board the Volvo AC semi-sleeper bus to Delhi for your onward journey.

LeisureOvernight Bus

Day 11: Reach Delhi | End of Trip

Delhi

After passing through the beautiful scenery, your bus will reach Delhi in the morning. Once you arrive in Delhi, your amazing trip to Ladakh comes to an end with a bag full of lifetime memories.

Arrival DelhiTour Ends
