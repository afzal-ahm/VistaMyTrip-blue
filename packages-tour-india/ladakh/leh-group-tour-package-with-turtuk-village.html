🏞️ Overview

Leh Ladakh Group Adventure Overview

Situated right on the border on the northwest edge of Ladakh is Turtuk which was once a part of Pakistan till the 1971 Indo-Pak war. With this 7 days tour to Ladakh, enjoy a journey to the top of the world featuring most of the world’s highest motorable passes. Admired for its unparalleled scenic views, pristine icy lakes, and swirling roads, Ladakh is becoming the most Instagrammable place in India and is on everyone’s bucket list.

Route Details

Leh - Sham Valley - Khardungla - Nubra - Turtuk - Pangong - Leh

Start & End Point

Leh Airport

Passes Covered

Khardung-la (18,000 ft) & Chang-la (17,688 ft)

Package Duration

7 Days & 6 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation at all locations on double/triple/quad sharing in standard hotels/camps.
  • Comfortable Vehicle (Innova/Xylo/Scorpio) for sightseeing & Airport transfers.
  • Buffet Breakfast and Dinner as per itinerary.
  • Assured Inner line permits, Ladakh Union fee, and toll/fuel charges.
  • Oxygen cylinder and Basic First-Aid during the tour.

Things Not to Miss

  • Enjoy Thukpa at The Tibetan Kitchen and coffee at Lala's Art Café.
  • River rafting at the world’s highest river-rafting point, Sangam.
  • Ride the remarkable double-humped (Bactrian) camels found exclusively at Nubra valley.
  • Feast on delicious Maggi at the Khardungla-pass.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Leh | Welcome to the Top of the World

Leh

After landing in Leh, get welcomed by a representative who will help in smooth transfer to the hotel. Spend a relaxing day getting acclimatized to the high-altitude climate. Overnight stay at the hotel in Leh.

ArrivalAcclimatization

Day 2: Leh to Sham Valley | Exploring Borders of Leh

Sham Valley

Get ready for an adventurous drive to Sham Valley. Pay homage to martyrs at the Hall of Fame, visit Spituk Gompa, and enjoy langar at Gurudwara Pathar Sahib. Experience the gravity-defying Magnetic Hill and watch the confluence of Zanskar and Indus Rivers.

Magnetic HillIndus Sangam

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La Pass

Nubra Valley

This journey takes you through Khardung-La Pass, the world's highest all-season motorable road. Descend into Nubra Valley, visit Diskit Monastery, and enjoy the exquisite double-humped Bactrian Camel Ride amidst the sand dunes. Camp under the stars.

Khardung-la PassCamel Ride

Day 4: Nubra Valley to Turtuk Village

Turtuk

Drive towards Turtuk, the last outpost in India before the Pakistan-controlled Gilgit-Baltistan begins. Experience a unique culture, dramatic narrow valleys, steep mountains, and green pastures yielding fresh apricots and apples. Return to Nubra for an overnight stay.

Indo-Pak BorderVillage Tour

Day 5: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

Pangong Tso

Head to Pangong Lake on the Indo-China border. Watch one of the highest altitude saline water lakes change its colors from shades of blue to green to red. Recreate moments from famous Bollywood movies and camp near the lake.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 6: Pangong Lake to Leh | Excursion to Rancho School

Leh

Mark your way back to Leh with a stop at Chang-la Pass and the Changla Baba Temple. Visit the famous Druk Padma School (famed for the movie ‘3 Idiots’) and the beautifully structured Thiksey Monastery before returning to Leh.

Chang-la PassRancho School

Day 7: Departure | Time to say Goodbye

Checkout

After having your breakfast in the morning, complete the check out formalities. You will be transferred to the Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport for your onward journey. Fly out of Leh with memories of a magical land.

Leh AirportDeparture
