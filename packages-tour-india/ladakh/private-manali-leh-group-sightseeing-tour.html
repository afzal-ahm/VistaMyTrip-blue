🏞️ Overview

Manali Leh Manali Adventure Overview

Get ready for the most adventurous drive-through of your life. This tour lets you explore both the beauty of Manali and the thrills of Ladakh, and going through the cultural side of Leh makes the tour even more interesting. The unlimited fun activities in Manali, driving through the hairpin bends of the route, and exploring the heart of Ladakh; are the experiences that make this tour full of crazy and unique experiences.

Route Details

Delhi - Manali - Jispa - Sarchu - Leh - Nubra - Pangong - Leh

Start & End Point

Delhi / Leh

Passes Covered

Khardung-la, Chang-la, Rohtang, Lachung La, Baralacha la

Package Duration

10 Days & 9 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • AC Volvo Semi-sleeper bus tickets from Delhi to Manali.
  • Airport drop as per your flight timings.
  • Comfortable and hygienic vehicle (Innova/Xylo/Similar) for sightseeing.
  • Stay in hygienic and sanitized standard hotels/camps.
  • Breakfast and Dinner as per itinerary.
  • Assured Inner-Line Permit for all restricted areas.
  • Oxygen cylinder and basic first-aid during the tour.

Things Not to Miss

  • Enjoy paragliding at Solang valley for a bird's eye view.
  • Enjoy famous pizzas and burgers at cafe 1947, Manali.
  • Enjoy Thukpa, traditional Ladakhi cuisine at The Tibetan Kitchen.
  • Ride the remarkable double-humped (Bactrian) camels in Nubra valley.
  • Feast on delicious Maggi at the Khardungla-pass.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Delhi to Manali | Overnight Journey

Overnight Bus

Your journey begins as you board the overnight Volvo bus from Delhi to Manali. The scenic route takes you through the majestic Himalayas.

DepartureOvernight Travel

Day 2: Arrival in Manali | Land of Scenic Beauty

Manali

Reach Manali and check into your pre-booked hotel. The rest of the day is at leisure for you to explore the charming town at your own pace.

ArrivalLeisure

Day 3: Manali to Jispa | Drive Through Atal Tunnel

Jispa

After breakfast, start your journey towards Jispa, passing through the famous Atal Tunnel. Explore Sissu Lake and Sissu Waterfall before reaching the lush green Bhaga valley and checking into your camp in Jispa.

Atal TunnelSissu Lake

Day 4: Jispa to Sarchu via Baralacha-la | A Night Under Starlit Sky

Sarchu

Travel to Sarchu, visiting Deepak Tal and Suraj Tal en route. Cross the picturesque Baralacha La Pass (16,000 ft) and enjoy the stunning vistas. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay at the camps in Sarchu.

Baralacha LaCamping

Day 5: Sarchu to Leh | Drive Through Gata Loops

Leh

Start your journey to Leh, driving through the Gata Loops (21 hairpin bends) and crossing Nakee La and Lachung La passes. Stop at the More Plains before reaching Leh in the evening for an overnight stay.

Gata LoopsMore Plains

Day 6: Leh Local Sightseeing | Soak in the Ladakhi Culture

Leh

After acclimatizing, visit the Shanti Stupa for panoramic city views and explore the historic Leh Palace. In the evening, wander through the local Leh Market for souvenirs and local cuisine.

Shanti StupaLeh Palace

Day 7: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La Pass

Nubra Valley

Drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La, the world's highest motorable road. Experience the white sand dunes and enjoy a Bactrian Camel Ride. Visit Diskit Monastery before camping under the stars.

Khardung-la PassCamel Ride

Day 8: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

Pangong Tso

Head to the famous Pangong Lake on the Indo-China border. Witness the lake's changing colors and recreate moments from your favorite Bollywood movies. Camp overnight near the lake.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 9: Pangong Lake to Leh | Excursion to Rancho School

Leh

Return to Leh via Chang-la Pass. Visit the Changla Baba Temple, the Druk Padma School (from '3 Idiots'), and the beautiful Thiksey Monastery before reaching Leh for an overnight stay.

Chang-la PassRancho School

Day 10: Departure from Leh

Checkout

After breakfast, check out from the hotel. You will be transferred to the airport for your onward journey, carrying memories of a magical land.

Leh AirportDeparture
