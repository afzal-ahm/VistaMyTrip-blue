Thrilling Leh Ladakh Overview

Get ready to make your dream of going on a road trip in Ladakh come true where one would be experiencing the fusion of thrill, serene beauty and culture of The "Land of High Passes", as the entire tour is exclusively elected by Thrillophilia's outdoor experts. A wonderful drive through Leh-Ladakh, a land covered with snow-capped mountains and the highest motorable passes, makes you feel at the top of the world. The unique thing about the Ladakh terrain is that it has mountains, forests, deserts, lakes, rivers, waterfalls in one beautiful and dynamic geography.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Flight from Delhi to Leh | Acclimatization & Sham Valley Tour

Leh

Board your included flight from Delhi and arrive at Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport. You will be transferred to your hotel in Leh to acclimatize. Later in the day, enjoy a short excursion to Sham Valley, visiting Magnetic Hill, the Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, and the mesmerizing confluence of the Indus and Zanskar rivers (Sangam). Return to the hotel for dinner.

Arrival FlightMagnetic HillIndus Sangam

Day 2: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La Pass

Nubra Valley

After breakfast, begin your thrilling drive to the famous Nubra Valley via the Khardung-La Pass, standing tall at 18,000 ft. Enjoy the snow-capped panoramic views and grab a bowl of Maggi at the pass. Upon reaching Nubra, explore the white sand dunes and take an exciting ride on the double-humped Bactrian Camels. Overnight stay at the camp.

Khardung-la PassCamel Ride

Day 3: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake | Recreate Bollywood Moments

Pangong Tso

Wake up in the beautiful Nubra Valley and get ready for a scenic drive towards Pangong Lake along the Shyok River. Upon arrival at the Indo-China border, witness the spectacular high-altitude saline lake, famous for changing its color in shades of blue, green, and red. Enjoy camping under the starlit sky near the lake.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 4: Pangong Lake to Leh via Chang-La Pass | Excursion to Rancho School

Leh

Wake up to a beautiful morning by the lake. After breakfast, start your return journey to Leh. On the way, cross the mighty Chang-La Pass (17,688 ft). Stop by SECMOL, the real '3 Idiots' school founded by Dr. Sonam Wangchuk, and the majestic Thiksey Monastery. Enjoy your final evening shopping for souvenirs at the Leh Market.

Chang-la PassRancho School

Day 5: Departure from Leh to Delhi | End of Trip

Checkout

After an early breakfast, check out from your hotel. You will be transferred back to the Leh Airport to board your return flight to Delhi. Leave with a heart full of memories, taking a piece of the magical 'Land of High Passes' with you.
