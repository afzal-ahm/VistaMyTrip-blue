🏞️ Overview

Leh Ladakh with Manali Overview

Starting from Leh, this tour features the most amazing journey, which covers both the beautiful valleys of Manali and the adventurous routes of Ladakh. Explore the adventurous side of your personality while enjoying this thrilling road trip to Ladakh from Manali via various exciting destinations and twisty roads. Cover breathtaking destinations during your road trip like Solang valley, Gata Loops, Manali, Pang, Magnetic hill, Confluence of Indus and Zanskar river and so much more!

Route Details

Leh - Sham valley - Khardung La - Nubra - Pangong - Leh - Jispa - Manali - Delhi

Start & End Point

Leh to Manali

Passes Covered

Khardung-la, Chang-la, Rohtang Pass, Lachung La and Baralacha la

Package Duration

9 Days & 8 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • Comfortable Vehicle (Innova/ Xylo/ Tavera/ Scorpio/ Similar) for sightseeing.
  • Airport pick up as per your flight timings.
  • AC Volvo Semi-sleeper bus tickets from Manali to Delhi.
  • Accommodation at all locations on quad sharing basis in standard hotels/camps.
  • Buffet Breakfast and Dinner as per itinerary.
  • Assured Inner line permits and Ladakh Union fee.
  • Oxygen cylinder, basic First-Aid, and evening Bonfire (in Nubra).

Things Not to Miss

  • Enjoy a bird's eye view with paragliding at Solang valley.
  • Enjoy famous pizzas and burgers at cafe 1947, Manali.
  • Enjoy Thukpa, traditional Ladakhi cuisine at The Tibetan Kitchen.
  • River rafting at the world’s highest river-rafting point, Sangam.
  • Ride the remarkable double-humped (Bactrian) camels in Nubra valley.
  • Feast on delicious Maggi at the Khardungla-pass.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Leh | Welcome to the Top of the World

Leh

Upon your arrival, the driver will transfer you from Leh Airport to your pre-booked hotel. Once you check-in, the remaining day is yours to enjoy, rejuvenate, and adjust to the weather conditions and altitude of the region.

ArrivalAcclimatization

Day 2: Sham Valley Excursion | Visit the Famous 3-Idiots School

Sham Valley

Take an excursion to Sham Valley, covering all major attractions such as the Hall of Fame, Spituk Gompa, Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Magnetic Hill, Indus Sangam Viewpoint, and SECMOL (the real 3-idiots school).

Magnetic HillIndus Sangam

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La Pass

Nubra Valley

Check out and drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La Pass, the world's highest all-season motorable road. Visit the oldest Diskit Monastery and enjoy a double-humped Bactrian Camel Ride before heading to your campsite.

Khardung-la PassCamel Ride

Day 4: Nubra valley to Pangong Lake | Recreate Bollywood Moments

Pangong Tso

Check out from Nubra and transfer to Pangong Lake. Explore the picturesque surroundings, watch the changing colors of the lake, and recreate iconic Bollywood movie moments near the Indo-China border.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 5: Pangong Lake to Leh | Excursion to Rancho School

Leh

Mark your way back to Leh via Chang-la Pass and visit the Changla Baba Temple. Stop by the Druk Padma School (from the movie ‘3 Idiots’), and the Thiksey Monastery before your overnight stay in Leh.

Chang-la PassRancho School

Day 6: Leh to Sarchu | Witness the Beauty of Barren Lands

Sarchu

Travel from Leh to Sarchu. Enjoy a stopover at the beautiful More Plains and Lachung La Pass. Continue your journey through Gata Loops (a series of 21 hairpin bends) before arriving at your Sarchu campsite.

More PlainsGata Loops

Day 7: Sarchu to Manali | Transported to Another World

Manali

Transfer from Sarchu to Manali. En route, stop at Baralacha La Pass, Suraj Tal, Deepak Tal, Sissu Lake, and Sissu Waterfall. Travel through the famous Atal Tunnel before checking into your hotel in Manali.

Baralacha LaAtal Tunnel

Day 8: Day at Leisure and Departure from Manali

Overnight Bus

Your 8-day adventure comes to an end. The rest of the day is at leisure to explore Manali. In the evening, head to the Manali bus stand to board your pre-booked AC Volvo bus for an overnight journey to Delhi.

LeisureDeparture

Day 9: Arrival in Delhi | End of Trip

Delhi

Arrive in Delhi in the morning. Carry back a bag full of lifetime memories from your spectacular Ladakh and Manali road trip.

Arrival DelhiTour Ends
