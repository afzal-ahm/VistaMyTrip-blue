🏞️ Overview

Thrilling Leh Ladakh Bike Adventure Overview

Unleash your adventurous spirit with a spectacular 7-day, 6-night bike expedition through the majestic landscapes of Leh-Ladakh. This journey is a dream come true for motorcycle enthusiasts, offering a perfect mix of challenging terrains, breathtaking views, and cultural exploration. Your adventure kicks off in Leh, where you'll acclimatize and gear up for the thrilling ride ahead. Traverse the world’s highest motorable passes, including Khardung La and Chang La, and experience the thrill of navigating through rugged mountain roads, pristine valleys, and shimmering rivers.

Route Details

Leh - Sham Valley - Nubra - Pangong - Stok - Leh

Start & End Point

Leh Airport

Passes Covered

Khardung-la (18,000 ft) & Chang-la (17,688 ft)

Package Duration

7 Days & 6 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation for 6 nights (4 in Leh, 1 in Pangong, 1 in Nubra).
  • Daily Breakfast and Dinner as per the itinerary.
  • Airport transfers in Leh.
  • An experienced Tour Leader and a mechanic.
  • Backup vehicle (SUV/Traveler) for support.
  • Inner Line Permits and Ladakh Union Fees.
  • Oxygen cylinder and basic first-aid kit.

Trip Highlights

  • Embark on a thrilling journey through Ladakh's wild terrain and towering peaks.
  • Witness the mesmerizing convergence of the Zanskar and Indus rivers at Sangam Point.
  • Conquer Khardung-La Pass, one of the world's highest motorable roads.
  • See the Himalayan peaks reflected in the ever-changing hues of Pangong Tso.
  • Explore ancient monasteries and the unique culture of the region.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Leh | Leh Sightseeing Tour

Leh

After reaching Leh Airport, you will be driven to your hotel. Check-in, and once settled, set out on a sightseeing tour of Leh, exploring Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, and the bustling Leh Market. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalAcclimatizationLeh Palace

Day 2: Ride to Sham Valley | Exploring Mysteries & Wonders

Sham Valley

After breakfast, hop on your bike for an exciting trip to Sham Valley. Your day includes stops at the Sangam point, Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, and the famous Magnetic Hill. You can also opt for thrilling White Water rafting before riding back to Leh.

Magnetic HillIndus Sangam

Day 3: Ride from Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass

Nubra Valley

Start your ride to Nubra Valley, passing through the breathtaking Khardung La Pass. Enjoy hot maggi at the renowned Khardung-la cafe. Visit Diskit Monastery and enjoy an ATV or Bactrian camel ride before checking into your camp.

Khardung-la PassCamel Ride

Day 4: Ride from Nubra Valley to Pangong Tso

Pangong Tso

Check-out and begin your bike ride towards the breathtaking Pangong Lake. The road offers spectacular views and adventurous stream crossings. Once you arrive, settle into your camp and stroll along the lake, enjoying its ever-changing colors.

Saltwater LakeCamping

Day 5: Ride from Pangong Tso to Leh via Chang La Pass

Leh

Ride back towards Leh, passing through the majestic Chang La Pass. Along the way, visit the Chang La Baba temple, the Druk Padma Karpo School (from '3 Idiots'), and the beautiful Thiksey Monastery before reaching your hotel in Leh.

Chang-la PassThiksey Monastery

Day 6: Bike Adventure to Stok Village | History & Heritage

Stok Village

After breakfast, hop on your bike for a full-day tour of Stok Village. Enjoy beautiful views as you ride through rugged terrain. Explore the historic Stok Palace and the breathtaking Stok Kangri region before returning to Leh.

Stok PalaceSindhu Ghat

Day 7: Departure from Leh

Checkout

Take one last glance at the beauty of Ladakh as you are transferred to the Leh Airport after checking out from your hotel. Bid farewell to Ladakh and board your flight back home, marking the end of your trip.

Leh AirportDeparture
