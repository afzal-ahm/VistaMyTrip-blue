🏞️ Overview

Spiti Valley from Delhi Overview

Imagine starting your journey in Delhi, leaving behind the city's chaos, and entering the serene landscapes of Manali and Spiti Valley, where rugged mountains meet clear blue skies. Kaza, Tabo, Dhankar, Pin Valley, and the shimmering Chandra Tal Lake will be your playgrounds, each offering a unique flavor of Spiti's natural beauty and ancient monasteries. This journey is a rare opportunity to explore remote high-altitude villages and ancient monasteries and connect with the warm-hearted locals.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Depart from Delhi to Manali

Overnight Bus

Upon reaching Delhi, you will have to proceed towards your boarding point for the overnight bus from Delhi to Manali.

DepartureOvernight Journey

Day 2: Arrival in Manali and Sightseeing

Manali

Upon your arrival in Manali, the driver will pick you up and take you to your hotel. After check-in and breakfast, go for a sightseeing tour, exploring Hadimba temple, Buddhist temple, Clubhouse, and Old Manali. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Hadimba TempleOld Manali

Day 3: Manali to Kaza via Kunzum Pass

Kaza

Post-check-out, the driver will pick you up and take you to Kaza. Pass through the Atal Tunnel and drive into the stunning landscape to approach Chatru and Batal. Visit Kunzum Pass (14,931 ft) before stopping at Losar Village. Continue to Kaza for an overnight stay.

Atal TunnelKunzum PassLosar Village

Day 4: Sightseeing Around Kaza

Kaza

The driver will pick you up for a sightseeing tour around Kaza. Visit Komik, Asia's highest village, then Hikkim for the world’s highest post office. Explore Langza village and the ancient Lang Temple. See the world-famous Key Monastery and the beautiful Kibber Village before returning to Kaza.

KomikHikkimKey Monastery

Day 5: Excursion to Tabo, Dhankar, and Pin Valley

Kaza

The driver will pick you up for an excursion to Tabo. Visit the amazing Tabo Monastery, then the tiny village of Dhankar. Explore Pin Valley National Park, home to the Snow Leopard. Visit Kungri Monastery and Mudh village before returning to your hotel in Kaza.

Tabo MonasteryDhankarPin Valley

Day 6: Kaza to Chandratal Lake

Chandratal

Post-check-out, the driver will take you to Chandratal Lake. Enjoy its mesmerizing beauty, framed by the Chandra Bhaga mountain range. The lake changes its appearance throughout the day. After admiring the lake, check into your pre-booked camp for an overnight stay.

Moon LakeCamping

Day 7: Chandratal to Manali and Departure to Delhi

Checkout

The driver will pick you up from the campsite and take you back to Manali for your bus to Delhi. Take in the beautiful views of Geyan Peaks and pass through the Atal Tunnel. Spend some time at Mall Road before heading to the bus stand for your overnight journey to Delhi.

Atal TunnelDepartureEnd of Trip
