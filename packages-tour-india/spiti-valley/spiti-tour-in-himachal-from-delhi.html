Chandigarh To Spiti Bike Expedition Overview

Get ready for an epic adventure trip Spiti Valley Bike Trip through the rugged and breathtaking terrain of the Spiti Valley on two wheels. Starting in Chandigarh, you’ll pass through the twists and turns that lead you to the snowy paradise of Narkanda, the remote beauty of Chitkul, and the peaceful charm of Kalpa. As you ride further, you'll encounter hidden gems like Nako, Gue, and Tabo. However, the real thrill comes when you hit the trails of Kaza, Key, Langza, Hikkim, Komic, Chicham, and the high-altitude Kunzum Pass.

Route Details

Chandigarh - Narkanda - Chitkul - Kalpa - Kaza - Chandratal - Manali - Chandigarh

Start & End Point

Chandigarh

Passes Covered

Kunzum La Pass & Rohtang Pass

Package Duration

9 Days & 8 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • Stay on a double- and triple-sharing basis in hygienic and sanitized hotels / camps.
  • Buffet breakfast and dinner on all days.
  • Royal Enfield Himalayan 411cc motorbike with Helmet (Except for Own Bike Variant).
  • Services of experienced trip leader and motorcycle Mechanic.
  • Fuel for the complete trip and backup fuel (Except for Own Bike Variant).
  • Inner Line permit, Manali Green Tribunal Permit, and Backup Vehicle.
  • First Aid kit, Oxygen cylinder, and Bonfire wherever possible.

Trip Highlights

  • Gear up your engines as you set out on a lifetime adventure through the rugged terrains of Spiti.
  • Stand tall before the sacred Kailash Parvat graced by the crimson hues of sunset at Kalpa.
  • Make a stop at the Key Monastery of Kaza, a peaceful retreat in the lap of Himalayan beauty.
  • Send postcards of your journey to your loved ones from the world’s highest post office of Hikkim.
  • Join this bike trip through the high roads of Spiti as you create new bonds and lasting memories.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Chandigarh | The First Planned City of India

Chandigarh

Reach your pre-booked hotel in Chandigarh, check-in and rest for sometime. You will then meet your road-captain for a briefing session and also the day-wise plan. After that, you will be introduced to the Royal Enfield Bikes that you will ride throughout the tour. Later, have dinner, retire to your bed and prepare for the next day.

ArrivalBriefing SessionBike Allocation

Day 2: Chandigarh to Narkanda | The Apple Capital of India

Narkanda

Check out from your hotel and embark on a scenic road journey towards Narkanda, known as the fruit bowl of Himachal Pradesh. As you continue, the terrain becomes more rugged, revealing sweeping vistas of the Himalayan ranges. On reaching Narkanda, check-in to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Himalayan ExpresswayChailApple Orchards

Day 3: Narkanda to Chitkul | The Last Inhabited Village

Chitkul

Post check-out, start your journey from the hotel to Chitkul, the last inhabited village before the Indo-China Border. Embark on a picturesque road trip, indulging in the limitless beauty of Kinnaur Valley. Follow the Sutlej River and turn right at Karcham for Chitkul. Check-in and rest at the hotel/homestay for an overnight stay.

Kinnaur ValleySutlej RiverHindustan ka Akhri Dhaba

Day 4: Chitkul to Kalpa | Spot the Sacred Shivling Rock

Kalpa

Check out from the hotel and resume your bike journey towards the quaint town of Kalpa. Retrace towards Karcham and take a left turn from Powari to reach ReoKong Peo. Continue to reach Kalpa and have a comfortable overnight stay in a camp/homestay, from where you can witness the beauty of Kinnaur Kailash Peak.

ReoKong PeoKinnaur Kailash Mountain

Day 5: Kalpa to Kaza | Explore the 'Ajanta of the Himalayas'

Kaza

Check out from your camp and embark on a road journey to Kaza. Cross the Khab Bridge and the merging of Spiti & Satluj Rivers. Stop at Nako Lake for lunch. Take a detour to see the 500-Year-Old Mummy at Gue Monastery. Proceed to Tabo Monastery, and finally arrive in Kaza.

Nako LakeGue MonasteryTabo Monastery

Day 6: Sightseeing Around Kaza | World's Highest Post Office

Kaza

Wake up and explore the grand Key Monastery with its historic murals. Head towards Langza Village and visit Lang Temple. Then, visit Hikkim, home to the world's highest post office. Continue to Komik, Asia's highest Village. Return to your Kaza hotel/homestay for an overnight stay.

Key MonasteryLangzaHikkimKomik

Day 7: Kaza to Chandratal Lake via Kunzum La

Chandratal Lake

Check out from your hotel in Kaza and begin your journey to Chandratal Lake. Pass through Kunzum La, which connects Lahaul Valley to Spiti Valley. Admire the beauty of Chandratal Lake as it changes color throughout the day. Conclude the day's adventure by savoring dinner and enjoying an overnight stay at the cozy campsites.

Kunzum LaMoon LakeCamping

Day 8: Chandratal Lake to Manali via Rohtang Pass

Manali

Check-out from your camps and start riding towards Manali. Pass the iconic Rohtang Pass, treating your eyes to the stunning Himalayan Range panorama. Upon arrival in Manali, settle into your hotel. Roam the charming Mall Road or seek solace at the revered Hadimba Devi Temple.

Rohtang PassMall RoadHadimba Temple

Day 9: Manali to Chandigarh | Bid Farewell to the Mountains

Checkout

Pack your bags and check-out from your hotel. Begin your bike journey back to Chandigarh. Upon reaching Chandigarh, hand over your bikes and bid farewell to your fellow riders. Return home with a bagful of memories.

Chandigarh ArrivalEnd of Trip
