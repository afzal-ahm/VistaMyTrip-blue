🏔️ Overview

A signature Manali experience

Experience the enchanting beauty and vibrant culture of Manali with this 3-night, 4-day tour that perfectly blends adventure, nature, and tradition. Nestled in the picturesque Kullu Valley, Manali is a haven for those seeking both relaxation and excitement.

Begin your journey by exploring the historic Hadimba Temple, a serene and cultural marvel surrounded by cedar forests. Stroll through Old Manali, where you’ll discover charming cafes, local markets, and vibrant Himalayan culture. A visit to the Manali Mall Road offers an opportunity to shop for souvenirs, woolen clothes, and handcrafted items.

For adventure enthusiasts, the Solang Valley awaits with activities like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing in winter months. Discover the beauty of Rohtang Pass, where snow-clad mountains and panoramic views leave you spellbound. Immerse yourself in nature’s splendor as you visit the Vashisht Hot Springs and take a walk through apple orchards.
