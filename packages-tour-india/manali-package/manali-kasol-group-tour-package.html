🏔️ Overview

A signature Manali experience

Embark on an unforgettable 4-night, 5-day journey from Delhi to the mesmerizing hill town of Manali, paired with a complimentary excursion to the serene Kasol. Explore Manali’s iconic attractions, including Solang Valley for adventure and Hadimba Temple for spiritual charm. Stroll along Mall Road and soak in the natural beauty of waterfalls and apple orchards.

Enjoy a day trip to Kasol, the “Mini Israel of India,” with its scenic landscapes, riverside cafes, and vibrant vibe. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers, this exclusive tour offers a perfect mix of excitement and relaxation amidst the Himalayas.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Delhi I Overnight Journey to Manali

Travel

Welcome to the capital city of India! Upon arrival at RK Ashram/Majnu-ka-Tilla bus stand in Delhi, board your AC Volvo and continue on an overnight journey towards Manali.

Delhi DepartureVolvo Transfer

Day 1: Arrival in Manali | Manali Sightseeing Tour

Manali

By the morning, your bus ride will come to a halt at the Manali Bus Stand. Get transferred to the hotel, freshen up, and head for a sightseeing tour including the Hidimba Devi Temple, Vashist Kund, and Van Vihar National Park.

Hidimba TempleVan ViharMall Road

Day 2: Excursion to Solang Valley

Adventure

Embark on an excursion to Solang Valley. Along the way, catch glimpses of the scenic beauty of Kothi, Gulaba, and Marhi. Indulge in thrilling adventure activities such as paragliding, skiing, and zorbing.

Solang ValleyAdventure Sports

Day 3: Excursion to Manikaran via Kasol and Kullu | Departure Day

Exploration

Board your transfer to Manikaran. Make a stop in Kullu to visit a local rabbit farm. Continue to Manikaran and visit the sacred Gurudwara Sahib. Spend leisure time in Kasol before boarding your return AC Volvo to Delhi in the evening.

KulluKasolManikaran Sahib

Day 4: Departure Day

Checkout

You would reach RK Ashram/Majnu-ka-tilla bus stand by morning. This marks the end of your trip. Have a safe journey ahead!
