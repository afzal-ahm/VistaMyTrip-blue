🌿 Overview

A dreamy honeymoon getaway

Escape to the picturesque hills of Himachal Pradesh with this 6-night, 7-day romantic getaway to Shimla and Manali, designed for couples seeking a perfect blend of tranquility, adventure, and breathtaking landscapes. This journey offers an idyllic retreat where love blossoms amidst the beauty of the majestic Himalayas.

Main Stops

Chandigarh, Shimla, Kufri, Jibhi, Manali, Solang Valley.

Trip Style

Romantic, scenic, and destination-rich.

Best For

Newlyweds and couples celebrating anniversaries.

Pace

Relaxed, allowing time for private moments.

Package Inclusions

  • 2 nights stay in Shimla with breakfast and lunch or dinner
  • 1 night stay in Jibhi with breakfast and lunch or dinner
  • 3 nights stay in Manali with breakfast and lunch or dinner
  • Shimla and Kufri Sightseeing Tour
  • Jibhi Sightseeing Tour
  • Manali Sightseeing Tour
  • Combo of Adventure Activities in Solang Valley - Excursion

Trip Highlights

  • Explore the scenic landscapes of Himachal, where Shimla's colonial charm, Manali's snow-capped peaks, and Jibhi's waterfalls offer a romantic escape.
  • Marvel at the stunning neo-Gothic architecture of the Christ Church in Shimla.
  • Admire the Hanuman statue at Jakhoo Hill, the highest point in Shimla and soak-in the stunning views of the city.
  • Witness the serene beauty of Van Vihar National Park in Manali, offering a peaceful retreat.
  • Glide through the skies with paragliding or enjoy skiing down the slopes with your partner in the Solang Valley.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival in Chandigarh I Transfer to Shimla

Day 0

Upon arrival at Chandigarh Airport/Railway Station/Bus Stop, get transferred to Shimla. Upon reaching, check-in to your hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. You can visit the Himachal State Museum & admire its unique architecture and antique collections. Also, you can stroll through Lakkar Bazaar, known for its wooden handicrafts, or take in the serene beauty of Chadwick Waterfall. Later, return to your hotel on your own for a comfortable overnight stay.

ChandigarhShimlaLeisure

Day 1: Sightseeing Tour of Shimla & Kufri

Day 1

In the morning, get ready and board your transfer to explore Shimla and Kufri. Start with a visit to Jakhoo Hill, Shimla's highest point, and home to the revered Hanuman Temple. Then, get driven to Kufri and explore the Himalayan Nature Park known for diverse wildlife like leopards and Himalayan monals. Later, get driven to Shimla and stroll along Mall Road while soaking in its bustling atmosphere. Also, explore other attractions before getting transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ShimlaKufriSightseeing

Day 2: Transfer to Jibhi | Day at Leisure

Day 2

In the morning, check-out from your Shimla hotel and get transferred to the scenic town of Jibhi. En route, pass through the stunning Jalori Pass, offering amazing views of the mountains and lush greenery. Upon arrival in Jibhi, check in at your hotel and relax for a while. Then, spend the rest of the day at leisure, where you can explore local landmarks like Serolsar Lake, a serene high-altitude lake. Later, return to your hotel on your own for a comfortable overnight stay.

JibhiJalori Pass

Day 3: Sightseeing Tour of Jibhi I Transfer to Manali

Day 3

Today, after check out, board your transfer for a sightseeing tour of Jibhi. Start with a visit to the cascading Jibhi Waterfall, a perfectly serene spot perfect to relax. Next, visit Mini Thailand and soak-in its tranquil vibe. Also, explore the historic Chehni Kothi, known for its unique architecture and panoramic views. Later, continue your journey to Manali. Upon arrival, check in at your hotel and enjoy your overnight stay.

Jibhi WaterfallManali

Day 4: Manali Sightseeing Tour

Day 4

In the morning, board your transfer for a sightseeing tour of Manali. Start with the Hidimba Temple and witness its distinctive pagoda style with a forest backdrop. Next, visit the Vashist Temple, where you can take a dip in its rejuvenating natural hot springs. Also, explore the Tibetan Monastery and soak-in its peaceful ambiance. Later, wander through Mall Road to visit the local shops and eateries. After an amazing day, get transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ManaliHidimba Temple

Day 5: Excursion to Solang Valley

Day 5

On this day, board your transfer for an amazing excursion to Solang Valley. Upon reaching, dive into thrilling activities like zorbing, paragliding, and skiing against the backdrop of stunning mountain views (at an additional cost). Also, admire the scenic beauty of the surroundings before getting transferred back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Solang ValleyAdventure

Day 6: Departure Day

Day 6

After a relaxing morning, check out of the hotel and get transferred to Chandigarh Airport / Railway Station / Bus Stop, marking the end of your unforgettable trip!
