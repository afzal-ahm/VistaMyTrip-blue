🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Delhi - Shimla

Day 0

The tour begins as you arrive at Shimla, and our tour executive will welcome you. After that, you will get a swift transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Check into a hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. Stroll around the local bazaar or enjoy the romantic background of the majestic Himalayas. You stay at the hotel overnight.

ArrivalShimla LeisureDinner Included

Day 1: Shimla - Kufri

Day 1

As you finish your breakfast, you tour Shimla exploring the eminent tourist spots. The popular ones are Mall Road, Jakhoo Hill, Chadwick Falls, Himachal State Museum, Summer Hill, and Prospect Hill. In the afternoon, you can make your way to Kufri Valley for an exciting excursion. Finally, return to your hotel for dinner and a night's stay.

Mall RoadKufri ExcursionBreakfast & Dinner

Day 2: Shimla – Manali

Day 2

After breakfast, you proceed to Manali and visit Kullu Valley on your way. Take a glimpse at the verdant fields and apple, cherry, and plum orchards. After exploring Kullu move on to Manali and check into a resort as you arrive for an overnight stay.

Kullu ValleyOrchardsBreakfast & Dinner

Day 3: Manali local sightseeing

Day 3

Enjoy your breakfast. Relax for some time and after getting refreshed, head out for sightseeing in Manali. The places you will visit include Hadimba Devi Temple, a four-storied wooden structure with a tower reaching up to 24m high. You will also visit the Manu Temple and the Vashisht Temple, whose waters are said to possess healing properties. Spend the evening at your own leisure and return to the hotel.

Hadimba TempleVashisht TempleBreakfast & Dinner

Day 4: Explore Solang Valley & Atal Tunnel (South Portal)

Day 4

Have a healthy breakfast at the hotel. Today you will be taking a day tour to Solang Valley with Atal Tunnel. This tunnel is one of the highest in the world. The route takes you from Manali to Solang Valley and through the Tunnel's south Portal to Sissu. Spend some time clicking pictures of the amazing Lahul & Spiti Valley. Later return back and enjoy some adventure activities at Solang valley (at your own cost). Have dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Atal TunnelSolang ValleyBreakfast & Dinner

Day 5: Manali (Full Day Trip to Kullu & Manikaran)

Day 5

After breakfast, drive to Kullu to enjoy thrilling River Rafting (on your own expenses), visit the World famous Pashmina Shawl Factory or explore the local Market. Later proceed to Manikaran enroute cover Vaishno Devi Temple. Manikaran is an important pilgrimage site featuring a Religious Ram Temple & Impressive Gurudwara (Consists of Extremely hot Water springs). In the evening, get back to the hotel & overnight stay.

Kullu RaftingManikaranBreakfast & Dinner

Day 6: Manali – Delhi

Day 6

After Breakfast, check out from the hotel & transfer to Delhi. On arrival, your trip ends with lots of memories.
