🌿 Overview

A broad Kerala route with strong variety

This package is designed for travelers who do not want to choose between the Kerala hills, wildlife stops, backwaters and coast. It is one of the strongest all-round options if the goal is to experience the state in a single larger journey.

Main Stops

Kochi, Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey, Kovalam and Trivandrum.

Trip Style

Scenic, varied and destination-rich.

Best For

Families and couples wanting fuller coverage.

Pace

Balanced, but better suited to longer vacations.

Package Inclusions

  • 7 nights accommodation across the route.
  • Cab transfers and sightseeing assistance.
  • Meals as per selected stay plan.
  • Kerala hill station, backwater and coastal route coverage.

Suggested Route Flow

  • Kochi arrival and onward transfer to Munnar.
  • Thekkady for wildlife and nature.
  • Alleppey for backwater experiences.
  • Kovalam and Trivandrum for a coastal finish.
