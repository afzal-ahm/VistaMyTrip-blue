🏞️ Overview

Andaman Coastal Retreat Overview

Escape to the serene and pristine Andaman Islands on this 5-night, 6-day coastal retreat. Experience the tropical beauty, crystal-clear waters, and lush landscapes of this paradise, with island-hopping adventures, relaxing beach days, and vibrant marine life.

Arrive at the capital city of the Andaman Islands and explore historical sites like Cellular Jail. Relax on the famous Radhanagar Beach, one of the most beautiful beaches in Asia, and indulge in water activities like snorkeling and scuba diving. Finally, discover the untouched beauty of Neil Island, known for its tranquil beaches and vibrant coral reefs, perfect for a day of swimming and relaxation.

Trip Highlights

  • Experience Andaman’s pristine beaches, and clear turquoise waters, offering a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation in a tropical paradise.
  • Visit the historic Cellular Jail, also known as Kalapani, to witness the famous light and sound show that brings to life the stories of India's freedom fighters.
  • Wander the white sandy shores of Neil Island, enjoying the views and the gentle sounds of waves surrounded by lush tropical woodlands.
  • Discover Elephant Beach's stunning white sands and crystal-clear waters, perfect for snorkeling and water sports amidst vibrant marine life.
  • Relax on the beautiful Radhanagar Beach, one of Asia’s finest, renowned for its breathtaking sunset and crystal-clear waters.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair | Trip to Corbyn Cove Beach and Cellular Jail

Port Blair

Welcome to Andaman! Upon arrival at the airport, get transferred to your hotel. Once you have checked in, take some time to relax. Later, get driven to Corbyn Cove Beach, where you can enjoy stunning ocean views and the peaceful environment. Then, experience the rich history of the Cellular Jail through an engaging light and sound show. After the show, board your transfer back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalCorbyn Cove BeachCellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Day 2: Transfer to Havelock Island | Radhanagar Beach Tour

Havelock Island

Post check out, get transferred to Phoenix Bay Harbour. Next, board your ferry to the picturesque Havelock Island. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and take some time to settle in. Later, get driven to the renowned Radhanagar Beach, famous for its pristine white sands and turquoise waters. Spend the afternoon basking in the sun and enjoying the serene beach environment. Afterward, return to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Ferry TransferHavelock IslandRadhanagar Beach

Day 3: Visit to Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach

In the morning, get transferred for a captivating day trip to the Elephant Beach. Upon arrival, take your time to explore the vibrant Underwater World at Neemo Reef with a range of water sports available (additional cost). Enjoy the stunning coastal views and the crystal-clear waters that make this beach a tropical paradise. After a day filled with adventure and relaxation, get driven to your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

Elephant BeachSnorkelingMarine Life

Day 4: Visit to Kalapathar Beach | Transfer to Neil Island

Neil Island

Today, check out from your hotel and get transferred to the serene Kalapathar Beach. There, you can enjoy a leisurely stroll along the pristine shoreline and take in the breathtaking natural beauty. After soaking in the serene atmosphere, get driven to the Havelock Island Harbour for your ferry ride to Neil Island. Upon arrival, you'll be transferred to your hotel. Check in and rest for an overnight stay.

Kalapathar BeachFerry RideNeil Island Arrival

Day 5: Neil Island Sightseeing Tour | Transfer to Port Blair

Port Blair

Post check out, you’ll get transferred to explore Neil Island. Visit the picturesque Laxmanpur Beach, the serene Sitapur Beach, and the vibrant Bharatpur Beach. There, you can also indulge in various water sports and activities. Following your island exploration, get driven to Neil Island Jetty and board your ferry to Port Blair. Upon arrival, get transferred to your hotel. Check in and rest for an overnight stay.

Laxmanpur BeachSitapur BeachBharatpur BeachFerry to Port Blair

Day 6: Departure Day

Checkout

After checking out from your hotel, you will be driven to Veer Savarkar International Airport for your flight back home. This marks the end of your trip.

CheckoutAirport TransferEnd of Trip
