Andaman Wonders Overview

Andaman's pristine beauty makes it an ideal and desirable tourist destination around the world. Here, you will find a perfect amalgamation of thrilling adventures and a serene environment. Shimmering white sand beaches, a turquoise lagoon, mesmerising coral reefs, and the historical brilliance of this place are a feast for the eyes.

The beautiful beaches of Andaman invite you to spend your vacation relaxing and taking in the tranquil atmosphere. Embark on this mesmerising tour to indulge in the thrill that awaits you.

  • Admire the grace of tropical islands in Andaman with this specially curated package ideal for Family and Friends.
  • Travel to Havelock Island via ferry ride and visit Radhanagar Beach which is recognised as one of the best beaches in Asia.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair | Corbyn Cove Beach & Cellular Jail Tour

Port Blair

Welcome to the Andaman Islands! Upon your arrival at Port Blair, you'll be transferred to your hotel for check-in and a brief rest. Later, enjoy a visit to Corbyn Cove Beach, where you can unwind amidst stunning ocean views. In the evening, immerse yourself in history with a light and sound show at the Cellular Jail. Afterward, return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalCorbyn Cove BeachCellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Day 2: Transfer to Havelock Island | Radhanagar Beach Excursion

Havelock Island

After check-out, head to Phoenix Bay Harbour to board your ferry to the beautiful Havelock Island. Upon arrival, check in to your hotel and relax. In the afternoon, explore Radhanagar Beach, known for its soft white sands and clear blue waters. Spend time enjoying the tranquil surroundings before returning to your hotel for the night.

Ferry TransferHavelock IslandRadhanagar Beach

Day 3: Excursion to Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach

Begin your day with a visit to Elephant Beach, a popular spot for water sports and underwater exploration. Enjoy activities like snorkeling or scuba diving at the vibrant Neemo Reef (additional cost). After a day of adventure, relax and soak in the natural beauty of the beach before heading back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Elephant BeachSnorkelingMarine Life

Day 4: Visit Kalapathar Beach | Ferry to Neil Island

Neil Island

After check-out, you'll be driven to Kalapathar Beach for a peaceful walk along its untouched shores, taking in the stunning coastal views. Later, transfer to the Havelock Harbour to catch your ferry to Neil Island. Upon arrival, check in to your hotel and enjoy a restful night.

Kalapathar BeachFerry RideNeil Island Arrival

Day 5: Neil Island Sightseeing & Return to Port Blair

Port Blair

After check-out, spend the day exploring Neil Island, including visits to Laxmanpur Beach, Sitapur Beach, and Bharatpur Beach. You can also enjoy water activities here. After your tour, board your ferry back to Port Blair and check in to your hotel for the night.

Laxmanpur BeachSitapur BeachBharatpur BeachFerry to Port Blair

Day 6: Departure from Port Blair

Checkout

After check-out, you'll be transferred to Veer Savarkar International Airport for your flight back home, marking the end of your memorable Andaman journey.

CheckoutAirport TransferEnd of Trip
