🏞️ Overview

Experience Offbeat Andaman Overview

Discover the untouched beauty of the Andaman Islands with this 6-night, 7-day offbeat tour, perfect for travelers seeking a unique and tranquil experience. Explore lesser-known islands, pristine beaches, and rich marine life away from the usual tourist spots. Immerse yourself in nature, adventure, and relaxation as you explore Port Blair, Havelock Island, Radhanagar Beach, Baratang Island, Neil Island, and North Andaman.

Route Details

Port Blair - Havelock Island - Baratang Island - Neil Island - North Andaman

Start & End Point

Port Blair

Key Attractions

Cellular Jail, Radhanagar Beach & Baratang Limestone Caves

Package Duration

7 Days & 6 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • 4 nights stay in Port Blair with breakfast.
  • 2 nights stay in Havelock Island with breakfast.
  • Elephant Beach Tour on a Shared basis.
  • Kalapathar Beach Tour on a Shared basis.
  • Baratang Island with Jalki Jeep Safari.
  • Ross Bay And North Bay Tour on a Shared basis.
  • Day Trip to Corbyn Cove Beach and Cellular Jail.
  • Radhanagar Beach Tour.
  • Airport transfers from Veer Savarkar International Airport.

Trip Highlights

  • With Port Blair's cultural sites and Havelock Island's serene shores, enjoy a blend of history and nature as you explore the Andaman Islands, famous for its pristine beauty.
  • Stroll along the coconut palm-fringed shoreline as you visit Corbyns Cove Beach, famous for its serene surroundings and the gentle waves that lap against the shore.
  • Capture beautiful photographs, with the unique black rocks as you visit Kalapathar Beach, renowned for its serene atmosphere and picturesque setting.
  • Explore the iconic North Bay Islands, famous for its diverse marine life and clear turquoise waters, making it a must-visit destination for adventure and natural beauty.
  • Venture into the rugged wilderness on the Jalki Jeep Safari, offering a thrilling ride through dense forests and rocky terrain, perfect for nature enthusiasts.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair | Port Blair Sightseeing Tour

Port Blair

Welcome to the Andaman Islands! Upon arrival at the airport, you'll be transferred to the hotel. After settling in, you'll be driven for Port Blair sightseeing tour. Stroll around the shores of Corbyn Cove Beach, where you can take in the breathtaking views. Next, delve into the rich history of the region with a visit to the historic Cellular Jail, followed by a light and sound show that brings its past to life. Later, get driven back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalCorbyn Cove BeachCellular Jail

Day 2: Transfer to Havelock Island | Visit to Radhanagar Beach

Havelock Island

After check out from the hotel, get transferred to Phoenix Bay Harbour to board a ferry for a scenic journey to Havelock Island. Upon arrival, get driven to the hotel to check in and relax for some time. Afterwards, get driven to the renowned Radhanagar Beach, known for its serene environment. Enjoy a stroll along the powdery white sands and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the area. After your visit, you'll be driven back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Ferry RideRadhanagar BeachLeisure

Day 3: Visit to Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach

In the morning, get driven to Havelock Jetty to embark on a speedboat ride to the pristine Elephant Beach. Once there, dive into a world of adventure with scuba diving and snorkelling in crystal-clear waters (activities available at an additional cost). Alternatively, unwind with a leisurely walk along the beach, taking in the serene surroundings and stunning views. After a wonderful day of exploration, you'll be transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Speedboat RideElephant BeachWater Sports

Day 4: Visit to Kalapathar Beach | Transfer to Port Blair

Port Blair

After checking out from your hotel, you'll be transferred to the serene Kalapathar Beach, where you can marvel at the stunning vistas of the Indian Ocean. Spend some time soaking in the beauty of its shoreline and turquoise waters. Later, get driven to Havelock Port for your ferry ride to Port Blair. Upon arrival in Port Blair, get driven to the hotel. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Kalapathar BeachFerry RidePort Blair Arrival

Day 5: Day Trip to Ross & North Bay Islands

North Bay Island

In the morning, you'll be driven to Phoenix Bay Harbour to board the speedboat for Ross and North Bay Islands tour. Upon arrival at North Bay Island, immerse yourself in a range of thrilling water activities, including snorkelling, glass-bottom boat rides, and scuba diving (at an additional cost). Later, visit the historic ruins of the Penal Colony on Ross Island, where you can explore the remnants of its intriguing past. Later, you'll be transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Ross IslandNorth Bay IslandPenal Colony Ruins

Day 6: Excursion to Baratang Island

Baratang Island

Get ready in the morning for an excursion to Baratang Island. During this visit, enjoy a boat ride through picturesque mangrove creeks, where you’ll marvel at the lush greenery and tranquil waters. Next, embark on a thrilling speedboat ride to the limestone caves, exploring their unique formations. Following this, enjoy a jeep safari to Jalki, where you can immerse yourself in the island's natural beauty. After a wonderful day, you'll be driven back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Mangrove CreeksLimestone CavesJalki Jeep Safari

Day 7: Departure Day

Checkout

As you wrap up your journey in the Andaman Islands, check out from the hotel. You'll be driven to the airport to board your flight back home. This is where your trip comes to an end.

CheckoutAirport TransferEnd of Trip
