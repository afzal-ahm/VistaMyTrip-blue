9 Days Andaman Island Delight Overview

Andaman's pristine beauty makes it a popular tourist destination all over the world. Here, you will find a perfect amalgamation of thrilling adventures and a serene environment. Shimmering white sand beaches, a turquoise lagoon, mesmerizing coral reefs, and the historical brilliance of this place are a feast for the eyes. This tour will also take you to Baratang Island, which offers you the opportunity to explore a totally different side of the Andamans with dense forests, mangroves, and tribal people. The Jarawa Tribes are the inhabitants of Baratang Island and usually like to stay away from outsiders. Embark on this mesmerizing tour to indulge in the thrill that awaits you.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival at Port Blair

Port Blair

Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel. After check-in, visit the Corbyn Cove beach and go to Cellular Jail[Kaala Paani]. In the evening, see a light and sound show at the jail. Finally, get transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay in Port Blair.

ArrivalCorbyn Cove BeachCellular Jail

Day 2: Port Blair to Havelock Island | Visit Radhanagar Beach

Havelock Island

Post check-out, you will be transferred to board a ferry to Havelock Island from Phoenix Bay Harbour. After reaching the island, you will dropped off at your hotel, check-in and visit Radhanagar Beach. Later, get transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Ferry TransferRadhanagar BeachLeisure

Day 3: Visit to Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach

Early morning, you will be picked to explore Elephanta Beach, famous for untouched corals, and water sports like diving, snorkelling, swimming, trekking, and kayaking[extra cost]. Relax, and enjoy food and drinks at the beach. Then, get dropped off at your hotel in Havelock for an overnight stay.

Water SportsCoral ReefsSnorkeling

Day 4: Kalapathar Beach | Return to Port Blair

Port Blair

Post check-out you will be picked up to visit Kalapathar Beach in Havelock Island. Later, you will be transferred to the Harbor to board a ferry to Port Blair Island. Upon reaching, you will be picked up from the harbour to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Kalapathar BeachFerry RidePort Blair Arrival

Day 5: Port Blair to Rangat Via Baratang

Rangat

Depart early from Port Blair to Baratang Islands, 110 km away via ATR Road. Pass through Jarawas Tribe Reserve, and take a 15-minute ferry ride from Nilambur Jetty to Baratang. Visit the Lime Stone Cave and Mud Volcanoes. Later, get transferred to your hotel in Rangat for an overnight stay.

Baratang IslandMud VolcanoesLimestone Cave

Day 6: Excursion to Diglipur

Diglipur

Start early morning for Diglipur. On the way, stop by Amkunj Beach, Moricedera, Ross & Smith Island, Kalipur Beach, and the Dhaninallah Mangrove Nature Walkway, twin volcanic rocks, a natural sandbar, and rare giant turtles. Post-tour, get dropped off at Rangat for an overnight stay at your hotel.

Amkunj BeachRoss & SmithGiant Turtles

Day 7: Rangat to Port Blair | The Return

Port Blair

Leave early in the morning for Port Blair, passing through the Jarawas Tribe Reserve on the ATR Road[Andaman Trunk Road]. Upon arrival, you can rest at the hotel or explore the surroundings. Later, get transferred for your overnight stay in Port Blair.

ATR RoadJarawas ReserveLeisure Evening

Day 8: Ross and North Bay Island | An Excursion

Ross Island

Visit Andaman Water Sports Complex for a ferry ride to Ross Island and North Bay. Ross Island, for nature walks and sightings. North Bay, for coral reefs, ornamental fish, glass-bottom boat rides and water sports. Later, you will be transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Ross IslandNorth BayMarine Life

Day 9: End of Tour | Departure Day

Checkout

Post breakfast and check-out, you will be transferred to the Port Blair Airport. Board your flight back home carrying the bags filled with memories and a ton of souvenirs.

Check-outAirport DropEnd of Trip
