Underwater Romance in Andaman Overview

Embark on a romantic 5-night, 6-day getaway to the Andaman Islands, where crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and secluded beaches set the perfect backdrop for a dreamy escape. Explore the wonders beneath the waves with exhilarating underwater adventures, along with private beach experiences, creating unforgettable memories with your loved one. Arrive in the capital of the Andaman Islands, visit the iconic Cellular Jail and enjoy a sunset at Chidiya Tapu. Spend quality time on the breathtaking Radhanagar Beach, and relax on idyllic, less-explored islands with pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters.

Trip Highlights

  • Enjoy a romantic escape to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, where secluded beaches and crystal-clear waters offer an unforgettable experience.
  • Witness an amazing light and sound show at Cellular Jail, where the poignant stories of freedom fighters come alive under the night sky.
  • Explore the Elephant Beach, where you can snorkel hand-in-hand through vibrant coral reefs, offering a perfect romantic getaway.
  • Relax at Radhanagar Beach, where soft white sands and crystal-clear waters create unforgettable moments.
  • Marvel at the unique limestone caves and get the rare sight of active mud volcanoes at the Baratang Island.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair | Port Blair Sightseeing Tour

Port Blair

Welcome to the Islands of Eternal Spring! Upon arrival, get transferred to your hotel. Post check-in, relax for a while, and later, board your transfer for a sightseeing tour of Port Blair. Start with a visit to the peaceful Corbyn Cove Beach, known for its pristine shores, and soak-in its amazing atmosphere. Then, visit the iconic Cellular Jail to witness the light and sound show, showcasing the hardships faced by India's freedom fighters. Afterwards, get driven to your hotel.

ArrivalCorbyn Cove BeachCellular Jail

Day 2: Transfer to Havelock | Visit to Radhanagar Beach

Havelock Island

Today, after check-out, enjoy a scenic drive to Phoenix Bay Harbour. Once there, board a ferry to Havelock Island. Upon arrival, get driven to your hotel and check in. In the afternoon, you'll be taken to the world-famous Radhanagar Beach, known for its stunning sunsets and calm, turquoise waters. You can also take time to relax on the pristine sands or swim in the crystal waters. After an amazing time at the beach, get transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Ferry TransferRadhanagar BeachLeisure

Day 3: Day Trip to Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach

In the morning, board your transfer to Jetty for a speedboat ride to Elephant Beach, renowned for its vibrant marine life and crystal-clear waters. Upon reaching, dive into adventure with scuba diving and snorkeling (at extra cost) to explore the underwater beauty of this emerald paradise. Also, you can stroll along the pristine shore and soak in breathtaking sunset views. After an amazing day, you'll be driven back to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Speedboat RideScuba DivingSnorkeling

Day 4: Kalapathar Beach Tour | Transfer to Port Blair

Port Blair

Start your day with a transfer to Kalapathar Beach to witness a stunning sunrise that bathes the Indian Ocean in a golden hue. After soaking in this magical moment, get driven to your hotel and prepare to check out. Then, board your transfer for a scenic drive to Havelock Jetty for your ferry ride to Phoenix Bay Harbour in Port Blair. Upon arrival, disembark from your ferry and check in to your hotel. Post check-in, relax and enjoy your comfortable overnight stay.

Kalapathar BeachSunrise ViewsFerry Ride

Day 5: Excursion to Baratang Island

Baratang Island

In the morning, board your transfer and get transferred to Baratang Island for a full day of exploration. Once there, marvel at the Limestone Caves, famous for their striking cave formations and intricate mineral deposits. Then, experience the thrill of a jeep safari to Jalki, where you can observe an active mud volcano in action. After an exciting day of exploration, get driven back to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Limestone CavesJeep SafariMud Volcano

Day 6: Departure Day

Checkout

Today, get ready and check-out from your hotel in Port Blair. You will get transferred from the hotel to Veer Savarkar International Airport for your flight back home. This marks the end to your amazing trip!

Check-outAirport TransferEnd of Trip
