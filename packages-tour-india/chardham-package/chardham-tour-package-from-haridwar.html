🛕 Overview

A profound journey of faith and devotion

The call of the divine is irresistible, so embark on this journey to the Dev Bhoomi – Uttarakhand which pulls devotees from all age groups throughout the country. Take this journey to the inner self and get a step closer to attaining salvation. This journey will not only help you discover yourself, but will also greet you with spellbinding views of the Himalayas, mountain forests, meadows, waterfalls, rivers, valleys, and other lovely faces of nature that one comes across on the way.

Begin the journey in traditional style – west to east starting from Yamunotri, followed by Gangotri and then to the most revered jyotirlinga Kedarnath and finally ending at Badrinath. Dedicated to different gods and goddesses, the journey to each temple will greet you with a number of myths and will infuse you with positive spiritual energy.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival | Haridwar to Barkot (216 kms)

Travel

You will be picked from Haridwar in the morning and will be taken to Barkot. On your way, there will be a halt at Rishikesh for your biometric test that will take around 4 hours. One can also witness the beauty of the yoga capital of India, Rishikesh while waiting for the test. On reaching Barkot, explore the small pilgrim town situated at the banks of the Yamuna river. Get transferred to the hotel and fulfill check-in formalities. Have dinner and retire for the day.

Haridwar DepartureRishikesh HaltBarkot Stay

Day 1: Barkot - Janki Chatti - Yamunotri | Visit Yamunotri Temple

Yamunotri

Rise and get ready to be served with divinity as today is the first stop of the pilgrimage. Drive to Yamunotri Dham. Reach Janki Chatti, the point from where the 5km trek starts, you can opt for walk, pony or palki. Take a bath in Jamnabai Kund before starting the trek. On reaching the temple, bow your heads down to praise mother Yamuna. Visit Surya Kund, a hot spring near the temple. Trek down to Janki Chatti and later drive back to Barkot for an overnight stay.

Janki Chatti TrekJamnabai KundSurya Kund

Day 2: Barkot to Uttarkashi | Visit Vishwanath Temple

Uttarkashi

Get ready to move ahead from Barkot after breakfast. Today drive towards Uttarkashi, the holy town situated along the banks of river Bhagirathi. On arrival check in to the hotel & freshen up for local sightseeing. Visit Vishwanath temple situated in the heart of the town. Followed by Shakti Temple, famous for its big Trishul. Return to the hotel to have dinner and overnight stay.

Uttarkashi ArrivalVishwanath TempleShakti Temple

Day 3: Uttarkashi - Gangotri - Uttarkashi | Visit Gangotri Temple

Gangotri

Early morning, post breakfast drive to Gangotri via Gangnani. Take a holy dip in Garam Kund at Gangnani. Drive further to Gangotri via the beautiful Harshil valley. On arrival at Shree Gangotri, take a holy dip in the sacred river Ganges which is also called Bhagirathi at its origin. Worship Goddess Ganga, who is said to have descended on earth to absolve the sins of humankind. Later drive back to Uttarkashi and enjoy dinner along with an overnight stay.

Garam KundHarshil ValleyGangotri Temple

Day 4: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi (230 kms) | Visit Ardh Narishwar Temple

Travel

Wake up early and get transferred to Guptkashi after breakfast. See the confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi river at Devprayag. Have a lunch stop at Srinagar. Have a view of Rudraprayag and Ukhimath on the journey. On reaching Guptkashi, get transferred to the hotel. Visit ArdhNarishwar Temple (if time permits). Enjoy dinner at the hotel and retire for the day.

DevprayagRudraprayagArdhNarishwar Temple

Day 5: Guptkashi - Gaurikund - Kedarnath | Visit Kedarnath Temple

Kedarnath

Have breakfast at the hotel in Guptkashi and get transferred to Gaurikund from where the hike to the temple will start. Start the 17kms trek to the Kedarnath temple from Gauri Kund. One can take ponies or palanquins for the journey. On reaching the temple, hail the almighty Lord Shiva. Indulge in evening aarti at Kedarnath. After the temple visit, stay overnight in the hotel at Guptkashi & have dinner.

Gauri Kund17km TrekEvening Aarti

Day 6: Guptkashi to Pipalkoti (121 kms) | Local sightseeing and leisure

Sightseeing

Early morning, after having your breakfast, check-out from your hotel. Get set for your mesmerizing journey to Pipalkoti. Enjoy the scenic view amidst the journey. Reach Pipalkoti and complete the check-in process. Go for local sightseeing (only if time permits). Retire for the day.

PipalkotiLeisure

Day 7: Pipalkoti - Mana Village - Badrinath | Visit Badrinath Temple

Badrinath

Wake up early in the morning and start the journey to Badrinath after breakfast. Get a mesmerizing view of Ganga all across the journey. Firstly, hit the Mana village for local sightseeing and then head towards the Badrinath Temple. Explore the pilgrim town on the holy confluence of the river Alaknanda and Mandakini. Get transferred back to the hotel & check-in. Dinner at the hotel and retire for the day.

Mana VillageBadrinath Darshan

Day 8: Pipalkoti to Rishikesh (220 kms)

Travel

Have Breakfast in the morning at the hotel. Board the traveller/bus and get geared for Rishikesh. Once you reach Rishikesh, get checked-in a hotel. Stroll around and go for local sightseeing (if time permits). You can also pay visit to Ram Jhula as well as Laxman Jhula (only if time permits). Return to hotel and have dinner.

RishikeshRam JhulaLaxman Jhula

Day 9: River Rafting - Neem Beach - Haridwar | End of Tour

Departure

Wake up early in the morning for the most exciting day. Tie your laces up for the most adventurous activity in Rishikesh - River rafting. After a fun-filled experience, head back to your hotel for packup. Enjoy your last meal of the package and return to your end point in Haridwar.
