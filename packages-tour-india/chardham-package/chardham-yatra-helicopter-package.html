🛕 Overview

A profound journey of faith and devotion

Take an amazing journey of Chardham Yatra by Helicopter and visit four sacred places including Kedarnath, Gangotri, Yamunotri & Badrinath. Arrive at the beautiful city of Dehradun and gear up for your devotional Chardham yatra to these four holy places. Reach Yamunotri, located in the Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand, and known as the birthplace of the Yamuna River. Pay homage to the goddess Yamuna and immerse yourself in the peaceful ambiance of the surroundings.

Enjoy VIP darshans at the sacred temples of the cities and have a comfortable stay at premium accommodations where you can unwind and rejuvenate after a day of spiritual exploration. Have a spiritual experience as you attend special pujas & sacred rituals, and seek divine blessings. Treat your taste buds with delicious meals and enjoy a taste of authentic regional cuisine.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Arrival In Dehradun | Welcome to Dev Bhoomi

Travel

Reach Dehradun and get transferred to your hotel from the airport or railway station in a well-maintained vehicle. Either spend the day at leisure or explore nearby beautiful attractions, and spend an amazing time in the city. Have a delicious breakfast and relax after the long journey before your flight tomorrow.

Dehradun ArrivalRest Day

Day 1: Dehradun to Yamunotri | Visit the land of lords!

Yamunotri

Get on the helicopter from SSD Helipad in Dehradun and fly for Yamunotri. Unload your baggage in the hotel, have your breakfast, and leave for Yamunotri temple in Palki/ Pony. Enjoy a 6 km hike and admire the breathtaking views of nature during the journey. Pay your homage and seek blessings in the temple. Visit the sacred Yamuna Mata Temple & Shani Mandir in the evening. Have an overnight stay in Yamunotri (Kharsali).

SSD HelipadPalki / PonyYamunotri Temple

Day 2: Yamunotri to Gangotri | Explore Beautiful Valleys in Uttarakhand

Gangotri

Arrive at Kharsali Helipad in Yamunotri and head towards Harsil, known as Mini Switzerland. Have your early morning breakfast and gear up for a journey to Gangotri temple for darshan. Visit the Gangotri Dham, situated at a high altitude, and admire the panoramic views of snow-capped peaks. Take a short walk and explore nearby beautiful valleys. Have an overnight stay in Gangotri.

Kharsali HelipadHarsilGangotri Dham

Day 3: Gangotri to Kedarnath | Explore Snow-capped Peaks

Kedarnath

Arrive at Harsil Helipad in Gangotri, get on a helicopter, and get transferred to Kedarnath Helipad. Leave for VIP Darshans in the Kedarnath Temple and seek blessings from Lord Shiva. Take in the scenic views of the majestic mountains. Enjoy a delicious dinner and have an overnight stay at Kedar Basecamp, the land of Kedara.

Harsil HelipadKedarnath VIP DarshanBasecamp Stay

Day 4: Kedarnath to Badrinath | See The Deep Valleys of Himalayas

Badrinath

Depart from the Base Helipad in Kedarnath and arrive at Badrinath Helipad. Get transferred to your hotel and enjoy a delicious breakfast or lunch. Head towards Badrinath temple, have VIP darshans, and seek blessings there. Explore Vyas Gufa, the origin point of the Saraswati River, & Mana Village and spend an amazing time. Have an overnight stay in Badrinath.

Badrinath HelipadMana VillageVyas Gufa

Day 5: Badrinath to Dehradun | Bid Adieu

Checkout

Kickstart your day with a delicious breakfast and fly back to Dehradun. Collect your luggage from the hotel, get transferred to the airport or train station, and head back to your destination with lots of cheerful memories.
