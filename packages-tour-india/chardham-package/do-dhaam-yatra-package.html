🛕 Overview

A profound journey of faith and devotion

Discover a spiritual and awe-inspiring journey with our Do Dhaam Yatra Package. Starting from Delhi, move towards the Guptakashi in Uttarakhand, where the pristine mountain air and the gentle sounds of nature set the tone for your spiritual voyage. Kedarnath, the abode of Lord Shiva, within the majestic Himalayan backdrop, and the Badrinath, which is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and surrounded by serene natural beauty.

The trip covers the Rudraprayag, where two rivers converge in a breathtaking confluence. This pilgrimage is not more than just a tour; it's an unforgettable experience, a chance to explore spiritual and natural wonders, and to return with a heart full of blessings.

Main Stops

Delhi, Guptkashi, Kedarnath, Badrinath, Rudraprayag.

Main Draw

Divine Darshan of Kedarnath and Badrinath shrines.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Start trip from Delhi | Embark on a journey to the Wonderland of India

Travel

Total distance: 220 kms | Total time: 5 Hrs

Your trip starts in the late evening of this day as you get picked up from a pre-decided location in Delhi for the transfers to Haridwar. The driver will pick you up from Delhi, from where you will travel overnight to reach Haridwar the next morning.

Delhi DepartureOvernight Journey

Day 1: Haridwar to Guptakashi | Sacred Journey Through Himalayas

Travel

Total distance: 210 kms | Total time: 6 Hrs

The driver will continue the overnight journey to reach Haridwar. After some time you will proceed towards Guptkashi. In the embrace of Guptakashi's serene beauty, the confluence of the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi Rivers gives birth to the sacred Ganga. En route have a small stopover at the Sangam and witness the birth of the sacred river Ganga and feel the divinity. Upon reaching Guptkashi, transfer to a hotel where you can take some rest.

Haridwar ArrivalSangam ViewGuptkashi Stay

Day 2: Guptkashi to Kedarnath | Enter the Holy Shrine of Lord Shiva

Kedarnath Darshan

Total distance: 50 kms | Total time: Full day

Embarking on a spiritual odyssey, the journey to Kedarnath leads pilgrims through the majestic Himalayas. Get ready early morning and have a healthy breakfast, the driver will help you with the transfer from Guptakashi to Sonprayag from where you will board a government vehicle to Gaurikund from the Kedarnath trek start. Get ready to behold the divine aura of Lord Shiva at the mesmerizing Kedarnath Mandir, nestled between two majestic hills. Proceed toward your hotel for an overnight stay and dinner.

SonprayagGaurikund TrekKedarnath Mandir

Day 3: Kedarnath to Guptkashi | Descending from Divine Heights

Return Trek

Total distance: 50 kms | Total time: Full day

From the sacred realms of Kedarnath, the journey back to Guptakashi is a descent from the divine heights. After having a healthy breakfast trek down to the point from where you will board your transfer back. The driver will meet you again at the point where he dropped you off and will help you with the transfer back from Sonprayag to Guptakashi. Complete the check-in formalities and the rest of the day is at leisure for an overnight stay and dinner.

Trek DownSonprayag Pick-upGuptkashi Stay

Day 4: Guptkashi to Badrinath | Divya Desam of Lord Vishnu

Badrinath

Total distance: 200 kms | Total time: 7 Hrs

After having a healthy breakfast the driver will help you with the transfer from Guptkashi to Badrinath Temple. The spiritual expedition to Badrinath unfolds amidst the scenic grandeur of the Garhwal region. Seek Blessings from Lord Vishnu at Badrinath Temple, and Immerse Yourself in the Profound Divinity of Badrinath. As you complete the darshan, the driver will drop you at the hotel. Complete the check-in formalities for an overnight stay and dinner.

Scenic DriveBadrinath DarshanBadrinath Stay

Day 5: Badrinath to Rudraprayag | Witness the Holy Sangam's

Rudraprayag

Total distance: 160 kms | Total time: 5 Hrs

Start a fresh day with the blessings of Lord Vishnu. Proceed toward Mana village, which is the last Indian village from the border of India and Tibet in the Himalayas. After that, you will depart for Rudraprayag and have short stopovers at 4 different prayags of river Alaknanda, namely, Vishnuprayag, Nandaprayag, Karnaprayag, and Rudraprayag. The blending of Alaknanda with Mandakini and other rivers in Rudraprayag creates a serene haven. Driver will drop you at the hotel in Rudraprayag for an overnight stay and dinner.

Mana VillagePanch PrayagRudraprayag Stay

Day 6: Rudraprayag to Delhi | Departure

Checkout

Total distance: 370 kms | Total time: 9 Hrs

Our spiritual adventure in the hills concludes with our hearts filled with the peace of the Dhams we explored. After having a healthy breakfast and completing the check-out formalities, the driver will help you with the transfer from Rudraprayag to a pre-decided spot in Delhi. Let the warmth of our dham visits stay with us, lighting our way and reminding us of the beauty in every journey.
