🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Haridwar to Barkot (210 km, 7-8 hours)

Travel

Meet your representative at the designated location—Hotel, Airport, or Railway Station. Start your journey to Barkot. Upon arrival, check in to your hotel. Enjoy a relaxing evening and overnight stay.

Haridwar DepartureDinner Included

Day 1: Barkot – Yamunotri – Barkot (36 km drive + 6 km trek one way)

Yamunotri

After breakfast, drive to Jankichatti or Phoolchatti. Begin your trek to Yamunotri (6 km). At Yamunotri, bathe in the warm waters of Surya Kund and visit the Yamunotri Temple. Return to Jankichatti and drive back to Barkot.

Surya KundYamunotri Temple

Day 2: Barkot to Uttarkashi (85 km, 4-5 hours)

Uttarkashi

After breakfast, drive to Uttarkashi. En route, visit Shivgufa. Upon arrival in Uttarkashi, visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple and check into your hotel. Rest for the day. Overnight stay in Uttarkashi.

ShivgufaKashi Vishwanath Temple

Day 3: Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Uttarkashi (100 km one way)

Gangotri

Early departure for Gangotri. Visit Gangnani Hot Spring and take a dip in the Kund. Continue your journey to Gangotri, located in the scenic Harsil valley. Bathe in the sacred river Ganges (Bhagirathi) and perform Pooja. Return to Uttarkashi.

Gangnani Hot SpringGangotri Temple

Day 4: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi (220 km, 8-9 hours)

Travel

After breakfast, drive to Guptkashi. Along the way, enjoy views of the Mandakini River at Tilwara. Upon arrival, visit the Ardh Narishwar Temple. Check in to your hotel and overnight stay in Guptkashi.

Mandakini RiverArdh Narishwar Temple

Day 5: Guptkashi – Kedarnath (30 km drive + 19 km trek/heli)

Kedarnath

Post breakfast, head towards Kedarnath. Drive to Sonprayag/Gaurikund, the base point. Trek (or take a helicopter) to Kedarnath Temple. Offer prayers and explore the surroundings. Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

GaurikundKedarnath Temple

Day 6: Kedarnath – Guptkashi (19 km trek + 30 km drive)

Return

Explore Kedarnath in the morning. Return to Guptkashi either by trekking or helicopter. Transfer back to your hotel in Guptkashi for overnight stay.

Kedarnath DarshanGuptkashi Stay

Day 7: Guptkashi to Badrinath (200 km, 8-9 hours)

Badrinath

After breakfast, drive to Badrinath. After a bath in Taptkund, visit the Badrivishal Temple and attend the evening Aarti. Overnight stay in Badrinath.

TaptkundBadrivishal Temple

Day 8: Badrinath – Rudraprayag (155 km, 6-7 hours)

Sightseeing

After breakfast, drive to Rudraprayag via Joshimath. On the way, visit VishuPrayag, Narsingh Temple, KarnPrayag, and NandPrayag. Overnight stay in Rudraprayag.

JoshimathPrayags

Day 9: Rudraprayag to Haridwar (165 km, 6-7 hours)

Checkout

After breakfast, drive to Haridwar or Dehradun/Rishikesh. Drop off at your hotel, airport, or railway station for your onward journey.
