🛕 Overview

A profound journey of faith and devotion

A journey that will take you on a quest for Moksha; a journey filled with fascination and spirituality. These Char Dhams - Kedarnath, Badrinath, Yamunotri, and Gangotri of Uttarakhand are some of the most sacred and significant pilgrimages in India which take you on a path of divinity.

Every one of these Dhams holds a legend and is devoted to a specific deity. Yamunotri being the seat of the Goddess Yamuna is dedicated to the Goddess Yamuna who goes along the pilgrims to the high altitudes of the picturesque Rawai Valley. Gangotri is dedicated to the Goddess Ganga. Kedarnath is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is also a part of the Panch Kedar. Badrinath is dedicated to Lord Vishnu. It is situated on the bank of the River Alaknanda.

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Delhi - Haridwar | Reach Haridwar

Travel

Start your Chaar Dhaam Yatra from Delhi. Reach the pre-determined point at Delhi where our representative will meet you & help you for the smooth transfers. Overnight journey to Haridwar to take a breath of fresh air as you arrive in Uttarakhand.

Delhi DepartureOvernight Journey

Day 1: Haridwar - Barkot | Scenic Views

Travel

After reaching Haridwar, freshen up & have breakfast. Depart for Barkot. On your way, there will be a halt at Rishikesh for your biometric test that will take around 4 hours. You will be passing through Dehradun & Mussoorie in between your journey to Barkot. After reaching Barkot check-in at the hotel. Dinner & overnight stay at hotel.

Rishikesh HaltMussoorie RouteBarkot Stay

Day 2: Barkot - Yamunotri Darshan | Blessings at Yamunotri

Yamunotri

Drive to Yamunotri Dham. Reach Jan Ki Chatti, the point from where the 6km trek starts. Before starting the trek, take a bath in Jamnabai's Kund. On reaching the temple, bow the heads down to praise the mother Yamuna. Visit Surya Kund, a hot spring near the temple. Trek down to Jan Ki Chatti and later drive back to Barkot for an overnight stay.

Jan Ki ChattiYamunotri TempleSurya Kund

Day 3: Barkot to Uttarkashi | Mesmerizing Views

Uttarkashi

Have Breakfast and head towards Uttarkashi. On your journey you will see some of the most mesmerizing views. Reach Uttarkashi, Check-in to the hotel, and take some rest. Rest of the day will be at leisure, where you can do local sightseeing and visit famous & ancient temple dedicated to lord shiva. Dinner & overnight stay.

Uttarkashi ArrivalShiva Temple

Day 4: Uttarkashi - Gangotri - Uttarkashi | Holy Dip in Ganges

Gangotri

Early morning, post breakfast, drive to Gangotri en route at Gangnani. Take a holy dip in Garam Kund at Gangnani. Drive further to Gangotri via the beautiful Harshil valley. On arrival at Shree Gangotri, take a holy dip in the sacred river Ganges (Bhagirathi). Worship Goddess Ganga and attend the Aarti. Later drive back to Uttarkashi.

Garam KundHarshil ValleyGangotri Dham

Day 5: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi | Confluence of Two Rivers

Travel

Wake up early and get transferred to Guptkashi. See the confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi river at Devprayag. Have a lunch stop at Srinagar. Visit a view of Rudraprayag and Ukhimath in the journey. Once reached Guptkashi, get transferred to the hotel. Visit ArdhNarishwar Temple (If time permits). Dinner at the hotel.

DevprayagRudraprayagGuptkashi Stay

Day 6: Guptkashi to Kedarnath Darshan | Lord Shiva's Blessings

Kedarnath

Leave for the Kedarnath darshan early morning. Our first stop is Gaurikund. Start your 16 km hike to Kedarnath temple (you can ride a pony or Palanquin if needed). After completing the darshan in Kedarnath, trek back to Gaurikund. Return to your pre-booked Guptkashi hotel for an overnight stay.

Gaurikund16km TrekKedarnath Temple

Day 7: Guptkashi to Pipalkoti | Amazing Journey Views

Travel

Wake up in the laps of mountains. Have Breakfast and head towards Pipalkoti. On your journey you will see some of the most mesmerizing views. Visit famous Karnaprayag in between your journey. Reach Pipalkoti, Check-in to the hotel, and take some rest. Dinner & overnight stay at hotel.

KarnaprayagPipalkoti Stay

Day 8: Pipalkoti to Badrinath | Blessings of Lord Vishnu

Badrinath

Wake up early and begin your journey to the holy temple of Badrinath. Before visiting the temple, you will visit the last village of India i.e. Mana Village. After sightseeing at Mana Village, you will go for darshan at Badrinath temple. After completing darshan, return back to the hotel at Pipalkoti.

Rishikesh

Have breakfast and start your journey towards Rishikesh. You will see mesmerizing views all along the way. After reaching, check in to your hotel and rest of the day is at leisure. You can visit some famous cafes in Rishikesh and do some local sightseeing. Return back to the hotel for overnight stay.

Rishikesh ArrivalLocal Cafes

Departure

Experience the thrill of rafting through the cold waters of Ganga. Get transferred to the start point. Experience a round of enthralling rapids and take a rest stop at maggie point for cliff jumping. Reach Neem Beach, freshen up, complete checkout formalities, and depart for Delhi to conclude the trip.
