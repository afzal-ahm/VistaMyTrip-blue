🛕 Overview

A spiritually enriching pilgrimage

The Bada Char Dham Yatra is considered the most sacred pilgrimage in Hinduism, connecting the four corners of India. It comprises Badrinath in the North, Dwarka in the West, Jagannath Puri in the East, and Rameswaram in the South. This journey offers devotees absolute spiritual liberation and divine blessings.

Main Destinations

Badrinath (Uttarakhand), Dwarka (Gujarat), Puri (Odisha), Rameswaram (Tamil Nadu).

Main Draw

Divine blessings, ancient temple architecture, and spiritual awakening.

Best For

Families, senior citizens, and devotees of Lord Vishnu and Lord Shiva.

Pace

Relaxed and deeply spiritual, tailored to your comfort.
