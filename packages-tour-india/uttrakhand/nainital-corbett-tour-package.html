🌿 Overview

A lively short break through lakes and forest

Gear up for an exhilarating nature-driven holiday with crisp mountain air, lush forests, tranquil lakes, and the exciting wilderness of Jim Corbett National Park. This route is built for travelers who want scenic hill-station views first and a stronger wildlife finale after that. It keeps the duration compact while still covering Nainital’s most loved stops and Corbett’s adventurous side.

Route

Delhi - Nainital - Corbett - Delhi.

Duration

4 days and 3 nights.

Highlights

Lakes tour, viewpoints and Corbett safari.

Best For

Friends, families and quick escapes.

Trip Highlights

  • Visit Naina Devi Temple, a revered hill-side shrine with beautiful views.
  • Admire Bhimtal, Naukuchiatal and Sattal on the lakes tour around Nainital.
  • Experience panoramic mountain views from Tiffin Top and Snow View Point.
  • Embark on a thrilling Corbett safari in India’s oldest national park.

Package Inclusions

  • Pickup and drop-off from Delhi bus stand or railway station.
  • Comfortable vehicle for sightseeing and transfers as per itinerary.
  • Stay on double- and triple-sharing basis in hygienic hotels.
  • Breakfast as per itinerary.
  • Experienced driver cum guide and sightseeing as per plan.
