🌿 Overview

A straightforward Nainital holiday

Nainital, often referred to as the "City of Lakes," is one of India’s most popular hill stations, renowned for its stunning mountain views and pleasant climate. Centered around the beautiful Naini Lake, the city is surrounded by scenic trekking trails and offers panoramic views of the majestic Himalayan peaks. Visitors can explore attractions like the Nainital Zoo, Snow View, Raj Bhavan and charming local markets. This 2 night 3 day itinerary is designed to cover the must-see parts of Nainital without stretching the trip too long.

Trip Highlights

  • Enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Naini Lake surrounded by lush hills.
  • Visit Naina Devi Temple for lake views and local spiritual atmosphere.
  • Explore viewpoints such as Snow View Point and Tiffin Top.
  • Spend time on Mall Road for shopping, local snacks and evening walks.
