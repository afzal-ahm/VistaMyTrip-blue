Goa Delights: A Perfect 4-Day Holiday Overview

Goa, a tiny emerald land on the west coast of India, is a destination that offers a perfect blend of sun, sand, and sea. This 4-day tour is designed to give you a delightful experience of Goa's vibrant culture, stunning beaches, and rich history. From the bustling nightlife of North Goa to the tranquil shores of South Goa, this package covers the best of both worlds. Explore ancient Portuguese churches, relax on pristine beaches, and indulge in delicious Goan cuisine.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Goa | Leisure Day

Arrival

On reaching Goa airport, meet our tour representative, who will escort you to your pre-booked hotel. After completing the check-in formalities, take some rest for the day. You can enjoy a stroll around the beach in the evening or indulge in your favorite beach activity. Stay overnight at the hotel.

ArrivalLeisureBeach Exploration

Day 2: North Goa Sightseeing Tour

North Goa

After breakfast, get ready for a drive to North Goa. Here you can visit Dona Paula, Calangute, Baga, and Miramar Beach. Head to Panjim and enjoy viewing its unique colonial architecture as you drive through the city. Enjoy lunch at Panjim, then head to Baga Beach for water sports or go on a shopping spree at the Tibetan market. Stay overnight at the hotel.

Dona PaulaBaga BeachPanjim City

Day 3: South Goa Sightseeing Tour

South Goa

After breakfast, head towards South Goa. Today, you will visit the historic churches of Old Goa like the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral. Later, proceed to "Ancestral Goa," a re-creation of Goan rural life with artifact centers and art galleries. After the long sightseeing tour of South Goa, head back to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Old Goa ChurchesAncestral GoaMenezes Braganza House

Day 4: Departure from Goa

Checkout

This is the last day of your Goa tour. Munch some delicious breakfast and then check-out of the hotel. Then, start driving towards the airport, and on reaching, board your flight home. The tour ends here.
