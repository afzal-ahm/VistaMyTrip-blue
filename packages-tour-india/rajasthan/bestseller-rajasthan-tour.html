Gems of Rajasthan Overview

Embark on a royal adventure through Rajasthan, where majestic fortresses, shimmering palaces, and golden deserts await. Begin your journey in Jaipur, the Pink City, and marvel at the grandeur of the Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. Next, venture to Jodhpur, the Blue City, and stand in awe of the imposing Mehrangarh Fort. Conclude your journey in the vast Thar Desert, where camel safaris across the golden dunes of Jaisalmer create an unforgettable experience.

Trip Highlights

  • Discover Rajasthan’s rich heritage with Jaipur's palaces, Jodhpur's blue streets, and Jaisalmer's golden dunes.
  • Visit Jaisalmer Fort, one of the world's few "living forts," where civilization still lives within ancient sandstone walls.
  • Explore Bhadariya Library, Asia's largest underground library, housing over 900,000 treasures.
  • Stroll through Navchokiya and soak in the magic of the Blue City, with its colorful streets and intricate architecture.
  • Admire Amer Fort, a stunning UNESCO World Heritage site and its “Sheesh Mahal”.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur | Half-Day Sightseeing Tour

Jaipur

Welcome to Jaipur! Begin your tour at the magnificent City Palace, then marvel at Jantar Mantar, the world's largest stone sundial. Make a photo stop at Hawa Mahal and experience rituals at Govind Dev Ji Temple. Explore the bustling Tripolia and Johari Bazaars before checking into your hotel.

ArrivalCity PalaceHawa Mahal

Day 2: Jaipur Forts Tour with Jal Mahal & Birla Mandir

Jaipur

Begin at Nahargarh Fort for panoramic city views. Visit Amer Fort and its magical Sheesh Mahal, then explore Jaigarh Fort. Take photos at Panna Meena ka Kund, Jal Mahal, and Birla Mandir. In the evening, visit Chokhi Dhani for a delicious Rajasthani feast.

Amer FortNahargarhChokhi Dhani

Day 3: Transfer to Jodhpur | Jodhpur Sightseeing Tour

Jodhpur

Travel to Jodhpur and check in. Visit the majestic Mehrangarh Fort for panoramic views of the blue city. Discover Jaswant Thada, a stunning marble cenotaph. Stroll through Navchokiya's charming blue houses and explore the local market at Ghanta Ghar.

Mehrangarh FortJaswant Thada

Day 4: Umaid Bhawan Palace | Transfer to Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer

Visit the stunning Umaid Bhawan Palace and its museum. Afterward, travel to Jaisalmer, stopping at India’s largest Date Palm Farm. Upon arrival, check into your hotel. Later, enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Gadisar Lake while catching a golden sunset.

Umaid BhawanGadisar Lake

Day 5: Jaisalmer Sightseeing | Desert Camping Experience

Desert Camp

Visit the iconic Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, and Nathmal Ki Haveli. Explore the famous ghost town of Kuldhara Village. In the afternoon, head to your desert camp for a traditional welcome, camel safari, dune bashing, and a cultural fire show.

Jaisalmer FortCamel SafariCultural Night

Day 6: Departure from Jodhpur

Checkout

Check out and transfer to Jodhpur. En route, stop at the Jaisalmer War Memorial and Bhadariya Library, Asia's largest underground library. Upon arrival in Jodhpur, continue your journey onward, marking the end of your trip.
