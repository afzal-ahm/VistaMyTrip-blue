🏞️ Overview

Rajasthan Blue Odyssey Overview

Udaipur, often referred to as the "City of Lakes," is a captivating city in Rajasthan, India. Known for its serene lakes, majestic palaces, and vibrant history, Udaipur offers a unique blend of natural beauty and royal charm. The iconic City Palace, overlooking Lake Pichola, along with attractions like Jag Mandir, Saheliyon Ki Bari, and the Lake Palace, make Udaipur a dream destination for history, culture, and photography enthusiasts. Its romantic ambiance and picturesque landscapes have earned it a reputation as one of the most beautiful cities in India.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur | Transfer to Pushkar

Pushkar

Upon arrival in Jaipur, embark on a scenic drive to Pushkar and check-in at your hotel. After settling in, visit the renowned Brahma Temple and explore the town. You can also visit Man Mahal and Gau Ghat on your own. Return to the hotel for a peaceful overnight stay.

ArrivalBrahma TemplePushkar Ghat

Day 2: Arrival in Jodhpur | Jodhpur Sightseeing

Jodhpur

Check out from your hotel and proceed towards Jodhpur. After check-in and some rest, enjoy a guided tour of Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada, two of the city's iconic landmarks. After the tour, return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Mehrangarh FortJaswant Thada

Day 3: Arrival in Mount Abu | Mount Abu Tour

Mount Abu

After breakfast, check-out and drive to Mount Abu, Rajasthan's only hill station. Upon arrival, check in at your hotel. In the afternoon, explore the stunning Dilwara Jain Temples and enjoy the scenic beauty of Nakki Lake. Later, return to your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

Dilwara TemplesNakki Lake

Day 4: Arrival in Udaipur | City Palace & Jagdish Temple

Udaipur

After check-out, travel to Udaipur. Upon arrival, check in and visit the magnificent City Palace and the historic Jagdish Temple. In the evening, enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

City PalaceJagdish TempleLake Pichola

Day 5: Udaipur Sightseeing | Photo Shoot Experience

Udaipur

Begin your day with a tour of Saheliyon Ki Bari and Sajjangarh Palace (Monsoon Palace). Afterward, enjoy a special photo shoot session to capture memories of your incredible journey. Later, return to the hotel for a restful overnight stay.

Saheliyon Ki BariSajjangarh PalacePhoto Shoot

Day 6: Departure from Udaipur

Checkout

After breakfast, check out and get transferred to Udaipur Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey, marking the end of your memorable vacation.
