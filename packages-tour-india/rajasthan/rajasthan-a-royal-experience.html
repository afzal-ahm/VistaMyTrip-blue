Rajasthan: A Royal Experience

This 4-day tour takes you through two of Rajasthan's most iconic cities, Jodhpur and Jaisalmer. Start your journey in Jodhpur, the "Blue City," where you can explore the majestic Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and the vibrant local markets. Then, head to Jaisalmer, the "Golden City," renowned for its stunning Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, and mesmerizing desert landscapes. Experience the unique charm of the Thar Desert with a camel ride and a desert safari, making this journey a perfect blend of history, culture, and adventure.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Jodhpur | Jodhpur City Tour

Jodhpur

Upon your arrival in Jodhpur, you'll be transferred to your hotel for check-in. Afterward, embark on a sightseeing tour of the city. Visit the iconic Mehrangarh Fort and the serene Jaswant Thada, both of which offer incredible insights into the region's history and stunning views. In the evening, return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalMehrangarh FortJaswant Thada

Day 2: Jodhpur to Jaisalmer | Travel to the Golden City

Jaisalmer

Check out from your hotel and travel to Jaisalmer, with a stop at the Jaisalmer War Museum along the way. After your journey, check in at your hotel in Jaisalmer. Later, explore the city at your leisure, discovering landmarks like Bada Bagh and Tazia Tower. Return to your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

War MuseumBada BaghTazia Tower

Day 3: Jaisalmer City Tour | Desert Camp Adventure

Desert Camp

Check out from your hotel and set off on a sightseeing tour of Jaisalmer. Visit attractions like Patwon Ki Haveli and Gadisar Lake. Afterward, head to the desert and check in to your desert camp. Enjoy a camel ride across the golden sands and immerse yourself in local cultural performances. Return to your tent for an unforgettable overnight stay under the stars.

Patwon Ki HaveliCamel SafariCultural Night

Day 4: Departure from Jaisalmer

Checkout

After breakfast, check out from your camp and transfer to Jaisalmer Airport or Railway Station to continue your journey, bringing your short but memorable Rajasthan adventure to a close.

Jaisalmer Airport/RailwayDepartureEnd of Trip
