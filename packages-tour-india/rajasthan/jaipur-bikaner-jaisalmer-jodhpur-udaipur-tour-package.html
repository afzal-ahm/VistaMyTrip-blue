🏞️ Overview

Best of Rajasthan Overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Rajasthan with this 8-night, 9-day package, offering a perfect blend of heritage, culture, and adventure. Explore the architectural marvels of Jaipur, discover the holy charm of Pushkar, and wander through Jodhpur's majestic Mehrangarh Fort. Experience Jaisalmer's golden sands with a complimentary desert camping adventure, and marvel at Udaipur's serene lakes and Mount Abu's cool climate. Uncover Rajasthan's vibrant heritage, local crafts, and royal tales.

Trip Highlights

  • Explore the best of Rajasthan visiting attractions from cities like Jaipur, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer, Mount Abu, and Bikaner.
  • Admire the beauty of Amer Fort and explore its popular areas such as Sheesh Mahal and Sila Devi Temple.
  • Have an amazing camping experience in Jaisalmer and enjoy traditional Rajasthani performances.
  • Visit Jaisalmer Fort, one of the few living forts in the world.
  • Explore Jodhpur's Umaid Bhawan Palace, one of the world's largest private residences.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Jaipur

Upon arrival, get transferred to the hotel and check-in. You can relax for the day or self-explore the city visiting attractions like Albert Hall Museum, Chokhi Dhani, etc. Later, return to the hotel by yourself for an overnight stay.

ArrivalAlbert Hall MuseumChokhi Dhani

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing Tour

Jaipur

Get transferred from the hotel for a sightseeing tour of Jaipur. Explore forts like Amer Fort, Jaigarh Fort, and Nahargarh Fort. Continue your tour by visiting beautiful attractions like the Jal Mahal and Birla Temple. Later, get assisted back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Amer FortNahargarh FortJal Mahal

Day 3: Arrival in Bikaner | Bikaner Sightseeing Tour

Bikaner

Check-out from the hotel and board your transfer to Bikaner. Upon arrival, check-in at the hotel. Then, get assisted for a sightseeing tour of Bikaner. Visit Karni Mata Temple and the Camel Breeding Farm. Later, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Karni Mata TempleCamel FarmSightseeing

Day 4: Junagarh Sightseeing Tour | Arrival in Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer

Check-out from the hotel and get transferred to visit Junagarh Fort. See areas like Gaj Mandir, Sheesh Mahal, Anup Mahal, etc. Then, continue your journey to the hotel in Jaisalmer and check-in for an overnight stay.

Junagarh FortTransferJaisalmer Check-in

Day 5: Jaisalmer Sightseeing Tour | Desert Camp Experience

Desert Camp

Check-out from the hotel and board your transfer for a city sightseeing tour. See Jaisalmer Fort, Gadisar Lake, etc. Then, get assisted to the sand dunes. Upon arrival, check-in at the camp and enjoy a camel ride and cultural performances. Later, return to your tent for an overnight stay.

Jaisalmer FortCamel SafariCultural Night

Day 6: Arrival in Jodhpur | Jodhpur Sightseeing Tour

Jodhpur

Check-out from the camp and board your transfer to Jodhpur. Upon arrival, check-in at the hotel. Then, get assisted for a sightseeing tour of Jodhpur. Visit the famous Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. Later, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Mehrangarh FortJaswant Thada

Day 7: Umaid Bhawan Palace Visit | Arrival in Mount Abu

Mount Abu

Check-out from the hotel and get transferred to Umaid Bhawan Palace. Explore its beautiful gardens and museums, while admiring its unique architecture. Then, continue your journey to the hotel in Mount Abu and check-in for an overnight stay.

Umaid Bhawan PalaceMount Abu Arrival

Day 8: Mount Abu Sightseeing Tour

Mount Abu

Get assisted from the hotel for a sightseeing tour of Mount Abu. Visit popular attractions like Guru Shikhar and Delwara Jain Temple. Then, visit Nakki Lake and admire the scenic views around it. Later, get transferred back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Guru ShikharDelwara TempleNakki Lake

Day 9: Departure Day

Checkout

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and get transferred to Udaipur airport/railway/bus station. This is where your unforgettable Rajasthan trip comes to an end.

DepartureUdaipur AirportEnd of Trip
