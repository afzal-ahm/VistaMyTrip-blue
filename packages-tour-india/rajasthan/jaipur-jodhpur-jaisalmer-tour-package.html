Golden Triangle of Rajasthan Overview

'Padharo Mahrey Des', Rajasthan with its romantic palaces and renovated Havelis invites you to unravel its opulence and unbeatable grandeur. Fondly called the ‘Desert Capital of India’ or the ‘Cultural Capital of India’, Rajasthan is one such destination that needs to be explored and not just traveled. Like an oasis in a desert, a trip to Rajasthan is what dreams are made of. Renowned globally as the ‘Pink City of India’- Jaipur reflects shades of cultures, traditions, history, and elegance.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur | Welcome to the 'Pink City'

Jaipur

Meet our representative at a pre-decided location in Jaipur and get transferred to your hotel. Start with a stop at the iconic Hawa Mahal, then visit the world's biggest sundial at Jantar Mantar. Explore the City Palace and Museum, the current residence of the royal family. In the evening, visit Govind Dev Ji Temple and the local market.

ArrivalHawa MahalCity Palace

Day 2: Sightseeing in Jaipur | Explore Amber Fort’s Sheesh Mahal

Jaipur

After breakfast, start a full day of local sightseeing. Dive into history at Amer Fort, which has witnessed numerous wars and royal celebrations. Visit Jaigarh Fort, home to one of the biggest cannons, and Nahargarh Fort for stunning city views. Take a photo stop at Jal Mahal and visit the shimmering white marble Birla Temple.

Amer FortNahargarh FortJal Mahal

Day 3: Jaipur to Jodhpur | Home to the Impregnable Mehrangarh Fort

Jodhpur

Enjoy breakfast and check out. Continue your drive to Jodhpur, reaching by afternoon. Proceed to the mighty Mehrangarh Fort, which sits above a hill and is visible throughout the city. You’ll also visit Jaswant Thada, the "Taj Mahal of Marwar," made of white marble. In the evening, you can explore the market at Ghanta Ghar.

Mehrangarh FortJaswant Thada

Day 4: Jodhpur to Jaisalmer | Travel to the 'Golden City'

Jaisalmer

After breakfast, transfer to Jaisalmer, the Golden City. Stop at the Jaisalmer War Memorial to honor war heroes. Upon arrival, check into your hotel. Head to the Jaisalmer Fort, a living fort that seems to rise from the sands, offering stunning city views. You can shop for amazing leather goods from the shops lining the streets inside.

War MemorialJaisalmer Fort

Day 5: Sightseeing in Jaisalmer | Explore the Sand Dunes

Sam Sand Dunes

Start your day with a visit to the beautiful Gadisar Lake. Then, explore Patwon ki Haveli, Nathmahal ki Haveli, and Salim Singh ki Haveli. In the afternoon, head towards the Sam Sand Dunes and check into a desert camp. Experience the exotic desert with Dune Bashing and a Camel Ride overlooking a spectacular sunset. Enjoy folk performances and an authentic Rajasthani meal.

Gadisar LakeCamel SafariDesert Camp

Day 6: Jaisalmer to Jodhpur and Departure

Checkout

After breakfast, check out from the desert camps. You will then be transferred to Jodhpur Railway Station/Airport for your onward journey, marking the end of your tour with wonderful memories.
