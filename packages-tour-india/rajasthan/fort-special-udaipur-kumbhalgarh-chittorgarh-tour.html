Rajasthan Regal Retreat Overview

Discover the timeless splendor of Rajasthan with the Royal Rajasthan Tour Package, offering an authentic taste of the region's regal heritage. Experience the grandeur of a bygone era, immersing yourself in the luxurious lifestyles of the Maharajas as you explore magnificent monuments and uncover hidden tales of history. From the golden sands of the Thar Desert to the azure lakes of Udaipur, this journey promises stunning landscapes, majestic forts, opulent palaces, and delicious Rajasthani cuisine.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Udaipur | City Exploration

Udaipur

Welcome to Udaipur! Upon arrival, you'll be transferred to your hotel. After relaxing, embark on a sightseeing tour. Visit Sahelio-ki-Bari, a beautiful garden, then head to Fateh Sagar Lake. Continue to Under The Sun Aquarium. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

ArrivalFateh Sagar LakeAquarium

Day 2: Visit to City Palace | Free Day to Explore Udaipur

Udaipur

Today, visit the grand City Palace on the banks of Lake Pichola. Discover the Crystal Gallery inside. In the afternoon, you can explore the city at your own pace, visiting highlights like Jagdish Temple and Bagore Ki Haveli. Spend the evening relaxing at the hotel.

City PalaceCrystal GalleryLeisure

Day 3: Excursion to Kumbhalgarh

Kumbhalgarh

After breakfast, enjoy a scenic drive to Kumbhalgarh. Explore the ancient Kumbhalgarh Fort, one of the largest in India, and admire the stunning views from its long defensive walls. Don’t miss the ancient temples and the magnificent Badal Mahal. Return to Udaipur for an overnight stay.

Kumbhalgarh FortBadal Mahal

Day 4: Day Trip to Chittorgarh

Chittorgarh

Embark on a full-day trip to the historical Chittorgarh Fort. Pass through seven grand gates and take in the views. Visit the Vijay Stambh, with its intricate Arabic engravings, and explore the grandeur of Padmini Palace. After an unforgettable journey, return to your hotel for the night.

Chittorgarh FortVijay StambhPadmini Palace

Day 5: Departure from Udaipur

Checkout

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Udaipur Airport or Railway Station to continue your onward journey, marking the end of your memorable trip.

DepartureEnd of Trip
