Cultural Gems of Rajasthan Overview

Dive into Rajasthan’s cultural riches with this 5-night, 6-day package exploring Jaipur, Udaipur, and Mount Abu. From majestic forts to serene lakes and hill station charm, this journey unveils the diverse heritage of India’s royal state. Indulge in Rajasthani cuisine, explore vibrant bazaars, and discover local traditions as you travel through the Pink City, the City of Lakes, and the tranquil peaks of the Aravalli range.

Route Details

Jaipur - Udaipur - Mount Abu - Udaipur

Start & End Point

Jaipur / Udaipur

Highlights

Amer Fort, Lake Pichola, Guru Shikhar

Package Duration

6 Days & 5 Nights

Package Inclusions

  • 2 nights stay in Jaipur, 2 nights in Udaipur, and 1 night in Mount Abu.
  • Daily Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Comfortable private vehicle (Sedan) for transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided sightseeing tours in Jaipur, Udaipur, and Mount Abu.
  • Excursion to Shrinathji Temple (Nathdwara) and Rayta Hills.
  • Driver night charges, toll tax, and parking.

Trip Highlights

  • Step into the Sheesh Mahal in Amer Fort, where a single ray of sunlight brightens the entire hall.
  • Discover Nahargarh Fort, offering breathtaking panoramic sunset views over Jaipur.
  • Visit Rayta Hills, a scenic viewpoint near Udaipur overlooking the Aravalli range.
  • Ascend Gurushikhar, the highest peak of the Aravallis, for spectacular landscapes.
  • Experience authentic Rajasthani culture and dining at Chokhi Dhani, Jaipur.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise plan

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur | Half Day Sightseeing Tour

Jaipur

Welcome to Jaipur! Upon arrival, begin your tour at the magnificent City Palace. Then, visit Jantar Mantar, the world's largest stone sundial and make a photo stop at Hawa Mahal. Experience the vibrant rituals at Govind Dev Ji Temple. Later, stroll through the bustling Tripolia Bazaar and Johari Bazaar. Check into your hotel for the night.

ArrivalCity PalaceHawa Mahal

Day 2: Jaipur Forts Tour with Jal Mahal & Birla Mandir

Jaipur

Begin your day at Nahargarh Fort, enjoying scenic views of the Pink City. Then, visit the Amer Fort, famous for its Sheesh Mahal. Next, explore Jaigarh Fort and marvel at the largest cannon on wheels. Take photos at Panna Meena ka Kund, Jal Mahal, and the Birla Temple. In the evening, visit Chokhi Dhani for a delicious Rajasthani feast.

Amer FortNahargarhChokhi Dhani

Day 3: Visit to Shrinathji Temple | Arrival & Sightseeing in Udaipur

Udaipur

Post check-out, travel to Udaipur. En route, seek blessings at the sacred Shrinathji Temple in Nathdwara. Once you reach Udaipur, check in to your hotel. Later, visit Lake Pichola, explore the Hathipole Market, and admire views from Ambrai Ghat. Explore Bagore ki Haveli and watch cultural performances before concluding your day at Gangaur Ghat.

NathdwaraLake PicholaBagore ki Haveli

Day 4: Udaipur Local Sightseeing Tour

Udaipur

Begin your day at the City Palace and its Crystal Gallery, exploring over 600 unique crystals. Next, explore Rayta Hills for scenic views and visit the peaceful Ubeshwar Ji Temple. Pay homage at the Maharana Pratap Smarak before making your way to Fateh Sagar Lake to enjoy a boat ride and local treats at Chopati.

City PalaceRayta HillsFateh Sagar Lake

Day 5: Transfer to Mount Abu | Mount Abu Sightseeing

Mount Abu

After check-out, travel to Mount Abu. Upon arrival, visit Guru Shikhar, the highest point of the Aravalli range. Head to Trevor Tank for birdwatching and crocodile spotting. Admire the exquisite craftsmanship of the Delwara Temple and visit Adhar Devi Temple. Capture stunning views from Toad Rock and unwind with a boat ride at Nakki Lake.

Guru ShikharDilwara TempleNakki Lake

Day 6: Departure from Udaipur

Checkout

In the morning, check out from your hotel in Mount Abu and get driven back to the Udaipur airport or railway station. Continue your journey onward, marking the end of your beautiful trip with memories of Rajasthan's cultural gems.
