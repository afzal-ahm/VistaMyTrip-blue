🏔️ Overview

A complete Kasol & Kheerganga experience

A 2N 3D Kasol-Kheerganga tour package is perfect for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those looking to experience the beauty of the Parvati Valley. Kasol is a beautiful village known for its stunning landscapes, while Kheerganga is famous for its hot springs and trekking opportunities.

Main Stops

Kasol, Kheerganga, Chalal Village, and Manikaran.

Main Draw

Trekking, Hot springs, and Cafe culture.

Best For

Adventure seekers, Backpackers, and Nature lovers.

Pace

Adventurous and active.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Departure From Delhi to Chandigarh

Overnight Journey

Departure in the evening to Kasol. The group will assemble at the pickup point. You'll be introduced to the team captains and the whole group. Halt for dinner en route.

DelhiChandigarh

Day 1: Kasol Arrival & Sightseeing

Exploration

Reach Kasol in the morning. Freshen up and relax. Proceed for Kasol sightseeing including the flea market, nearby cafes, and a short 3 km trail to Chalal village. Return to stay for bonfire, music, and dinner.

KasolChalal Village

Day 2: Kheerganga Trek

Adventure Trek

Wake up early and have breakfast. Check out and leave for Barsheni (base point for Kheerganga trek). Reach Kheerganga top after a 5-6 hours trek. Enjoy hot dinner and an overnight stay at the campsite.

BarsheniKheerganga

Day 3: Manikaran & Departure

Hot Springs

Go for a hot water spring bath, have breakfast, and trek down to Barsheni. Transfer to Manikaran to visit the famous Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara. Depart in the evening for your return journey.

ManikaranDeparture
