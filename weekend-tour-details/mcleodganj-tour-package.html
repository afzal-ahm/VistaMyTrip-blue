🏔️ Overview

A complete Mcleodganj & Bir experience

A 3-night, 4-day tour package to McLeodganj can be a great way to explore the picturesque town of McLeodganj in Himachal Pradesh, known for its Tibetan culture, scenic landscapes, and spiritual experiences.

Main Stops

Mcleodganj, Triund, and Bir Billing.

Main Draw

Trekking, Paragliding, and Tibetan Culture.

Best For

Adventure seekers, backpackers, and groups.

Pace

Fast-paced and Adventurous.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation: Hotel stay in McLeodganj, camp stay at Triund Top, and hotel stay in Bir.
  • 6 Meals: 3 breakfasts & 3 dinners.
  • AC Transportation.
  • Taxi Charges from McLeodganj to Bir.
  • McLeodganj & Bir Billing Sightseeing.

Package Exclusions

  • Any personal expenses or adventure activities.
  • Anything not mentioned in the itinerary.
  • Any kind of entry tickets or fees.
  • Any meals or drinks other than inclusions.
  • GST (5%) is applicable extra.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Delhi to Mcleodganj

Overnight Journey

Depart from Delhi and head towards McLeodganj. Halt for dinner en route (at your own expense).

DelhiMcleodganj

Day 1: Mcleodganj Arrival & Sightseeing

Exploration

After reaching McLeodganj, check in and freshen up. Explore the flea market, Bhagsu Waterfall, and Dalai Lama Monastery.

Bhagsu WaterfallDalai Lama Monastery

Day 2: Triund Trek

Adventure

After breakfast, proceed for the Triund trek with stunning views of Dhauladhar Mountains. Overnight stay in a camp at Triund.

Triund TrekCamping

Day 3: Triund to Bir

Scenic Drive

Trek down from Triund and leave for Bir. En route, visit Dharamshala HPCA Stadium and Palampur Tea Garden. Check-in at the homestay in Bir.

DharamshalaBir

Day 4: Bir Billing & Departure

Paragliding

Head to Billing for paragliding (at additional cost). Enjoy the scenic views in Bir and depart for Delhi in the evening.

Trip Ends
