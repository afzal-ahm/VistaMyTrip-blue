🏔️ Overview

A complete Manali and Kasol experience

A Manali Kasol Tour Package is typically designed for travelers who want to explore the beautiful and scenic areas of Manali and Kasol, two popular destinations in Himachal Pradesh, India. These destinations are known for their stunning landscapes, serene atmosphere, and opportunities for adventure and relaxation.

Main Stops

Manali, Sissu, Kasol, and Manikaran.

Main Draw

Scenic mountains, cafes, and nature walks.

Best For

Backpackers, groups, and nature lovers.

Pace

Adventurous, scenic, and relaxed.

Package Inclusions

  • Transportation from Delhi to Delhi via AC Semi-Sleeper Volvo Bus or Tempo Traveler.
  • 3 Nights Accommodation on Sharing Basis (2 Nights Manali, 1 Night Kasol).
  • 6 Meals starting from Dinner on Day 1 to Breakfast on Day 4.
  • Experienced Trip Captain, Bonfire, and driver allowances.

Package Exclusions

  • Any extra meals and stay arrangements not mentioned in inclusions.
  • Travel Insurance, entry fees, or tickets for optional activities.
  • Cost of snow-chained or 4x4 Vehicle if needed due to heavy snowfall.
  • GST (5%) is applicable extra.
