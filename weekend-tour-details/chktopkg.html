🏔️ Overview

A complete Chakrata experience

A 1N 2D (1 Night, 2 Days) Chakrata tour package offers a serene and refreshing getaway to one of the most tranquil hill stations of Uttarakhand. Chakrata, known for its lush green surroundings, scenic beauty, and various adventure activities, is a great place for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Main Stops

Chakrata, Bhuder Cave, Chilmiri Neck Top, and Tiger Falls.

Main Draw

Serene hills, majestic waterfalls, and limestone caves.

Best For

Nature lovers, groups, and quick weekend trips.

Pace

Relaxing but with light trekking.

Package Inclusions

  • AC traveller/bus for travel & transfers.
  • 1-night stay in a Chakrata resort.
  • Meals: 1 breakfast, 1 dinner & evening snacks.
  • Trek to Budher Caves, visit Chilmiri Neck Top & Tiger Waterfalls.
  • Bonfire, music, and trip coordinator assistance throughout.

Package Exclusions

  • Meals/food not in package inclusions.
  • Entry tickets to monuments & public places.
  • Charges for photos/videos during activities.
  • Expenses not mentioned in package inclusion.
  • Extra costs due to natural calamities or unforeseen situations.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Departure From Delhi

Overnight Journey

Board from Akshardham Metro Station, Delhi. Reporting time is 11:00 PM. Begin your overnight journey towards Chakrata.

DelhiChakrata

Day 1: Reach Chakrata & Bhuder Cave

Exploration

Reach Chakrata in the morning, check-in & rest. Drive to Budher Cave (5km hike). Return to the resort in the evening to enjoy a bonfire, snacks, and music. Dinner & overnight stay.

Resort StayBhuder Cave

Day 2: Tiger Waterfall & Chilmiri Necktop

Nature Views

After breakfast & check-out, visit Tiger Falls, Uttarakhand's highest waterfall. Explore Chilmiri Neck Top & enjoy the sunset. Depart for Delhi, reaching by early morning.
