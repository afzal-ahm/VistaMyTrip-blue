🏔️ Overview

A complete Jibhi & Tirthan experience

A 2N 3D Jibhi tour package is ideal for those who want to explore the scenic beauty of Himachal Pradesh, away from the crowded tourist spots. Jibhi is a small village nestled in the Tirthan Valley, known for its lush green landscapes, serene atmosphere, and beautiful rivers. It's a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those looking to unwind in the lap of nature.

Main Stops

Jibhi, Jalori Pass, Serolsar Lake, and Tirthan Valley.

Main Draw

Scenic landscapes, waterfalls, and nature treks.

Best For

Nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Pace

Relaxed but active with treks.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation (2-night stay in Jibhi / Tirthan).
  • 4 Meals (2 Breakfast, 2 Dinner).
  • Transfer to/from in AC Vehicle and all sightseeing mentioned in the itinerary.

🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Departure for Jibhi

Overnight Journey

Departure in the evening. The group will assemble at the pickup point. You'll get a small briefing from the Trip Leader(s), followed by an overnight journey with a halt for dinner.

DepartureBriefing

Day 1: Jibhi Arrival & Exploration

Waterfalls

Reach in the morning and check-in at the stay. By noon, leave for Jibhi Waterfall & Mini Thailand. Enjoy the mesmerizing view of the valley and go for a riverside walk. Enjoy a bonfire with light music at night.

Jibhi WaterfallMini Thailand

Day 2: Jalori Pass & Serolsar Lake Trek

Adventure Trek

Wake up early and enjoy the beautiful sunrise. Have breakfast and leave for Jalori Pass. Start your 4KM trek to Serolsar Lake, which has a narrow walking track lined up with thick tall trees.

Jalori PassSerolsar Lake

Day 3: Tirthan Valley & Departure

Departure

Check out and leave for the Tirthan Valley Choiee waterfall trek (3km). Drive towards Kullu and enjoy river rafting (if time allows). Depart with lots of breathtaking views and reach Delhi the next morning.

Tirthan ValleyKullu
