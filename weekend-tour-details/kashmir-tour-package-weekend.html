🏔️ Overview

A mesmerizing Kashmir experience

A Kashmir tour package offers a fantastic opportunity to explore the mesmerizing beauty of one of India’s most famous tourist destinations. Known for its stunning landscapes, pristine valleys, snow-covered mountains, and lush greenery, Kashmir is a dream destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Main Stops

Jammu, Pahalgam, Srinagar, Gulmarg, and Sonamarg.

Main Draw

Houseboat stay, Shikara ride, and Snow activities.

Best For

Families, couples, and nature lovers.

Pace

Moderate, scenic, and well-balanced.

Package Inclusions

  • 1 Night stay at Jammu hotel, 1 Night stay at Pahalgam, and 2 Nights in Srinagar.
  • 1 Night Stay at a Houseboat in Dal Lake with a complimentary 1-hour Shikara ride.
  • Meals: 5 Breakfasts and 5 Dinners as per the itinerary.
  • Pick up & Drop from Delhi and sightseeing as specified.

Package Exclusions

  • 5% GST, Airfare / Train fare, and any personal expenses.
  • Guide & Entrance fees during sightseeing.
  • Cable Car / Pony Ride in Gulmarg, Pahalgam & Sonamarg.
  • Visit to Aru & Chandanwari in Pahalgam (union car) and Tangmarg chain jeep.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Delhi To Jammu

Overnight Journey

You will depart from Delhi in the evening by Volvo bus, beginning your overnight journey to Jammu. The drive takes approximately 12-14 hours, allowing you to rest and arrive fresh on Day 1.

DelhiVolvo Bus

Day 1: Jammu Arrival

Arrival

Arrive in Jammu and transfer to Pahalgam by tempo or cab. Check into the hotel in Pahalgam, enjoy dinner, and stay overnight.

JammuPahalgam

Day 2: Pahalgam to Srinagar

Valley Visit

Have breakfast and check out from the hotel. Visit Aru, Baisaran, and Betaab Valley by union cab (self-paid). Travel to Srinagar in the evening, check into the hotel, have dinner, and stay overnight.

Betaab ValleySrinagar

Day 3: Srinagar - Gulmarg - Srinagar

Snow Activities

Wake up & freshen up, have breakfast. Visit Gulmarg Gondola (self-paid). Enjoy skiing or trekking, and visit Drung/Frozen Waterfall. Return to your hotel in the evening for dinner.

Gulmarg GondolaFrozen Waterfall

Day 4: Srinagar - Sonamarg - Srinagar

Glacier

Wake up, freshen up, and have breakfast. Visit Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy horse riding or trekking (self-paid). Return to the hotel in the evening, have dinner, and stay overnight.

SonamargThajiwas Glacier

Day 5: Srinagar Sightseeing

Houseboat Stay

After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Visit Mughal Gardens (Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh), Shankaracharya Temple, and Dal Lake. Enjoy a one-hour Shikara ride. Check in to the houseboat for dinner and overnight stay.

Mughal GardensDal Lake

Day 6: Jammu - Delhi

Departure

Wake up, have breakfast, and check out from the houseboat. Transfer from Srinagar to Jammu. Arrive in Jammu, board the Volvo, and depart for your return journey to Delhi.

JammuDelhi Return
