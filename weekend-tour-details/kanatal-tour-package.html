🏔️ Overview

A complete Kanatal experience

A 1N 2D (1 Night, 2 Days) Kanatal tour package typically includes a short but enjoyable trip to the beautiful hill station of Kanatal, located in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. It's the perfect escape featuring stunning artificial lakes, revered temples, and thrilling suspension bridges.

Main Stops

Kanatal, Tehri Lake, and Surkanda Temple.

Main Draw

Camping, Zip lining, and Water sports.

Best For

Adventure seekers, groups, and quick weekend trips.

Pace

Adventurous and thrilling.

Package Inclusions

  • Travel by AC & comfortable traveler.
  • 1 night stay at Kanatal camp-site.
  • Meals: 1 buffet breakfast, 1 buffet lunch, 1 buffet dinner, evening snacks at stay site.
  • Surkhanda Devi temple, zip line & Burma bridge at Kanatal, and Tehri lake visit for water sports.
  • Dedicated Trip Coordinator.

Package Exclusions

  • Any personal expenses or optional adventure activities.
  • Hot water availability.
  • Any kind of entry tickets/fees and meals/drinks other than inclusion.
  • Cost escalation due to unforeseen reasons like weather, landslide etc.
  • 5% GST is applicable extra.
