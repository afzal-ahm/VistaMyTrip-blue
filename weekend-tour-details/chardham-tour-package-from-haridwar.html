🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Haridwar to Barkot

Travel

Meet your representative at the designated location—Hotel, Airport, or Railway Station. Start your journey to Barkot (210 km, 7-8 hours). Upon arrival, check in to your hotel. Enjoy a relaxing evening and overnight stay.

HaridwarBarkot

Day 1: Barkot – Yamunotri – Barkot

Darshan

After breakfast, drive to Jankichatti or Phoolchatti. Begin your trek to Yamunotri (6 km). At Yamunotri, bathe in the warm waters of Surya Kund and visit the Yamunotri Temple. Return to Jankichatti and drive back to Barkot for an overnight stay.

JankichattiYamunotri

Day 2: Barkot to Uttarkashi

Scenic Drive

After breakfast, drive to Uttarkashi (85 km, 4-5 hours). En route, visit Shivgufa. Upon arrival in Uttarkashi, visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple and check into your hotel. Rest for the day. Overnight stay in Uttarkashi.

ShivgufaKashi Vishwanath

Day 3: Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Uttarkashi

Darshan

Early departure for Gangotri. Visit Gangnani Hot Spring and take a dip in the Kund. Continue to Gangotri in the Harsil valley. Bathe in the sacred river Ganges (Bhagirathi) and perform Pooja. Drive back to Uttarkashi for an overnight stay.

Gangnani KundGangotri

Day 4: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi

Travel & Visit

After breakfast, drive to Guptkashi (220 km, 8-9 hours). Enjoy views of the Mandakini River at Tilwara. Upon arrival, visit the Ardh Narishwar Temple. Check in to your hotel in Guptkashi for an overnight stay.

Mandakini RiverArdh Narishwar

Day 5: Guptkashi – Kedarnath

Trek/Heli

Post breakfast, head towards Kedarnath. Drive to Sonprayag/Gaurikund, then trek (or take a helicopter) to Kedarnath Temple. Offer prayers, explore the surroundings, and stay overnight in Kedarnath near the temple.

GaurikundKedarnath

Day 6: Kedarnath – Guptkashi

Return Trek

Explore Kedarnath in the morning. Return to Guptkashi by trekking to Gaurikund and taking a jeep to Sonprayag, or via helicopter. The driver will transfer you to your hotel. Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

KedarnathGuptkashi

Day 7: Guptkashi to Badrinath

Darshan

After breakfast, drive to Badrinath (200 km, 8-9 hours). Check into your hotel. After a bath in Taptkund, visit the Badrivishal Temple and attend the evening Aarti. Overnight stay in Badrinath.

TaptkundBadrivishal Temple

Day 8: Badrinath – Rudraprayag

Scenic Drive

After breakfast, drive to Rudraprayag via Joshimath (155 km, 6-7 hours). On the way, visit VishuPrayag, Narsingh Temple, KarnPrayag, and NandPrayag. Check in to your hotel in Rudraprayag for an overnight stay.

JoshimathPrayags

Day 9: Rudraprayag to Haridwar

Departure

After breakfast, drive to Haridwar or Dehradun/Rishikesh (165 km, 6-7 hours). You will be dropped off at your hotel, airport, or railway station, concluding the holy yatra.

RudraprayagHaridwar/Rishikesh
