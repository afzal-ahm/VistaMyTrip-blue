🏔️ Overview

A complete Kasol & Tosh experience

A 2-night, 3-day Kasol-Tosh tour package offers a perfect mix of adventure, nature, and relaxation in the serene Parvati Valley of Himachal Pradesh. Kasol is known for its scenic beauty and vibrant Israeli culture, while Tosh is a peaceful village known for its stunning views of snow-capped mountains and waterfalls.

Main Stops

Kasol, Tosh, Chalal Village, and Manikaran.

Main Draw

Scenic views, cafe culture, and nature walks.

Best For

Backpackers, nature lovers, and groups.

Pace

Relaxed and scenic.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation (1-night stay in Kasol + 1-night camp stay in Tosh).
  • 4 Meals (2 breakfast + 2 dinners).
  • Transfer to/from Delhi in AC Vehicle and all sightseeing mentioned.
  • Bonfire, Light music, and Tour curator assistance.

Package Exclusions

  • Any other Food and Beverage charge not included in the package.
  • Any entry tickets to viewpoints or monuments.
  • Costs from natural calamities or forced circumstances.
  • GST (5%) is applicable extra.
🗓️ Suggested Itinerary

Day-wise flow

Day 0: Delhi to Kasol

Overnight Journey

Depart from Delhi and head towards Kasol. Enjoy an overnight journey in a comfortable Tempo Traveller or private car.

DelhiKasol

Day 1: Kasol Arrival & Cafe Hopping

Exploration

Reach Kasol in the morning, check-in, and relax. Explore Kasol's flea market, nearby cafes, and take a short trail to Chalal village. Return for dinner and a bonfire.

KasolChalal Village

Day 2: Kasol to Tosh

Scenic Village

After breakfast, check out and leave for Tosh. Check into your hotel/homestay, explore the local village, and enjoy the mesmerizing views of Parvati Valley. Enjoy a bonfire, dinner, and an overnight stay in Tosh.

Tosh VillageParvati Valley

Day 3: Tosh, Manikaran & Departure

Departure

After breakfast, visit Tosh waterfall and go cafe hopping. Head towards Kasol and visit the holy Manikaran Gurudwara en route. Depart for Delhi in the evening.

Trip Ends

Reach Delhi by morning, concluding your journey with amazing memories.
